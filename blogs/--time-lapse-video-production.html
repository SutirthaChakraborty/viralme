Time Lapse Video Production

Definition

Time lapse video production involves capturing a sequence of frames at set intervals to record changes that take place slowly over time. The resulting video plays back at a faster rate, revealing things that are often too slow to detect with the naked eye.

Applications

  • Nature Observation
  • Construction Progress
  • Event Documentation
  • Artistic Projects

Equipment Needed

To produce stunning time lapse videos, you need:

  • Camera (DSLR or smartphone)
  • Tripod
  • Intervalometer or time-lapse app
  • Video editing software
