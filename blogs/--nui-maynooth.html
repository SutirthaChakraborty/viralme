NUI Maynooth

Welcome to NUI Maynooth, a leading university in Ireland

NUI Maynooth is a constituent university of the National University of Ireland, located in Maynooth, County Kildare, Ireland. The university was founded in 1795 and is known for its strong programs in the arts, humanities, social sciences, and sciences.

The university has a strong research focus and is a member of the Irish Research Council, which provides funding for research projects.

Academic Programs
