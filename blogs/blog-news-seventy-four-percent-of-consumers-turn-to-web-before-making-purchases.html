How 74% of Consumers Research Online Before Buying

In today's digital age, a staggering 74% of consumers turn to the internet to research products and services prior to making a purchase. Understanding this behavior is crucial for businesses aiming to reach their target audience effectively.

Key Statistics:

  • 78% use search engines to research products.
  • 67% check reviews before purchasing.
  • 53% follow brands on social media for recommendations.
