Digital Storytelling

Digital storytelling is the practice of using digital tools and media to tell stories. It combines traditional storytelling techniques with multimedia elements such as images, video, audio, and interactive elements.

By incorporating digital technologies, storytellers can create engaging and immersive experiences for their audiences. Digital storytelling is often used in marketing, education, entertainment, and various other fields to convey information and evoke emotions.

Some common tools and platforms for digital storytelling include social media, websites, blogs, podcasts, and interactive storytelling apps.
