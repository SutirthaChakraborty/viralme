About Ardmore Studios

Located in County Wicklow, Ireland, Ardmore Studios is a hub for film and television production. Established in 1958, it has played a significant role in the production of various acclaimed movies and TV shows.

With state-of-the-art facilities and a picturesque backdrop, Ardmore Studios attracts both local and international filmmakers.

  • Michael Collins
  • Into the West
  • Excalibur
