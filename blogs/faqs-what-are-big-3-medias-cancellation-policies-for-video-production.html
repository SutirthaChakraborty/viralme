What is the cancellation policy for video production?

Big 3 Media's cancellation policy for video production allows for a full refund if the cancellation is made at least 30 days prior to the scheduled production date. For cancellations made within 30 days, a 50% refund will be provided. No refund will be issued for cancellations made within 7 days of the scheduled production date.
