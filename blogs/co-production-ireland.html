Co-Production in Ireland

Co-production is important for enhancing community engagement and collaboration in public services.

Join us in understanding the power of co-production and its impact on social change in Ireland.

What is Co-Production?

Co-production involves actively involving service users and stakeholders in the design and delivery of services.

Benefits of Co-Production

  • Enhanced Service Quality
  • Stronger Community Ties
  • Increased Trust in Public Services

Examples in Ireland

Community projects across Ireland have successfully implemented co-production, leading to sustainable solutions.
