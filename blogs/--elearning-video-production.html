E-Learning Video Production

What is E-Learning Video Production?

E-Learning video production refers to the process of creating video content for educational purposes, such as online courses, tutorials, and training programs.

It involves planning, scripting, filming, editing, and publishing video content to engage learners and facilitate learning.

Interactive E-Learning Video Production

Interactive e-learning video production involves adding interactive elements to video content, such as quizzes, games, and assessments, to enhance learner engagement and interaction.

Interactive e-learning video production can be achieved using various tools and technologies, such as:

  • Authoring tools like Articulate Storyline, Adobe Captivate, and Lectora Inspire
  • Video editing software like Adobe Premiere Pro, Final Cut Pro, and DaVinci Resolve
  • Interactive video platforms like Wibbitz, GoAnimate (now Vyond), and Raw Shorts
