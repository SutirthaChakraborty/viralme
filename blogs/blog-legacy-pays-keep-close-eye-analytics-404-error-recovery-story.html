Blog Legacy: Keep a Close Eye on Analytics

Understanding 404 Errors

A 404 error indicates that a webpage could not be found. This can negatively impact user experience and SEO.

Tip: Use analytics tools to monitor 404 errors and address them promptly to enhance site performance.

Recovery Story

Our blog faced multiple 404 errors that affected our visibility. By implementing a robust strategy, we analyzed traffic patterns and redirected broken links, leading to a significant recovery in user visits.
