Case Study: Brafton Helps Concentric Turn Random Acts of Marketing into a Scalable Content Strategy

Concentric Turn

Concentric Turn is a leading B2B marketing agency that helps companies grow their business through data-driven marketing strategies. They were looking to transform their random acts of marketing approach into a scalable content strategy that would help them engage with their target audience and drive more leads.

Brafton was brought in to help with the project, and together they developed a comprehensive content strategy that included blog posts, social media content, email campaigns, and more. The new strategy helped Concentric Turn establish themselves as thought leaders in their industry and attract more high-quality leads.
