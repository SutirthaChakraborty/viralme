**HTML and CSS** are essential for creating a search engine friendly website. They allow you to structure and style your content effectively, which is crucial for SEO. Using proper HTML tags and CSS techniques can significantly impact your website's visibility on search engines.

Importance of Meta Tags for SEO

Meta tags play a vital role in SEO. They provide information about the webpage to search engines and social platforms. Utilize meta descriptions and titles effectively to improve your website's visibility and user engagement.

Utilizing Header Tags for SEO

Header tags not only organize your content for readers but also contribute to SEO. They help search engines understand the structure and relevance of your content. Properly utilizing header tags can enhance the visibility of your website in search results.
