What is Above the Fold?

The term "above the fold" refers to the portion of a webpage that is visible without scrolling. This area is crucial for capturing user attention and improving engagement.

Click here to learn more!

Key elements to include above the fold are:

  • Compelling headlines
  • Engaging visuals
  • Clear calls to action
  • Essential information
