August's All-Time High in American Web Video Viewership

September 1, 2024
Breaking News: Video Streaming Giants
In a surprising turn of events, American web video viewership reached an all-time high in August, surpassing previous records set in 2021. This surge was attributed to the release of several blockbuster web series and movies, leading to a 20% increase in viewership compared to the same period last year.
