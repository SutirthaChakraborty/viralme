Turnbox Information

\n

Sunnyvale turnboxes are used to control the flow of traffic in and out of a neighborhood or city. They help prevent accidents by directing cars to the correct lanes and reducing congestion on busy roads.

Traffic Lights

\n

Traffic lights are another important component of road safety and traffic management. They help regulate the flow of vehicles and pedestrians, ensuring that everyone can move safely and efficiently through an area.

Road Markings

\n

Road markings are used to indicate the direction and speed limits of a road, as well as other important information such as stop signs and crosswalks. They help drivers stay safe and informed while driving, reducing the risk of accidents and injuries.
