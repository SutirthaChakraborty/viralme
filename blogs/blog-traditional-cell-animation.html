Traditional Cell Animation

The timeless art of frame-by-frame animation

What is Traditional Cell Animation?

Traditional cell animation, also known as hand-drawn animation, is a technique where each frame is drawn by hand on transparent celluloid sheets. This method has historical significance in the animation industry, paving the way for today's digital techniques.

Why is Traditional Animation Important?

Despite the rise of digital animation, traditional cell animation holds an important place in storytelling through art. It offers a unique charm and artistic quality that resonates with audiences around the world.
