What is the Best CMS?

With so many Content Management Systems (CMS) out there, it can be overwhelming to choose the right one for your website. Here's a rundown of the top contenders:

  • WordPress: The most popular CMS, with over 60 million websites using it. It's free, open-source, and highly customizable.
  • Joomla: A powerful and flexible CMS, known for its ease of use and scalability. It's also free and open-source.
  • Drupal: A highly secure and scalable CMS, popular among enterprise-level businesses. It's also free and open-source.
  • Shopify: A popular CMS for e-commerce websites, known for its ease of use and scalability. It's not free, but offers a 14-day trial.

So, which CMS is best for you? It ultimately depends on your needs and preferences. But with this infographic, you'll have a better idea of what each CMS has to offer.
