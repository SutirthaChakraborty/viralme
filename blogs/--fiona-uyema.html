Recipe: Asian-Inspired Quinoa Salad

  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, bell peppers, peas)
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • Chopped green onions for garnish

Instructions: Cook quinoa in water until fluffy. Stir in vegetables, soy sauce, and sesame oil. Serve warm or cold, garnished with green onions.
