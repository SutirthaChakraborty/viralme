Understanding Blog Analytics with GA4

Google Analytics 4 (GA4) is essential for tracking user interactions on your blog. It provides insights into page views, user engagement, and traffic sources.

GA4 Views Explained

In GA4, views are now referred to as "pageviews." This metric helps you understand how many times your blog pages are viewed, allowing you to optimize content based on user interest.

To set up GA4, you need to implement the global site tag (gtag.js) in your HTML. This enables tracking across your website and provides valuable data for improving your content strategy.
