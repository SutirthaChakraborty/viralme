Best Interactive Marketing Examples of 2018

Interactive Blog Content Marketing

In 2018, brands utilized interactive content to engage audiences like never before. Here are some standout examples:

1. Quiz Campaigns

Quizzes that provide personalized results significantly boost engagement and shareability.

2. Interactive Infographics

Infographics that users can click through allow for a deeper understanding of complex data.

3. Polls and Surveys

Creating polls for audience feedback fosters a sense of community and involvement.

Utilizing these strategies can take your content marketing to new heights!
