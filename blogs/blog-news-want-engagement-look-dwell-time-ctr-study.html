Understanding Dwell Time and CTR

Dwell time is the time a visitor spends on your blog before returning to search results. Higher dwell time indicates quality content. Improving click-through rates (CTR) is crucial for attracting visitors.

Read more about Dwell Time

To increase dwell time, create engaging headlines, use images, and end posts with questions to spark conversation.

Optimizing your meta descriptions, crafting compelling CTAs, and testing different headlines can significantly improve your CTR.
