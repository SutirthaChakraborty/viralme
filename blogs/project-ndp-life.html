NDP Life

NDP stands for New Democratic Party, which is a political party in Canada. It was founded in 1988 and has been the official opposition party since then. The party's goal is to promote social justice, equality, and democracy in Canada.

The NDP has had many prominent members over the years, including former Prime Minister Justin Trudeau and current leader Elizabeth May. The party has also played a significant role in shaping Canadian policy on issues such as healthcare, climate change, and immigration.
