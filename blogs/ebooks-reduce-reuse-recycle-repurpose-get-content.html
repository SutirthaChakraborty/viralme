eBooks and Sustainability

eBooks significantly reduce paper waste, contribute to recycling efforts, and allow content to be reused and repurposed globally.

  • Reduce: Less paper means fewer trees cut down.
  • Reuse: Easily shareable and transferable across devices.
  • Recycle: Digital content does not contribute to landfill waste.
  • Repurpose: Adapt and modify content for various audiences and platforms.
