What is Time Lapse?

\n

Time lapse is a technique used in photography and videography where the same scene is captured at different intervals and then played back in rapid succession to create the illusion of time passing quickly. This can be done manually or using specialized equipment. Time lapse videos are often used to showcase the beauty of nature, cityscapes, and other landscapes over time.
