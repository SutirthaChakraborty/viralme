Documentary Production

What is Documentary Production?

Documentary production encompasses the entire process of creating a documentary film, including

  • Research
  • Pre-production
  • Filming
  • Post-production

Key Stages:

Understanding the key stages in documentary filmmaking is crucial for successful production.

  1. Research: Thorough investigation to gather information and develop a strong narrative.
  2. Pre-production: Planning, scripting, and organizing crews and resources.
  3. Filming: Capturing footage, interviews, and B-roll as per the storyboard.
  4. Post-production: Editing, sound design, and finalizing the documentary.
