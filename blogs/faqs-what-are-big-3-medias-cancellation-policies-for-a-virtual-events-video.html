FAQs on Big 3 Media Cancellation Policies for Virtual Events

What is Big 3 Media's cancellation policy for virtual events?
Big 3 Media offers a full refund if cancellation occurs 30 days before the event. For cancellations made between 15 to 29 days, a 50% refund is provided. No refunds are available for cancellations made less than 14 days before the event.
Are there exceptions to the cancellation policy?
Yes, exceptions may apply for extreme circumstances such as illness or natural disasters. Documentation may be required for consideration.
Can I transfer my virtual event registration to another person?
Yes, Big 3 Media allows transfers if notified at least 7 days before the event. The new participant must meet all registration requirements.
What happens if Big 3 Media cancels the event?
If Big 3 Media cancels the event, attendees will receive a full refund or the option to transfer to a future event.
