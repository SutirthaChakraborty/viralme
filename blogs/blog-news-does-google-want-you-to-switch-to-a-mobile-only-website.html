Should You Switch to a Mobile-Only Website?

Understanding Google's Mobile-First Indexing

Google has shifted towards a mobile-first indexing, meaning it predominantly uses the mobile version of the content for indexing and ranking. This change emphasizes the importance of having a mobile-friendly website.

Switching to a mobile-only website can enhance user experience and improve your SEO rankings. Google prioritizes sites that are optimized for mobile devices, which can lead to higher visibility in search results.
