FAQs on Big 3 Media Cancellation Policies

What is Big 3 Media's general cancellation policy?
Big 3 Media typically allows cancellations within 24 hours of booking for a full refund. After that, a fee may apply based on the timing of the cancellation.
Are there any fees for late cancellations?
Yes, cancellations made less than 24 hours before the scheduled video walkthrough may incur a cancellation fee, which varies by service type.
How can I initiate a cancellation?
You can cancel your booking by contacting customer service through email or the official website's chat function.
Will I receive a confirmation of my cancellation?
Yes, you will receive an email confirmation of your cancellation once it has been processed.
