FAQs: Big 3 Media Cancellation Policies

What is Big Media's cancellation policy?
Most Big 3 Media platforms have a 30-day cancellation policy that allows users to cancel their subscription at any time within the billing cycle.
Is there a fee for canceling my subscription?
Typically, no cancellation fees are applied, but make sure to check specific terms on your chosen platform.
How do I cancel my subscription?
You can cancel your subscription through your account settings on the respective Big 3 Media platform.
Will I lose access immediately upon cancellation?
Access usually continues until the end of the billing cycle, after which your subscription will be inactive.
