**Social Housing**

Social housing refers to affordable housing provided by the government or non-profit organizations to individuals and families with low incomes or special needs. It aims to ensure that everyone has access to safe and secure housing, regardless of their financial situation.

**Key Features of Social Housing:**

  • **Affordability:** Rents are typically set at a percentage of the tenant's income.
  • **Security of Tenure:** Tenants have legal protection against eviction without proper cause.
  • **Community Integration:** Social housing developments often include communal spaces and facilities to promote a sense of community.
