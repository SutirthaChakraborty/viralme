Wooshii Signs Up to AdGreen's Carbon Calculator

Wooshii, a leading video production platform, has announced that they are now using AdGreen's carbon calculator to measure and reduce their carbon footprint. This move aligns with their commitment to sustainability and environmental responsibility.

The AdGreen carbon calculator helps companies in the advertising industry estimate the carbon emissions from their production activities. By tracking and analyzing their carbon footprint, businesses can make informed decisions to decrease their impact on the environment.

Wooshii's decision to sign up to the AdGreen carbon calculator showcases their dedication to promoting eco-friendly practices in the video production sector. This initiative will not only benefit the environment but also set a positive example for other companies in the industry.
