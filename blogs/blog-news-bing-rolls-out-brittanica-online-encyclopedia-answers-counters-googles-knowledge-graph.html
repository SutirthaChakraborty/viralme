Bing's Britannica Online Encyclopedia Answers

Overview

Bing has recently rolled out its Britannica Online Encyclopedia Answers, aiming to counter Google's Knowledge Graph. This new feature provides users with direct answers from Britannica's extensive database, enhancing the search experience.

Key Differences

Unlike Google's Knowledge Graph, which displays information in a sidebar with links to related searches, Bing's feature integrates answers directly into the search results. This means users can access encyclopedia entries without navigating away from the main search page.
