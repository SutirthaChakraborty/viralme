Bridge Interpreting in Sign Language

Understanding Bridge Interpreting

Bridge interpreting connects deaf and hearing individuals through skilled interpreters using sign language. It enhances communication in various settings.

What is Bridge Interpreting?

Bridge interpreting is a specialized service that enables effective communication between deaf and hearing individuals by facilitating understanding and interaction through sign language interpretation.

Benefits of Sign Language Interpreting

  • Enhances Accessibility
  • Promotes Inclusivity
  • Facilitates Clear Communication
  • Supports Education and Employment Opportunities
