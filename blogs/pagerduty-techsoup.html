Understanding PagerDuty and TechSoup

What is PagerDuty?

PagerDuty is an incident management platform that helps organizations manage IT operations and response. It's essential for maintaining uptime and ensuring customer satisfaction through effective incident resolution.

It integrates with various tools to send alerts, manage on-call schedules, and automate incident resolution to improve operational efficiency.

What is TechSoup?

TechSoup provides technology resources and tools to non-profits. It connects organizations with software, hardware, and knowledge to maximize their impact.

Through partnerships with tech companies, TechSoup offers discounted or donated products that can help non-profits leverage technology for their missions.

Benefits of Integrating PagerDuty and TechSoup

Integrating PagerDuty with TechSoup provides non-profits with tools to ensure operational resilience while maximizing their resources.
