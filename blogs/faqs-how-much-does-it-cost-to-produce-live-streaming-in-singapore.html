FAQs: How Much Does It Cost to Produce Live Streaming in Singapore?

What factors influence the cost of live streaming?
The cost of live streaming in Singapore is influenced by several factors, including the type of event, required equipment, duration, and level of production required. Additional costs may arise from hiring professionals such as cameramen and sound engineers.
What is the typical price range for live streaming services?
Typically, live streaming services in Singapore can range from SGD 500 to SGD 5,000 or more, depending on the complexity and features needed, such as multiple camera angles, live editing, and platform integration.
Are there ongoing costs associated with live streaming?
Yes, ongoing costs may include platform subscription fees, bandwidth charges, and maintenance of equipment. It's essential to budget for these when planning your live stream.
