2012 Stadium Animation

In 2012, Matcha Design Partners joined forces with MidFirst Backyard Bowl to bring an innovative stadium animation to life. This partnership was a groundbreaking event in the world of digital design and animation, showcasing the future of interactive entertainment.

The animation was designed to engage fans of all ages, providing an immersive experience that went beyond traditional sports broadcasting. The stadium was transformed into a vibrant, interactive playground, featuring dynamic visuals and engaging content that captured the imagination of audiences worldwide.

This collaboration was a testament to the creativity and innovation of both Matcha Design Partners and MidFirst Backyard Bowl, as they pushed the boundaries of what was possible in digital design. The project not only showcased their technical expertise but also their ability to create memorable experiences that resonated with the public.
