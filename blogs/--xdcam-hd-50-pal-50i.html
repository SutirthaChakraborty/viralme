XDCAM HD 50 PAL 50i

XDCAM HD 50 is a professional video format used primarily in broadcast and production. It offers high-quality HD video with a frame rate of 50i (interlaced).

Key Features

  • Resolution: 1920 x 1080
  • Bit Rate: 50 Mbps
  • Color Space: 4:2:2
  • Interlaced Format: Yes (50i)
  • Recording Medium: Optical Disc or SxS Memory Card

Applications

XDCAM HD 50 is widely used in:

  • Television Broadcasting
  • Documentary Filmmaking
  • Corporate Video Production
