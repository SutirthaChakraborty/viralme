Targeted Advertising: Ethics & Impact

Understanding Targeted Advertising

Targeted advertising leverages data analytics to optimize ad relevance, improving user engagement. However, it raises ethical questions about privacy and data security.

Ethical Considerations

Marketers must balance personalization with privacy. Ethical frameworks should guide data usage to protect consumer rights.

Impact on Consumer Behavior

Effective targeting can enhance conversion rates, but excessive targeting may lead to ad fatigue or resentment.
