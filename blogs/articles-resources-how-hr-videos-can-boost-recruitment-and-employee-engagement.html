Boost Recruitment and Employee Engagement with HR Videos

HR videos can be a powerful tool for recruitment and employee engagement. They provide an opportunity to showcase your company culture, values, and mission, and can help to attract top talent and improve employee retention.

75% of job seekers prefer to watch a video to learn more about a company than reading text. 64% of employees say that watching a video about a company would make them more likely to apply for a job. 50% of employees say that watching a video about a company would make them more likely to stay with a company.
