2016 Content Trends: Build Trust & Make More Sales

Longform Content

In 2016, **longform content** emerged as a powerful tool for engaging readers and driving conversions. Articles exceeding 1000 words not only provide depth but also keep readers on the page longer, enhancing trust and authority.

Building Trust

Establishing **trust** with your audience is crucial. By addressing common pain points and providing valuable insights, you can foster a loyal readership that is more likely to convert into customers.

Sales through Content

Effective content marketing strategies can significantly boost your **sales**. By utilizing **keywords** strategically and creating engaging content, you can attract more visitors and convert them into customers.
