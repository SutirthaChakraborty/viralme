Blog News: Why Marketers will like Facebook's New Timeline for Targeting

With the new timeline feature on Facebook, marketers will have better control and insights into their targeting strategies. The ability to track user interactions over time can help in creating more personalized and effective advertising campaigns. By utilizing this feature, marketers can improve their ROI and reach their target audience more efficiently.
