Enhancing Tech Skills for Senior Level Marketers

A Study on 39 Senior Level Marketers

As digital marketing evolves, senior marketers must adapt by improving their tech skills. This study highlights the challenges faced by 39 marketing professionals and offers insightful strategies for upskilling in technology. Key areas of focus include:

  • Data Analytics and Interpretation
  • SEO and Content Marketing
  • Email Automation Tools
  • Social Media Strategies
  • Customer Relationship Management (CRM) Systems

To stay ahead, it's crucial to invest in online courses and workshops that specialize in digital marketing technology. Building a personal learning network can also enhance your growth.
