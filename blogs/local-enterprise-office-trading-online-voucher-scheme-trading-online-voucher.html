Trading Online Voucher Scheme

What is the Trading Online Voucher Scheme?

The Trading Online Voucher Scheme is designed to assist small businesses with up to 10 employees to trade more online, boost sales, and reach new markets. It provides financial support to small businesses, offering grants of up to 2,500 to help them get their business online.

How to Apply for the Voucher

To apply for the voucher, you must attend a Trading Online Voucher seminar organized by one of the Local Enterprise Offices around the country. After successfully applying, an authorization letter will be sent to you, and you can then proceed with the application process.
