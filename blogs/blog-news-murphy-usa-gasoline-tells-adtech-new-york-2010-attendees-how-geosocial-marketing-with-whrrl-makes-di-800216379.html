Murphy USA Gasoline Tells AdTech New York 2010

Attendees Explain

Attendees at the AdTech conference in New York City in 2010 discussed how geosocial marketing with Whrrl makes it possible to connect with customers in a highly targeted and interactive way. Whrrl, a location-based social networking platform, allows businesses to engage with their customers in real-time, based on their physical location. This approach is particularly effective for services such as Murphy USA, a chain of gas stations and convenience stores, which can use Whrrl to offer promotions and updates to customers in their vicinity.
