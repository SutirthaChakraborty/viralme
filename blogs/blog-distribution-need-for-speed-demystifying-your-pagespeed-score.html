Demystifying Your PageSpeed Score

Understanding PageSpeed

Your PageSpeed score is crucial for blog distribution and SEO. It affects your site's search engine ranking and user experience. Key metrics include First Contentful Paint (FCP) and Time to Interactive (TTI).

Key Metrics Explained:

  • First Contentful Paint (FCP): Measures the time it takes for the first visible content to appear.
  • Time to Interactive (TTI): The time it takes for your page to become fully interactive.
  • Speed Index: Shows how quickly the contents of a page are visibly populated.
  • Largest Contentful Paint (LCP): Measures loading performance and when the largest content element is visible.
