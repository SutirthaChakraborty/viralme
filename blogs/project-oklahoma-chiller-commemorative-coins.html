Project Oklahoma Chiller Commemorative Coins

The Project Oklahoma Chiller Commemorative Coins are a unique tribute to the resilience and spirit of the people of Oklahoma, particularly those affected by the devastating Chiller Fault. These coins are more than just a piece of metal; they are a symbol of hope, renewal, and the unbreakable bond that holds communities together in times of crisis.

Each coin is meticulously crafted to honor the historical significance of the Chiller Fault and the ongoing efforts to rebuild and revitalize the affected areas. The obverse of the coin features an intricate design that captures the essence of the Chiller Fault, while the reverse showcases the iconic symbols of Oklahoma, including the state flag and the Sooner Tree.

These commemorative coins are an excellent addition to any collection and a meaningful gift for anyone who has been touched by the Chiller Fault or is simply inspired by the story of recovery and resilience in Oklahoma.
