Google Enhances Webmaster Tools with Indexing Data

Google has recently introduced advanced indexing data features within Google Webmaster Tools. This new addition allows webmasters to gain insights into how their websites are indexed by Google.

Understanding indexing is crucial for improving SEO performance and ensuring that your content reaches your audience effectively.

Learn More

With these enhancements, users can now see detailed reports on their site's indexing status, including:

  • Index Coverage: Insights on indexed and excluded pages.
  • Mobile Usability: Data on how mobile-friendly your site is.
  • Enhancements: Information on AMP and other page experiences.

This valuable data empowers webmasters to make informed decisions and optimize their site for better performance on search engines.
