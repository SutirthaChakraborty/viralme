Wreath-Laying by the Prince of Wales & Duchess of Cornwall

On their visit to Glasnevin Cemetery, the Prince of Wales and Duchess of Cornwall paid tribute by laying ceremonial wreaths at the final resting place of notable Irish figures.

Significance of Wreath-Laying

Laying wreaths is a poignant tradition that honors deceased individuals, reflecting respect and remembrance.

Click here to learn more about the significance of Glasnevin Cemetery

Glasnevin Cemetery is one of the most historically significant burial grounds in Ireland, holding the remains of prominent national figures and reflecting the country's rich heritage.
