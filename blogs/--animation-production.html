Animation Production

What is Animation?

Animation is the process of creating motion and shape change by rapidly displaying a sequence of static images that minimally differ from each other.

Animation can be produced through various techniques, including traditional hand-drawn animation, stop-motion, and computer-generated imagery (CGI).

Steps in Animation Production

  • Concept Development
  • Scriptwriting
  • Storyboarding
  • Designing Characters
  • Voice Recording
  • Animation
  • Post-Production

Each step involves specific techniques and tools that contribute to the final output of the animation.

Types of Animation

Common types of animation include:

  1. 2D Animation
  2. 3D Animation
  3. Stop Motion
  4. Motion Graphics

Each type has its unique styles and software requirements.
