Latest News

Google is testing expansions to its Knowledge Graph, aiming to showcase more query-relevant content. This update could significantly enhance search results, providing users with more comprehensive and contextual information.

What is Knowledge Graph?

The Knowledge Graph is a search technology developed by Google to provide more relevant and comprehensive results to users' queries. It draws from a vast database of information about entities such as people, places, and things.
