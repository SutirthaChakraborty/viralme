Ebooks: The Guide to Drip Email Marketing

What is Drip Email Marketing?

Drip email marketing is a strategy that involves sending a series of automated emails to nurture leads and engage customers over time. This method helps in building relationships and guiding potential customers through the sales funnel.

4 Examples You Can Steal

1. Welcome Series

Send a series of welcome emails to new subscribers, introducing them to your brand and offering a special discount.

2. Abandoned Cart Reminders

Remind customers of items left in their cart with a friendly email, possibly including a discount to encourage completion of the purchase.

3. Re-engagement Campaigns

Target inactive subscribers with a re-engagement email, offering them an incentive to return.

4. Post-Purchase Follow-Up

Send a thank you email after a purchase, along with recommendations for related products.
