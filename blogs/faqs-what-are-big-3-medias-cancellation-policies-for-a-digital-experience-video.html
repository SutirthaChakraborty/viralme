FAQs: Big 3 Media Cancellation Policies for Digital Experience Videos

What is Big 3 Media's cancellation policy?
Big 3 Media allows cancellations up to 24 hours before the scheduled digital experience video for a full refund. Any cancellations made after this period may incur a fee.
Can I get a refund if I cancel?
Yes, you can receive a full refund if you cancel at least 24 hours prior to your scheduled video. Late cancellations may result in a partial refund.
How can I cancel my digital experience video?
Cancellations can be made via our website or customer service. Please provide your booking reference for faster processing.
Is there a rescheduling option?
Yes, you can reschedule your digital experience video without any penalty if done at least 24 hours in advance.
