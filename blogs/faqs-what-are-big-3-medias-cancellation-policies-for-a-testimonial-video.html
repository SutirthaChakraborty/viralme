FAQs on Big 3 Media Cancellation Policies

What are the Big 3 Media's cancellation policies for a testimonial video?

General Policies

If you need to cancel your testimonial video project with Big 3 Media, you must notify us at least 7 days prior to the scheduled shoot date. This gives us enough time to reschedule the shoot with another client, if possible. If you cancel within 7 days of the scheduled shoot date, you will be charged a 50% cancellation fee. If you do not show up for the shoot without prior notice, you will be charged the full cost of the project.

Special Considerations

For special projects or events, Big 3 Media may have additional cancellation policies. These policies are determined on a case-by-case basis and will be discussed and agreed upon at the time of booking.
