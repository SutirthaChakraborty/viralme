Twitter & Bing Extend Their Partnership

Latest Announcement

Published on: October 20, 2023

Twitter and Bing have officially announced an extension of their existing partnership, focusing on enhanced news delivery and improved social media integration into search results.

This extended relationship aims to provide users with real-time updates on trending topics, blending Twitter's dynamic social feed with Bing's powerful search algorithms. Expect improved visibility of tweets in search results, allowing for a more integrated experience.

The collaboration is expected to deliver a significant boost to news distribution, enabling businesses and influencers to connect with larger audiences efficiently.
