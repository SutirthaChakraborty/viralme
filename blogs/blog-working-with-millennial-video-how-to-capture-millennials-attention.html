How to Capture Millennials' Attention with Video

Engaging Tips

  • Use Authentic Storytelling
    Share relatable experiences or hardships to evoke emotions.
  • Utilize Short Form Content
    Keep videos under 60 seconds for quick consumption.
  • Incorporate Humor
    Funny content is more likely to be shared and remembered.
  • Engage with Interactive Elements
    Pose questions or include polls to create dialog.
  • Focus on Visuals
    Use bold colors and striking graphics to catch the eye.
