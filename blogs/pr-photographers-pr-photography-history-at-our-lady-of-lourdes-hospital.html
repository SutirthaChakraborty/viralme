Our Lady of Lourdes Hospital is proud to have a team of professional photographers who capture the beauty and spirit of our patients and staff. From portraits to event coverage, our photographers provide high-quality images that help us tell our story.

To learn more about our photography services, please visit our website or contact us directly.

Pr Photography History

Photography has played an important role in the history of Our Lady of Lourdes Hospital since its founding in the early 1900s. Over the years, our hospital has used photography to document its many achievements, including the groundbreaking surgeries and treatments performed by its skilled physicians and nurses.

Today, our hospital continues to rely on photography to capture the lives and stories of our patients, staff, and volunteers. From patient testimonials to fundraising events, our photographers help us share the love and compassion that defines Our Lady of Lourdes Hospital.
