European Union Lecture

Welcome to the European Union Lecture

This lecture provides an overview of the European Union, its institutions, policies, and impact on the global stage.

Institutions

The European Union is governed by a complex structure of institutions, including the European Commission, the European Parliament, the Council of the European Union, and the Court of Justice of the European Union.

Policies

The EU has a wide range of policies covering areas such as economy, environment, education, and justice.

Global Impact

The European Union plays a significant role in international relations, contributing to global governance and economic stability.
