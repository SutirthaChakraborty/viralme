Rugby

Rugby is a team sport played with a ball and two teams of eleven players each. The objective of the game is to score points by carrying or kicking the ball over the opponent's goal line.

The game is played on a rectangular field with a goal at each end. Players can pass, catch, tackle, and kick the ball. Rugby is known for its physicality and aggressive play style.

Some popular rugby unions include England, New Zealand, Australia, South Africa, France, and Japan.
