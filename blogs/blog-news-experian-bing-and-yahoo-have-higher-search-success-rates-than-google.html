Search Success Rates

Experian, Bing, and Yahoo Outperform Google

Recent studies indicate that **Experian**, **Bing**, and **Yahoo** have higher search success rates than **Google**. This trend highlights the importance of exploring multiple search engines for better results.

In a recent report, Bing showed a success rate of 75%, Yahoo at 70%, and Experian at 68%, compared to Google's 65%.
