Irish Beauty Blog Awards

Celebrating Excellence in Beauty Blogging

About the Awards

The Irish Beauty Blog Awards recognize and celebrate the outstanding contributions of beauty bloggers in Ireland. These awards showcase talent, creativity, and passion within the beauty community.

The awards cover various categories including Best Beauty Blog, Best Makeup Artist, Best Skincare Blog, and more. Winners are selected based on public and industry votes, promoting engagement across social media.

Categories

  • Best Beauty Blog
  • Best Makeup Artist
  • Best Skincare Blog
  • Best Haircare Blog

Each category highlights unique talents and exceptional contributions, encouraging bloggers to showcase their work and engage with their audience.

Nomination Process

Nominations for the awards open annually, inviting bloggers and their followers to submit entries for their favorite beauty influencers.

Nominees are then shortlisted based on public votes, leading to a final round of judging by industry experts, ensuring a fair and transparent process.
