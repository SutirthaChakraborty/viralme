Breaking Red Lights: A Dangerous Mistake

Consequences of Running Red Lights

Breaking a red light can lead to severe accidents, injuring drivers, passengers, and pedestrians. The legal penalties include hefty fines and increased insurance premiums.

In fact, nearly 50% of traffic accidents involve red-light running incidents. Always stop at red lights to ensure your safety and the safety of others.

In many jurisdictions, running a red light is classified as a serious traffic violation. Convictions can lead to points on your driver's record, affecting your driving privileges.
