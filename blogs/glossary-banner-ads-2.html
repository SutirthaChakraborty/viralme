Glossary of Banner Ads

Banner Ad
A graphic display ad on a web page that links to the advertiser's site.
Click-Through Rate (CTR)
The ratio of users who click on a specific link to the number of total users who view the page.
Impression
A metric that indicates how many times an ad is fetched and displayed to viewers.
Cost Per Click (CPC)
An online advertising model where advertisers pay each time a user clicks on their ad.
