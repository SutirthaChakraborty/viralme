Email Marketing Campaigns

Types of Email Marketing Campaigns

  • Newsletter Campaigns
    This type focuses on regular updates, tips, and news to engage subscribers.
  • Promotional Campaigns
    These campaigns aim to drive sales by promoting specific products or services.
  • Transactional Emails
    Automatically generated emails sent after a user's action, such as purchase confirmations.
  • Re-engagement Campaigns
    Designed to win back customers who have not interacted with your brand for a while.
