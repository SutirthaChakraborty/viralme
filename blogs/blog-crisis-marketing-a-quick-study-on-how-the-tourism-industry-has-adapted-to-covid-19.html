Crisis Marketing in Tourism: Adapting to COVID-19

Understanding the Crisis

The tourism industry faced unprecedented challenges due to the COVID-19 pandemic. Travel bans and health concerns significantly impacted global travel behavior.

Adaptation Strategies

Tourism businesses implemented effective crisis marketing strategies:

  • Enhanced sanitation protocols
  • Flexible booking options
  • Health and safety marketing campaigns
  • Virtual experiences to engage customers

Many companies adopted digital marketing strategies, focusing on social media engagement and online promotions to maintain customer interest.
