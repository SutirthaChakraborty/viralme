What is Content Marketing?

Content marketing involves the creation and sharing of valuable content to attract and engage a target audience with the goal of driving profitable customer action.

Key Strategies in 2012

Strategies included focusing on SEO, utilizing social media platforms for distribution, and engaging in storytelling to connect with the audience.

Challenges Faced

Marketers faced challenges such as content saturation, evolving algorithms, and the need for measurable results.
