Understanding Social Media's Impact on Different Age Groups

Social media plays a crucial role in how older and younger customers receive news. Recent studies indicate that platforms like Facebook and Instagram are utilized differently by various age demographics.

Older individuals tend to prefer Facebook for news, valuing the connection to family and long-time friends, while younger customers are drawn to platforms like TikTok and Twitter for quick, engaging updates.
