Celebrating Irish Olympians

Notable Irish Olympians

Katie Taylor

Sport: Boxing

Katie Taylor is a two-time Olympic gold medalist in lightweight boxing, winning her medals in 2012 and 2016.

Paul O'Donovan

Sport: Rowing

Paul O'Donovan won gold in the Men's Lightweight Double Sculls at the 2016 Olympics and is a world champion.

John Treacy

Sport: Athletics

John Treacy won a silver medal in the marathon at the 1984 Olympics and is a prominent figure in Irish athletics.
