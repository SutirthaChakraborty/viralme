Blog News: Google Voice Search and Image Search

\n

\n Did you know that Google has been improving its voice search capabilities? With the latest update, users can now search for information by speaking their queries aloud. This is a great feature for those who are on the go or have difficulty typing.\n

\n

\n In addition to voice search, Google also offers image search. This allows users to search for images based on their visual content. For example, if you see an image of a cat, you can use Google's image search to find more pictures of cats online.\n
