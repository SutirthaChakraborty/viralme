Is Facebook Still Relevant for Marketing?

With the evolution of digital marketing, social media platforms like Facebook continue to be crucial for B2B and B2C marketing strategies.

A recent study highlights that brands leveraging Facebook for social marketing experience higher engagement rates.

Studies indicate that nearly 70% of marketers find success using Facebook. It facilitates targeted advertising, customer engagement, and aids in building brand loyalty.
