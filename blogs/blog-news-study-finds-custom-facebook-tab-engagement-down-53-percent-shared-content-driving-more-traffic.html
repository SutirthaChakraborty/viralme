Study Finds Custom Facebook Tab Engagement Down 53%

Recent research indicates a significant 53% decline in user engagement with custom Facebook tabs. This shift necessitates a reevaluation of social media strategies for brands and content creators.

Shared Content Driving More Traffic

Contrary to engagement with custom tabs, shared content has emerged as a key driver of traffic to blogs and websites. Understanding this trend can enhance marketing efforts and content strategy.

To maximize reach, focus on creating shareable content that resonates with your audience. Engage your followers through interactive posts and encourage sharing across multiple platforms.
