Social Media Posting During COVID-19

Optimal Timings for Engagement

During the pandemic, social media usage surged. Here are the best times:

  • Facebook: Weekdays between 1 PM - 3 PM
  • Instagram: Mondays and Thursdays at 11 AM
  • Twitter: Weekdays at noon and 5-6 PM
  • LinkedIn: Tuesdays and Wednesdays at 9 AM

These timings help maximize engagement by reaching users during peak activity hours.
