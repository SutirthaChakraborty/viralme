Understanding Time Lapse and Hyperlapse

Time Lapse: A photography technique that captures a sequence of images at set intervals to record changes that take place slowly over time.

Hyperlapse: A more advanced form of time lapse that involves camera movement over considerable distances, creating a dynamic visual effect.
