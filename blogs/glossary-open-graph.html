Open Graph Glossary

Open Graph Protocol
A protocol used to integrate web pages into the social graph, allowing for rich previews on social media platforms.
og:title
The title of your web page as it should appear in the social media post.
og:description
A brief summary or description of the content of your web page for social sharing.
og:image
The image that represents your content and appears in your social media post.
og:url
The canonical URL of your web page that you want to share on social media.
