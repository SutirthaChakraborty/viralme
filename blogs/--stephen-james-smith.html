Stephen James Smith

Explore the Life and Work of the Esteemed Poet

Biography

Stephen James Smith is an acclaimed Irish poet known for his powerful words and exploring themes of identity, love, and social issues. His work resonates with audiences both in Ireland and abroad.

Major Works

Some of his notable collections include "The Last of the Survivors" and "A Narrative of Reckoning," which showcase his lyrical prowess and poignant reflections on modern life.

Influences

Stephen draws inspiration from both contemporary and classical poets, incorporating elements of Irish folklore and personal experience into his writing.

Public Speaking & Readings

He is a sought-after speaker, often participating in literary festivals and events where he shares his insights on poetry and creativity.
