Pew Research: 6 Types of Twitter Topic Networks

1. News Junkies

Individuals who are passionate about current events and breaking news.

This network prioritizes real-time updates and engages actively with media personalities.

2. Political Junkies

People deeply involved in political discourse, advocating for various causes.

This group often shares and debates political opinions, news, and events.

3. Influencers

Key individuals who shape opinions through their social reach and content.

They often leverage their platform for brand partnerships and audience engagement.

4. Conversationalists

Users who thrive on interaction and discussions about various topics.

They encourage dialogue and engage in multiple Twitter threads.

5. Social Butterflies

Individuals focused on social experiences and trends.

This audience is integral to shaping online communities and social initiatives.

6. Entertainment Enthusiasts

People who follow pop culture, movies, and music trends.

They drive engagement through memes, celebrity news, and trending topics.
