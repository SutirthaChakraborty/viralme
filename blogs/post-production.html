Post Production Essentials

What is Post Production?

Post production is the phase in film and video production that occurs after the shooting is completed. It involves editing, sound design, color correction, and visual effects.

Editing Techniques

Editing is crucial for storytelling. Techniques include cutting, transitions, and pacing to enhance narrative flow.

Color Grading

Color grading adjusts the color and tone of the footage to create mood and consistency across scenes.

Sound Design

Sound design involves creating the audio landscape of a film, including dialogue, sound effects, and music.

Visual Effects (VFX)

VFX enhances or creates imagery that cannot be captured during live-action shooting, adding depth and excitement to the film.
