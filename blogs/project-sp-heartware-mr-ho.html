About the Project

Project SP Heartware is an innovative initiative designed to foster community engagement and technological advancement. Spearheaded by Mr. Ho, this project utilizes cutting-edge technology to promote sustainable development, digital literacy, and social inclusion.

Through workshops, online resources, and community events, Project SP Heartware empowers individuals to embrace technology and improve their quality of life. Join us in making a difference!
