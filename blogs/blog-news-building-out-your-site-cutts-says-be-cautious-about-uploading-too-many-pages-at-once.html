Building Out Your Site Cutts

Cutts are an important part of your website's design and can greatly impact user experience. It is important to carefully consider the placement and design of cutts on your site to ensure they are effective and do not distract from the content.

Be Cautious About Uploading Too Many Pages at Once

When building a website, it can be tempting to add as many pages as possible in order to showcase your content. However, it is important to be cautious about this approach as it can lead to slower page load times and difficulty for users navigating your site.
