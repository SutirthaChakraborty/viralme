Ruthless Law Firms & PPC Tactics

Understanding PPC Strategies

Pay-per-click (PPC) advertising enables law firms to bid on competitors' names, directing potential clients to their services. This controversial practice raises ethical questions, but results in high visibility.

Advantages:

  • Increased brand exposure
  • Higher click-through rates
  • Stealing market share from rivals

Concerns:

  • Legal implications
  • Potential backlash from consumers
  • Damage to brand reputation
