Asthma Action Plan

An asthma action plan is a personalized plan that outlines what steps to take to manage your asthma symptoms and prevent attacks.

What is an Asthma Action Plan?

The plan is designed to help you manage your asthma symptoms by identifying triggers, setting goals, and outlining steps to take when your symptoms worsen.

Components of an Asthma Action Plan

  • Step 1: Identify your asthma triggers, such as allergens, irritants, or respiratory infections.
  • Step 2: Set goals for managing your asthma symptoms, such as reducing frequency or severity of attacks.
  • Step 3: Outline steps to take when your symptoms worsen, such as increasing medication or seeking medical attention.
  • Step 4: Monitor your symptoms and adjust your plan as needed.
