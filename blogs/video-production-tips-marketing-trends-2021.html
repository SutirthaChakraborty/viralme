Marketing Trends

1. Short-Form Video Dominance

Short videos are more engaging. Platforms like TikTok and Instagram Reels are perfect for quick content delivery.

2. Live Streaming Popularity

Live streams foster engagement. Consider Q&As or behind-the-scenes content to connect in real-time.

3. Personalization and Storytelling

A personalized approach resonates with audiences. Combine storytelling with testimonials for authentic engagement.
