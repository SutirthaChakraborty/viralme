Blog SEO: 8 Voice Search Statistics in 2019

Understanding voice search is crucial for effective blog SEO. Here are 8 significant statistics from 2019 that highlight its impact:

1. 50% of Searches Will Be Voice Searches

By 2020, it was projected that half of all searches would be conducted via voice.

2. 70% of Voice Search Results Are from Featured Snippets

Optimizing for featured snippets can significantly improve visibility in voice search results.

3. 65% of Smart Speaker Owners Use Voice Search Daily

This statistic emphasizes the growing reliance on voice-activated devices.

4. Long-Tail Keywords Are Essential

Voice searches tend to be longer and more conversational, making long-tail keywords vital.

5. Voice Search Is More Popular on Mobile

Mobile devices account for a significant portion of voice searches, highlighting the need for mobile optimization.

6. Local Searches Are Dominated by Voice Queries

Many users utilize voice search for local information, making local SEO strategies crucial.

7. 25% of Households Have Smart Speakers

This increase in smart speaker ownership indicates a shift in how users access information.

8. Voice Search Will Drive Increased E-Commerce

As voice search grows, so does its influence on online shopping behavior.

These statistics matter because they highlight a shift in consumer behavior towards voice search, which is critical for adapting SEO strategies. Understanding these trends can help bloggers optimize their content for voice queries, leading to increased traffic and engagement.
