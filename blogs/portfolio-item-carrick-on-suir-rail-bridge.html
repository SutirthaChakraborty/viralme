Carrick-on-Suir Rail Bridge

An Engineering Marvel

The Carrick-on-Suir Rail Bridge, located in County Tipperary, Ireland, is a stunning example of 19th-century engineering. This historic bridge spans the River Suir and plays a critical role in connecting communities and enhancing transportation.

Built in the 1850s, the bridge showcases intricate stone masonry and a breathtaking design that has stood the test of time. It is not only essential for rail transport but also serves as an iconic landmark in the region.
