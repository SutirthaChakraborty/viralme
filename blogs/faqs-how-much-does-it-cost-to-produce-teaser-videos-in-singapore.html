FAQs: Cost of Producing Teaser Videos in Singapore

How much does it cost to produce a teaser video?
The cost can vary widely based on the complexity and length of the video. Generally, prices range from SGD 1,000 to SGD 10,000 depending on the production quality and requirements [[1]](https://www.big3.sg/services/teaser-videos).
What factors influence the cost of teaser video production?
Factors include the length of the video, the experience of the production team, location, and additional services like scriptwriting and editing.
How long does it take to produce a teaser video?
Production time can range from a week to several months, depending on the project's complexity [[1]](https://www.big3.sg/services/teaser-videos).
