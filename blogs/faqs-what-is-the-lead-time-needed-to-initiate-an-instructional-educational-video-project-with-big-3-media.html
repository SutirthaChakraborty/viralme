Frequently Asked Questions

What is the lead time needed to initiate an instructional educational video project with Big 3 Media?
The lead time for an instructional educational video project with Big 3 Media can vary depending on the complexity of the project and the availability of resources. Generally, we recommend giving us at least two months' notice to allow sufficient time for planning, production, and post-production. However, if you have a tight deadline or specific requirements, please let us know and we will do our best to accommodate your needs.
