5 Brands Setting the Bar for Pride Month 2022

1. Nike

Nike launched their "Play with Pride" campaign, featuring limited edition Pride-themed sneakers and apparel.

2. Starbucks

Starbucks showcased their commitment to inclusivity with rainbow-themed merchandise and fundraising for LGBTQ+ organizations.

3. Target

Target introduced a vibrant line of Pride products, promoting messages of love and acceptance across their stores and online.

4. Adobe

Adobe celebrated Pride Month with a series of workshops and creative challenges, empowering LGBTQ+ creatives.

5. Airbnb

Airbnb launched "Open Homes," offering short-term housing to LGBTQ+ travelers attending Pride events around the world.
