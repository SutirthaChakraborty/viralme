26% of Brands Avoid Content Analytics - Staffing Issues

Recent reports indicate that 26% of brands are steering clear of content analytics due to a lack of dedicated staff. This oversight hampers their ability to leverage data-driven insights for effective marketing strategies.

Without adequate staffing, brands struggle to interpret data, missing opportunities for engagement and growth. Establishing a specialized team is vital for maximizing content performance and understanding audience behavior.

Tips for Improving Content Analytics

  • Invest in training staff on analytical tools
  • Consider outsourcing analytics tasks
  • Utilize automated analytics platforms
  • Prioritize hiring data-savvy marketers
