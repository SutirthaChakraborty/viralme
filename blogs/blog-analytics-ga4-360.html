Blog Analytics with GA4 360

Understanding GA4 360

Google Analytics 4 (GA4) 360 is a powerful tool for tracking website performance and user engagement on your blog. It provides insights into audience behavior, traffic sources, and content effectiveness.

Key Metrics to Track

  • User Engagement
  • Traffic Sources
  • Conversion Rates
  • Bounce Rates
