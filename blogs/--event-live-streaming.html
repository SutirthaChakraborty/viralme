What is Live Streaming?

Live streaming allows you to broadcast video content in real-time over the internet.

Benefits of Live Streaming:

  • Global Reach
  • Engagement with Audiences
  • Cost-Effective
  • Enhanced Interaction

Frequently Asked Questions:

1. What equipment do I need?
A good camera, microphone, and stable internet connection.

2. How can I enhance viewer interaction?
Use chat features and polls during the stream.

3. Is it expensive?
Costs can vary; many platforms offer free or budget-friendly options.
