FAQs: Cancellation Policies for Live Streaming Videos

What is Platform A's Cancellation Policy?

Platform A allows cancellations within 24 hours of purchase with a full refund. For cancellations after this period, a partial refund may be issued based on usage.

What about Platform B?

Platform B has a 7-day cancellation policy for live events. Full refunds are provided if canceled 48 hours before the event starts.

How does Platform C handle cancellations?

Platform C offers a flexible cancellation policy, allowing full refunds up until 1 hour before the live stream begins. No refunds are issued after that time.
