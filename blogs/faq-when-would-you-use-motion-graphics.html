FAQ: When to Use Motion Graphics

1. What are motion graphics?
Motion graphics are animated graphic designs. They are often used in marketing, advertising, and video production to create engaging visual content.
2. When should you use motion graphics?
Use motion graphics when you want to capture attention, explain complex ideas, promote a product, or enhance storytelling in videos.
3. What are the benefits of using motion graphics?
They increase engagement, simplify information, improve retention rates, and can convey emotions effectively.
