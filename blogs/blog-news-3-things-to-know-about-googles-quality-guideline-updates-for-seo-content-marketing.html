Google now prioritizes user experience over sheer content volume. Ensure your site is easy to navigate, responsive, and mobile-friendly to retain visitors and enhance SEO performance.

Stale content can negatively impact your rankings. Regularly updating your blog articles with fresh, relevant information can boost your visibility in search engines and maintain user engagement.
