What is Whiteboard Animation?

Whiteboard animation is a visual storytelling technique that uses a simple, hand-drawn style. It's effective for explaining complex ideas in an engaging way.

Whiteboard animations typically present content in a step-by-step manner, helping viewers retain information better. They are widely used in marketing, education, and training.
