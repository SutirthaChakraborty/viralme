**Glossary Authorship**

Authorship in a glossary refers to the identification of the individuals responsible for creating and maintaining the glossary content. It is essential for ensuring the accuracy and reliability of the information provided in the glossary.

**Key Elements of Authorship:**

  • Attribution of definitions to specific authors or contributors
  • Revision history and version control
  • Quality assurance processes
