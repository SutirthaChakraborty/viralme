Researching Holiday Purchases

Did you know that savvy shoppers spend around 12 to 15 hours researching their holiday purchases? This deep dive into product reviews, price comparisons, and seasonal deals is essential for making informed buying decisions.

This blog explores the importance of being a well-informed consumer during the holiday shopping season. By investing time in research, shoppers can save money, find the best deals, and avoid impulse buys.

  • Consumers prefer online resources for price comparisons.
  • Social media influences holiday shopping decisions significantly.
  • Email promotions are a key factor in consumer engagement.
