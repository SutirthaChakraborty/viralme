FAQs About Walkthrough Video Projects

What is the lead time needed to initiate a walkthrough video project with Big 3 Media?

The typical lead time to initiate a walkthrough video project with Big 3 Media is approximately 4-6 weeks. This timeframe allows for thorough planning, scripting, and production coordination.

What factors influence the lead time for the project?

Factors include project complexity, required resources, client availability for feedback, and production schedules. Each project is unique, and customization may extend the timeline.

Can the lead time be expedited?

Yes, an expedited service can be arranged by discussing your specific needs with our team. We will do our best to accommodate urgent requests without compromising quality.
