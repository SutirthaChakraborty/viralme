Duchess Cornwall Visits Irish National Stud

The visit of the Duchess Cornwall to the Irish National Stud highlighted her dedication to equestrian culture and interest in horse breeding.

About the Irish National Stud

The Irish National Stud is renowned for its exceptional horse breeding programs, focusing on thoroughbred racehorses.

The stud is a major tourist attraction, offering visitors insights into horse care, breeding, and the rich history of Irish equestrian traditions.
