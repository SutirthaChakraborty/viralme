Lead Time: Typically within 2-4 weeks after receiving all necessary materials and approvals from clients.

Types of Music Videos Handled: All types of music videos including live performances, music videos for social media platforms, promotional videos for concerts/events, etc.

Cost: Pricing varies depending on the complexity of the project and the length of the video. Contact us for a custom quote.

Submission Process: Clients provide us with all necessary materials including script, lyrics, band photos/videos, and any other relevant assets. We review and approve the materials before beginning production. Once approved, we will create a rough concept and send it to the client for feedback. Once finalized, we begin filming and editing the video.

Completion Time: Depending on the complexity of the project and our workload at the time, completion can range from a few days to several weeks. We work closely with our clients to ensure timely delivery of their final product.
