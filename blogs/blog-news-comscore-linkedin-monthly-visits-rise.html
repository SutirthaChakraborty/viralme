Comscore Reports Rise in LinkedIn Monthly Visits

Overview

Recent data from Comscore indicates a significant increase in monthly visits to LinkedIn, highlighting the platform's growing importance in professional networking and job searching.

Key Insights

The rise in visits can be attributed to various factors, including enhanced user engagement features and a surge in remote job postings.

Explore the Data

LinkedIn's monthly visits have increased by 25% over the last year, reaching over 900 million visits in July 2024.
