Blog News Pinterest Mobile Apps

\n

Pinterest has recently released two new mobile apps to help users extend their social reach on the platform. The first app is called \"Promoted Pins\" and it allows users to promote their pins to a wider audience by targeting specific interests and demographics. The second app is called \"Shoppable Pins\" and it enables users to add product links directly to their pins, making it easier for them to shop from the platform. These new apps are aimed at helping users increase engagement and drive more traffic to their profiles.
