Pain Points in Graphic Design - Part 2

1. Communication Gaps

Misunderstandings between clients and designers can lead to frustration. Establishing clear communication channels is essential for successful projects.

2. Time Management

Designers often face tight deadlines. Prioritizing tasks and efficient scheduling can help mitigate stress and enhance productivity.

3. Feedback Overload

Too much feedback can be overwhelming. A structured feedback process ensures that critiques are constructive and actionable.
