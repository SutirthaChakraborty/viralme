What's New in Google Search?

\n

Google search engineer Matt Cutts recently shared some insights on what's new in the search engine. Here are some of the highlights:

\n
    \n
  • Mobile-first indexing is now live for all websites. This means that Google will prioritize mobile content when ranking pages in search results.
    • \n
  • Google is rolling out a new algorithm called \"Medic\" that aims to improve the quality of local search results. This algorithm will take into account factors such as business hours, location, and reviews when ranking listings.
    • \n
  • Google is also making it easier for businesses to manage their online reputation by providing more data on reviews and ratings. This will help companies identify areas for improvement and respond to customer feedback more effectively.
    • \n
