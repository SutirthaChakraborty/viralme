How Much Should You Budget for Visual Content?

Understanding Your Needs

When planning your budget for visual content, consider factors such as the type of content (images, videos, infographics), the quality required, and the platforms you will use.

Low Budget

$100 - $500

Basic graphics and stock images.

Medium Budget

$500 - $2000

Custom graphics and short videos.

High Budget

$2000+

Professional photography and extensive video production.
