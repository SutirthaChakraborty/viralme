Top Animation Companies in the UK

1. Aardman Animations

Known for its stop-motion techniques, Aardman creates beloved characters like Wallace and Gromit. Their innovative storytelling has made them a cornerstone of British animation.

2. Blue Zoo Animation

Blue Zoo is renowned for its high-quality CGI animation and has worked on numerous children's shows, expanding its reach globally with a focus on creativity.

3. Studio AKA

Famed for their unique visual styles, Studio AKA creates content that ranges from advertising to award-winning short films with a strong narrative focus.

4. Framestore

Framestore specializes in visual effects and has contributed to major film productions, blending animation with live-action in seamless ways.

5. Passion Pictures

This company has a reputation for creating heartfelt animated films and commercials, focusing on storytelling that resonates with audiences.

6. The Mill

The Mill combines cutting-edge technology with artistry to deliver stunning commercials and imagery in both animation and VFX.
