Discover Fermanagh Lakelands

Welcome to the Stunning Fermanagh Lakelands

The Fermanagh Lakelands is a breathtaking region in Northern Ireland, known for its serene lakes, vibrant landscapes, and rich cultural heritage.

Lough Erne

Explore the largest lake in Fermanagh, famous for its water sports and scenic beauty.

Lough Melvin

A picturesque spot ideal for fishing and enjoying the tranquility of nature.

Lough Macnean

Known for its diverse wildlife and stunning landscapes; a perfect place for adventure.

Fermanagh is not only known for its stunning lakes but also for its historical sites such as Castle Coole and the Marble Arch Caves. Enjoy activities like kayaking, cycling, and hiking.
