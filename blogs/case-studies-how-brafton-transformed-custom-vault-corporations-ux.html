Brafton Transforms Custom Vault Corporation's UX

Custom Vault Corporation, a leading provider of secure data storage solutions, partnered with Brafton to transform their user experience (UX). The goal was to create a more intuitive and user-friendly interface for their customers.

New UX Design

Brafton's UX design team worked closely with Custom Vault Corporation to understand their customers' needs and pain points. The resulting design was a sleek and modern interface that improved navigation, reduced clutter, and increased accessibility.
