Types of Construction

There are various types of construction including residential, commercial, industrial, and infrastructure. Each type has different requirements, regulations, and practices.

Construction Materials

Common materials used in construction include concrete, wood, steel, and masonry. Each material has its benefits and drawbacks depending on the project.

Construction Safety

Safety in construction is critical. Key practices include wearing protective gear, proper equipment use, and following safety regulations to prevent accidents.

Construction Phases

The construction process typically consists of five phases: design, pre-construction, construction, post-construction, and maintenance.
