Track Your Instagram Metrics

Essential metrics to boost your blog's social media performance

Key Metrics to Track

Engagement Rate

Measure likes, comments, and shares to gauge audience interaction.

Follower Growth

Track your follower count over time for insights into audience expansion.

Impressions vs. Reach

Understand how content is being displayed and to how many unique users.

Performance Benchmarks

  • Engagement Rate: 1% to 5%
  • Follower Growth: 2% per month
  • Reach: Aim for 10% of total followers
