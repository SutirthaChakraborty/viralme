Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I convert my video to a podcast?
You can use audio extraction tools or software to extract the audio from your video files. Platforms like Audacity or online converters can help you with this process.
2. Do I need to edit my audio?
Yes, editing your audio is recommended to ensure clarity and quality. You can remove pauses, background noise, and add music or effects.
3. How do I distribute my podcast?
You can use podcast hosting platforms like Libsyn, Podbean, or Anchor to distribute your podcast to major directories like Apple Podcasts and Spotify.
