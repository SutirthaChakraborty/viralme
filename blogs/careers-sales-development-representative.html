Careers in Sales Development Representative

What is a Sales Development Representative?

A Sales Development Representative (SDR) focuses on qualifying leads and setting up sales appointments. This role is critical in the sales process to drive business growth.

Key Responsibilities

  • Qualifying inbound leads
  • Conducting outbound calls and emails
  • Setting appointments for sales executives
  • Building and managing a pipeline

SDRs often use tools like CRM software to track interactions. Developing effective communication skills is vital.

Skills Required

  • Strong communication skills
  • Time management abilities
  • Problem-solving mindset
  • Familiarity with CRM systems

SDRs should also be comfortable with sales techniques and have a passion for helping customers.

Career Path

Starting as an SDR can lead to various roles in sales such as Account Executive, Sales Manager, or Business Development Manager.
