Holiday Purchase Decisions

How Consumers Consult 12 Sources

Researching Before Shopping

In today's digital world, consumers consult multiple sources before making holiday purchases. Understanding these decision-making processes can enhance marketing strategies and customer engagement.

Online Reviews

Consumers often check reviews on platforms like Amazon and Yelp for feedback on products.

Social Media

Platforms like Facebook and Instagram heavily influence buying decisions, showcasing trends and customer opinions.

Blogs and Articles

Many shoppers seek expert advice through blogs and articles that compare products and provide insights.

Product Videos

Video reviews and unboxing showcases help consumers visualize products better before buying.

Price Comparison Websites

These tools allow consumers to find the best prices and deals available across multiple retailers.
