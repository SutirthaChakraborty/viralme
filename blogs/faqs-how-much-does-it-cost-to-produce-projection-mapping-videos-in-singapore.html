How much does it cost to produce a projection mapping video in Singapore?

The cost of producing a projection mapping video in Singapore can vary significantly depending on factors such as:

  • The complexity of the project
  • Duration and footage requirements
  • Timeline and urgency
  • Skills and experience of the production team
  • Equipment rental or purchase
