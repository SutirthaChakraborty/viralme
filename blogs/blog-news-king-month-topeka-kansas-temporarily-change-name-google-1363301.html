King Month in Topeka, Kansas

Temporary Name Change Announcement

In an exciting turn of events, the city of Topeka, Kansas, has temporarily changed its name to honor the contributions of King Month. This initiative aims to celebrate community and diversity within the region.

About King Month

King Month is a celebration that pays homage to influential leaders and diverse cultural identities. This campaign encourages residents to explore their heritage and foster inclusivity.
