Zero Moment of Truth for B2B Buyers

The zero moment of truth (ZMOT) refers to the point in the buying process where a potential buyer researches a product or service online before making a purchase decision. In the B2B context, the ZMOT is crucial as it allows buyers to gather information, compare options, and evaluate vendors before engaging with sales representatives.
