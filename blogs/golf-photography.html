Golf Photography Guide

Capture the essence of the game with stunning golf photography. Here are essential tips and techniques to elevate your photography skills on the golf course.

Photography Tips:

  • Use a fast shutter speed to freeze motion during swings.
  • Experiment with different angles to showcase the beauty of the course.
  • Golden hour (early morning or late afternoon) provides the best lighting.
  • Capture candid moments of players to narrate a story.
  • Invest in a zoom lens to get close-up shots from a distance.
