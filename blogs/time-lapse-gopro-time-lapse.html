What is Time Lapse?

Time lapse is a photography technique that captures a sequence of images at set intervals to record changes that take place slowly over time. When played at normal speed, time appears to be moving faster.

GoPro Time Lapse Tips

Use a stable tripod for consistent shots and avoid camera shake.
Adjust the interval settings based on the speed of your subject.
Consider using the GoPro time lapse video mode for ease of use.
