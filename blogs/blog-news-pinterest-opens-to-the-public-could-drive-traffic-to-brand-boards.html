Pinterest Opens to the Public

Maximize Brand Exposure

Pinterest has opened its doors to public boards, allowing brands to increase visibility and connect with their audience effectively. By creating engaging content and using relevant keywords, brands can enhance their reach on this visual discovery platform.

Leverage Blogging

Integrating a blog with Pinterest can drive significant traffic to your boards. Publish posts that include keywords related to your niche, and share them on Pinterest. High-quality images from your blog will attract clicks and engagements.

Engagement Strategies

Engage with comments and create interactive pins. Use Rich Pins to attach more information to your idea. Signal to your audience the benefits of clicking through to your content.
