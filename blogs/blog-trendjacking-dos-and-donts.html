Blog Trendjacking Dos and Don'ts

Dos

  • Do stay relevant to current trends.
  • Do add your unique perspective to the trending topic.
  • Do use trending keywords to optimize for SEO.
  • Do share on social media for wider reach.

Don'ts

  • Don't copy content from others.
  • Don't go off-topic just to chase trends.
  • Don't forget to credit original sources.
  • Don't ignore audience engagement.
