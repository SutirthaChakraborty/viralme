Understanding Typography in Design

Typography is a crucial element in web design that affects readability, user experience, and overall aesthetics. Choosing the right fonts and styles can enhance your message and engage your audience.

Key Elements of Typography

  • Font Choice: Selecting appropriate fonts that align with your brand identity.
  • Hierarchy: Using size and weight to create a visual hierarchy that guides the reader.
  • Spacing: Adjusting line height and letter spacing for improved readability.
