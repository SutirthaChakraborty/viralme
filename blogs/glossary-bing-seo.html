Bing SEO Glossary

SEO (Search Engine Optimization)
The process of optimizing a website to rank higher in search engine results, particularly on Bing.
Bing Webmaster Tools
A free service provided by Bing that helps webmasters manage their site's presence in Bing search results.
Keywords
Words or phrases that users type into search engines, crucial for targeting the right audience.
Backlinks
Links from other websites that point to your site, important for improving search rankings.
