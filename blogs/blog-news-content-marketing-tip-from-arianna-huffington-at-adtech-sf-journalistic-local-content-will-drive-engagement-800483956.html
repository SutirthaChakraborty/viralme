Blog News Content Marketing Tip from Arianna Huffington at Adtech SF

Local content will drive engagement. According to a recent study by Adtech, journalistic local content is the most effective way to engage readers and increase brand awareness. In fact, it was found that companies who prioritized local content saw a 57% increase in organic traffic compared to those who didn't. So if you want to boost your online presence and attract more customers, consider incorporating local content into your marketing strategy.
