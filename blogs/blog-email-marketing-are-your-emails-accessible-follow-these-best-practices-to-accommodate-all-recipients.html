Email Marketing Accessibility

Are Your Emails Accessible?

Email accessibility ensures that all recipients, including those with disabilities, can easily read and interact with your content.

Best Practices to Accommodate All Recipients

  • Use Semantic HTML: Structure your email with HTML tags to help screen readers.
  • Provide Alt Text: Use descriptive alt text for images to convey information to visually impaired users.
  • Test Color Contrast: Ensure sufficient contrast between text and background for readability.
  • Keep Layout Simple: Avoid complex designs that may be difficult to navigate.
  • Include ARIA Roles: Implement ARIA landmarks and roles for improved navigation.
