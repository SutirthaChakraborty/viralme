Six Social Media Success Stories of 2010

Discover how brands leveraged social media to achieve remarkable growth.

1. Old Spice: The Man Your Man Could Smell Like

Old Spice transformed its brand image using humorous YouTube videos, becoming an instant viral sensation.

Through personalized video responses to Twitter users, Old Spice engaged directly with fans, leading to a massive increase in sales.

2. Oreo: Daily Twist Campaign

Oreo's Daily Twist campaign featured creative and relevant content relating to current events on social media.

This timely engagement helped Oreo strengthen its brand presence on social media platforms.

3. Starbucks: Twitter and the “Tweet a Coffee” Initiative

Starbucks utilized Twitter to enhance customer interactions, allowing users to send gift cards via tweets.

This innovative approach to social gifting significantly boosted customer loyalty and engagement.

4. Facebook: Open Graph Enhancements

Facebook's Open Graph API allowed brands to connect more deeply with users through shared content.

Brands adapted their marketing strategies to utilize this feature, greatly expanding their outreach.

5. Dove: Real Beauty Sketches

Dove's emotional campaign focused on self-esteem and beauty, resonating with audiences worldwide.

The campaign encouraged conversations about body image, resulting in millions of views and shares.

6. Nike: The Chosen Campaign

Nike empowered its community by allowing fans to vote for athletes to be featured in advertisements.

This interactive approach not only engaged their audience but also fueled anticipation for the ads.
