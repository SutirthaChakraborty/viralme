Creating a Marketing eBook

Step 1: Conduct Keyword Research

Identify relevant keywords to enhance your eBook's visibility. Use tools like Google Keyword Planner to find trending topics.

Utilizing keywords effectively can significantly improve your eBook's reach and engagement.

Step 2: Choose the Right Platform

Select a distribution platform that aligns with your target audience, such as Amazon Kindle or your own website.

Consider factors like royalty rates and ease of use when choosing a platform.

Step 3: Design Your eBook

Use tools like Canva or Adobe InDesign to create visually appealing layouts that attract readers.

Good design enhances readability and keeps your audience engaged.
