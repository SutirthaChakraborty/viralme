Blog News: Authorship Decisions

When Not to Use Authorship

Determining when to attribute authorship in your blog posts can affect your SEO and reader engagement. Here are some scenarios where authorship may not be necessary:

  • Company News Articles
  • Collaborative Posts
  • Evergreen Content Pieces
  • SEO-Driven Keyword Articles

In cases where the content is generic or provides value as a collective entity, such as company updates or informational articles, emphasizing an individual author may not add significant value. Consider focusing on the content's quality and relevance instead.
