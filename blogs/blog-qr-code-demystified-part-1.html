Blog: QR Code Demystified - Part 1

Welcome to our series where we decode QR codes! In this first part, we will explore what QR codes are and how they are used.

What is a QR Code?

A QR (Quick Response) code is a two-dimensional barcode that can store links, text, and data and is scannable by smartphones.

How Do QR Codes Work?

When scanned, QR codes can redirect users to websites, provide information about products, or even initiate downloads.
