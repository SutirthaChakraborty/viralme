What Makes a Great Social Media Strategist

Kari Kennedy's Insights

Kari Kennedy, a lead social media strategist at Brafton, shares her thoughts on the essential skills and traits that define a successful social media manager. From understanding audience engagement to creating compelling content, learn how a strategist influences brand success.

  • Strong communication skills
  • Creativity and adaptability
  • Data-driven decision making
  • Proficient in social media tools
  • Understanding trends and audience behavior
