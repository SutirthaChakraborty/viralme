Blog News: One Third of Online Shoppers to Own Tablets by 2014

Tablet Ownership Trends

By 2014, it was projected that **one third of online shoppers** would own tablets, significantly impacting e-commerce and digital marketing strategies. This shift indicated a growing trend towards mobile shopping, as tablets offered a more user-friendly experience compared to smartphones.

Keywords: Online Shopping, Tablet Ownership, E-commerce Trends, Mobile Shopping, Digital Marketing

The rise in tablet ownership among consumers has led to increased investments in mobile-friendly websites and applications, enhancing user engagement and sales conversions.
