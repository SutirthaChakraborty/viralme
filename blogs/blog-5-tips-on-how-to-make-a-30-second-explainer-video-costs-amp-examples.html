5 Tips for Making a 30-Second Explainer Video

Key Strategies & Cost-Effective Examples

1. Define Your Audience

Understanding your target audience helps tailor your message effectively.

2. Keep It Short and Sweet

Focus on delivering your main message in 30 seconds to keep viewers engaged.

3. Use Engaging Visuals

Utilize colorful animations or graphics to illustrate key points and maintain viewer interest.

4. Include a Clear Call-to-Action

Guide viewers to the next steps after watching your video, whether visiting a website or subscribing.

5. Optimize for Mobile Viewers

Ensure your video is easily viewable on mobile devices to reach a larger audience.
