The Benefits of Video Content for B2B Relationships

Enhanced Engagement

Video content captures attention and increases user engagement significantly.

Businesses using video on landing pages can increase conversions by up to 80%!

Building Trust

Video helps humanize your brand, fostering trust and credibility in B2B relationships.

93% of businesses believe video helps build customer trust.

Effective Communication

Video can simplify complex ideas, making it easier for your audience to grasp concepts.

People retain 95% of information presented in a video compared to 10% when reading text.

Increased Reach

Video content is highly shareable, allowing your message to reach a broader audience.

Video posts on social media get 1200% more shares than text and images combined.
