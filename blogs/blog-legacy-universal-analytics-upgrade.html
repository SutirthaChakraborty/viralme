Universal Analytics has sunset and is no longer available as of July 1, 2024. To upgrade to the new version, Google Analytics 4, follow these steps:

  1. Visit the Google Analytics website.
  2. Sign in to your Google account or create a new one if you don't have an account.
  3. Click on "Admin" in the bottom left corner of the page.
  4. Under the "Property" column, click on "Tracking Info" and then "Tracking Code".
  5. Your Tracking ID will be displayed at the top of the page. Copy this ID.
  6. Scroll down to the "Website Tracking" section and copy the Google tag (gtag.js) code.
  7. Paste the Google tag code into the head section of your website's HTML code, just before the closing tag.
  8. Replace the placeholder "UA-XXXXXXXXX-X" in the Google tag code with your actual Tracking ID.
  9. Save and publish your website.

For more information and resources on Google Analytics 4, you can visit the Analytics Learning Center. This website includes videos, articles, and guided flows to help you get the most out of Google Analytics 4.
