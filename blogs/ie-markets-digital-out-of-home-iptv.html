IE Markets: Digital Out of Home & IPTV

What is Digital Out of Home (DOOH)?

Digital Out of Home (DOOH) refers to digital advertising that appears in public spaces, such as billboards and transit displays. It leverages technology to deliver dynamic content and engage audiences effectively.

Understanding IPTV

Internet Protocol Television (IPTV) is a service that delivers television programming and other video content through the internet. It allows for on-demand viewing and a wide range of channels, enhancing user experience.
