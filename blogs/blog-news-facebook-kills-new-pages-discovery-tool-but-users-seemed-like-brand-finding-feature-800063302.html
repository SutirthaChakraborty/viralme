Facebook Kills New Pages Discovery Tool

Facebook recently announced the discontinuation of its pages discovery tool, which aimed to help users find new pages based on their interests. Users have expressed concerns over this decision, but many are finding the brand discovery feature beneficial for connecting with businesses.

User Reactions

Despite the removal, users appreciate the brand finding function that enables them to engage with businesses and discover relevant content. This shift highlights users' preference for direct brand interactions over page discovery.
