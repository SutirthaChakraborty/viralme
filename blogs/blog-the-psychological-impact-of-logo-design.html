The Psychology of Logo Design

Logos play a crucial role in shaping the perception of a brand. The use of color, shape, and symbolism in logo design can evoke specific emotions and associations in the minds of consumers.

Color Psychology

Color choice in logos can influence how a brand is perceived. For example, **warm colors** like red and orange can convey energy and passion, while **cool colors** like blue and green can evoke feelings of trust and harmony.

Shape and Symbolism

The use of shapes and symbols in a logo can communicate various meanings. **Circular shapes** can signify unity and community, while **angular shapes** may convey strength and stability.
