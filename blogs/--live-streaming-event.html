What is Live Streaming?

Live streaming is the delivery of content in real-time over the internet. It allows users to view or participate in events as they happen.

  • Accessibility: Watch from anywhere.
  • Engagement: Real-time interaction with audiences.
  • Cost-effective: Reach a large audience with fewer resources.
  • Flexibility: Host events at your convenience.
  1. Choose a platform (YouTube, Twitch, Zoom, etc.).
  2. Setup necessary equipment (camera, microphone, etc.).
  3. Promote your event prior to the stream.
  4. Go live and interact with your audience!
  • Ensure a stable internet connection.
  • Engage with the chat for viewer interaction.
  • Experiment with different formats to see what works best.
  • Follow up with your audience post-event.
