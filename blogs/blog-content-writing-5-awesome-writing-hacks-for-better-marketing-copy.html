5 Awesome Writing Hacks for Better Marketing Copy

Tip 1: Repurpose Your Content

Re-purpose your content in several different ways. If you have written a post with a list, dig deeper into each item and write a separate post on each of those items.

Tip 2: Use Topic Generator Tools

Utilize topic generator tools to come up with compelling titles based on keywords related to your niche.

Tip 3: Gather Data from SEO Tools

Collect data from tools such as SEMrush, Moz, and Ahrefs to understand the connections between leading posts in your chosen topic.

Tip 4: Include Relevant Topical References

Ensure your content includes relevant references to current topics and trends in your industry.

Tip 5: Optimize Your Content

Check if your content includes the focus keyword and meets the user agenda. Optimize as needed.
