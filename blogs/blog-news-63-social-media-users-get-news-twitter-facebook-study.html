Social Media News Consumption

Study Insights

A recent study shows that 63% of social media users prefer platforms like Twitter and Facebook to stay updated with news.

As traditional news sources struggle to maintain their audience, social media channels are emerging as primary news outlets.

This trend highlights the power of social media in shaping public opinion and disseminating information rapidly.

In this study, younger demographics are more likely to use social media for news, with Twitter being favored for breaking news.
