Glossary of White Paper Marketing Terms

White Paper
A white paper is an authoritative report that informs readers about a complex issue, often used in business and marketing to present research findings or proposals.
Call to Action (CTA)
A CTA is a prompt on a website or in a document that encourages users to take a specific action, such as downloading a white paper or signing up for a newsletter.
Lead Generation
Lead generation refers to the process of attracting and converting strangers and prospects into someone who has indicated interest in your company's product or service, often facilitated by white papers.
Content Marketing
Content marketing is a strategic marketing approach focused on creating and distributing valuable, relevant content to attract and retain a clearly defined audience.
