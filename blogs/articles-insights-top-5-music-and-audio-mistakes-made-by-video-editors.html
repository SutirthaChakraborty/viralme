Top 5 Music and Audio Mistakes Made by Video Editors

1. Ignoring Audio Levels

Many editors overlook the importance of balancing audio levels, leading to inconsistent sound quality.

2. Poor Music Selection

Choosing the wrong music can detract from the video's message and emotional impact.

3. Overusing Sound Effects

Excessive sound effects can overwhelm the viewer and distract from the main content.

4. Neglecting Background Noise

Failing to eliminate background noise can make the audio unprofessional and hard to listen to.

5. Not Using Proper Formats

Using incorrect audio formats can lead to compatibility issues and poor playback quality.
