Introduction to Time Lapse

Time lapse photography is a technique that captures a series of images at set intervals to record changes that take place slowly over time.

Benefits of Time Lapse

Time lapse videos provide a unique perspective on construction projects, showcasing progress in a condensed format that is engaging and informative.

Techniques Used

Common techniques include interval shooting, using stable mounts, and post-production editing to enhance the final video.

Applications in Construction

Time lapse is widely used in construction to document the building process, monitor progress, and create promotional materials.
