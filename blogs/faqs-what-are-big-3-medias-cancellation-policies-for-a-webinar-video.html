FAQs: Big 3 Media's Webinar Cancellation Policies

What is Big 3 Media's general cancellation policy for webinars?
Big 3 Media typically requires cancellations to be made at least 7 days prior to the webinar date for a full refund.
Can I reschedule my webinar booking?
Yes, participants can reschedule their booking up to 48 hours before the webinar without incurring a fee.
Are there any exceptions to the cancellation policy?
Exceptions may apply in cases of emergencies or unforeseen circumstances. Contact customer support for assistance.
What happens if I miss the webinar?
If you miss the webinar, a recorded version will be sent to your registered email within 24 hours.
