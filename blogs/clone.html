Understanding Clones

A clone is an identical copy of something, often used in various fields such as technology and biology.

Types of Cloning:

  • Biological Cloning: Replicating organisms or cells.
  • Software Cloning: Creating software duplicates or versions.
  • Data Cloning: Copying files or databases for backup or redundancy.

This is a simple representation of a clone.

Cloning copies exist autonomously while maintaining the same characteristics.
