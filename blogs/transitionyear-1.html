Transition Year 1: Understanding the Basics

2019-2020

Transition Year 1 (TY1) is a critical phase in a student's educational journey, marking the beginning of their transition from primary to secondary education. It's a time of significant change, and it's essential to understand the basics to ensure a smooth transition.

In this section, we'll explore the key concepts and skills students need to develop during Transition Year 1. From building confidence and independence to developing essential skills in literacy and numeracy, we'll cover it all.

Stay tuned for interactive lessons, quizzes, and games that will help you navigate this critical phase of your educational journey.
