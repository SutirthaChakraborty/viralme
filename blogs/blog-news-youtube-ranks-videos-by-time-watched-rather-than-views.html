YouTube Video Ranking: Watch Time Vs Views

YouTube prioritizes videos that keep viewers engaged by focusing on watch time rather than just the number of views. This approach helps channels grow their audience and maintain viewer interest.

In recent updates, YouTube's algorithm has shifted towards rewarding content that users enjoy for longer periods. This provides benefits for creators who produce engaging, relevant content.

  • Higher watch time leads to better video visibility.
  • Engaging videos increase subscriber counts.
  • Focus on quality content to enhance watch time.
