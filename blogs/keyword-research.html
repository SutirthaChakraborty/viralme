Keyword Research

Keyword research is the process of identifying and analyzing the keywords and phrases that your target audience uses to search for your products or services online.

Why is Keyword Research Important?

  • Helps you understand what your target audience is searching for
  • Allows you to create content that is relevant to your audience
  • Improves your website's visibility in search engine results pages (SERPs)
  • Helps you track your website's performance and make data-driven decisions
