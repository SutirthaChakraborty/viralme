DuckDuckGo Limits Content Discovery
DuckDuckGo, known for its commitment to privacy, has recently announced limits on content discovery features to protect user privacy. This move comes after Google's controversial changes to its search algorithm, which limited personal search results to improve content discovery.
Google's New Limits on Content Discovery
Google has introduced new limits on content discovery to personalize search results. This change is aimed at improving user experience but has raised concerns about privacy and the potential for bias in search results.
