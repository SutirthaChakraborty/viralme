Insights into Instagram Reels

Understanding Reels Insights

Instagram Reels engage around **2.35 billion active users** monthly, making them crucial for social media marketing.
Reels receive **22% more interactions** than standard video posts, enhancing your reach and engagement.
Utilizing **Topics** in Reels helps the algorithm categorize content, increasing visibility to relevant audiences.
Insights include metrics like **Accounts Reached**, **Peak Concurrent Viewers**, and **Shares**.
Regular posting of Reels can significantly boost your engagement rates.
