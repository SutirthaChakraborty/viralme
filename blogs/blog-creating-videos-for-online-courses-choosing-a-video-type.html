Choosing a Video Type

When creating videos for online courses, the choice of video type can significantly impact the quality of the learning experience. Here are some common types to consider:

  • Screen Recording - Captures the screen and webcam simultaneously, allowing for demonstrations and interactions.
  • Webinar - Interactive sessions with live Q&A and audience participation.
  • Whiteboard Animation - Explains complex topics visually, using simple drawings.
  • Podcast - Audio-only recordings, ideal for learners on the go.
  • Live Streaming - Real-time broadcasting, great for interactive teaching and events.
