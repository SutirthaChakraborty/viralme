Twitter's New Advertising Pricing Model

Understanding Twitter's Pricing Changes

Twitter has recently revamped its advertising pricing model to align with evolving social marketing goals. This update affects how brands can promote their content and engage audiences.

Key Features of the New Model

  • Performance-based costs
  • Enhanced targeting options
  • Flexible budget management

Pricing Breakdown:

1. Cost per Click (CPC): Pay when users click on your ads.

2. Cost per Impression (CPM): Pay based on ad views.

3. Cost per Engagement (CPE): Pay for user interactions.
