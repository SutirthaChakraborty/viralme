Overview

Netflix's Sweet Home is a thrilling series that explores the world of monsters in a modern city. The show delves into the lives of these creatures, their struggles, and how they navigate the complexities of human society.

How They're Made

The monsters in Sweet Home are not just creatures of the night but also characters with depth and complexity. Their creation involves a blend of CGI, practical effects, and the actors' performances to bring these characters to life. This process ensures that each monster is unique and adds to the narrative's richness.

Character Analysis

The series features a diverse cast of monsters, each with their own backstory and motivations. From the gentle giant to the cunning predator, each character brings a different layer to the story, making Sweet Home a compelling watch.
