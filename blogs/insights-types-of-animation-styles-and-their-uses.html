Types of Animation Styles and Their Uses

1. Fade Animation

Show Details

Fade animations are used to smoothly transition objects in and out, creating a calm visual flow. Ideal for website modals and notifications.

2. Slide Animation

Show Details

Slide animations create movement from one direction, enhancing navigation. Commonly used in carousels or sidebar menus.

3. Bounce Animation

Show Details

Bounce animations add a playful touch, perfect for calls-to-action and stress the importance of elements. Great for buttons.

4. Rotate Animation

Show Details

Rotate animations convey movement and dynamism. Often used in icons and logos to grab attention.
