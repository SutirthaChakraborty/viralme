Irish Olympic Council

About Us

The Irish Olympic Council is the national body responsible for the promotion and development of Olympic sports in Ireland.

To inspire the nation through Olympic values and enhanced performance in sport.

To support athletes in their pursuit of excellence at the Olympic Games and promote participation in sports.

Founded in 1922, the Irish Olympic Council has been a significant player in representing Ireland at the Olympic Games.

The Council supports multiple sports including athletics, rowing, boxing, and many others.
