Glossary of Integrated Marketing

**Integrated Marketing**
A strategic approach that aligns and integrates all marketing communications to deliver a consistent message across all channels.
**Brand Consistency**
The practice of ensuring that all marketing materials and messages are uniform in style and tone, reinforcing the brand identity.
**Cross-Channel Marketing**
A marketing strategy that uses multiple channels to reach customers, ensuring a seamless experience across platforms.
**Customer Journey**
The complete experience a customer has with a brand, from initial awareness to post-purchase interactions.
