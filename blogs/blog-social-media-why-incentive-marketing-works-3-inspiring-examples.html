Why Incentive Marketing Works

Incentive marketing is a valuable strategy because it engages a segment of consumers that may not otherwise consider your organization's products or services. Non-monetary rewards, peer recognition, and discounts are all effective incentives that can drive customer engagement [[1]].

Here are three inspiring examples of incentive marketing:

  1. Example 1: McDonald's Monopoly game piece promotion, where customers purchase larger orders of fries to have a chance to win prizes [[1]].
  2. Example 2: Loyalty programs that offer rewards for repeat purchases or referrals [[1]].
  3. Example 3: Gamification techniques that make interacting with a brand or product fun and rewarding [[1]].
