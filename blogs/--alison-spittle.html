Alison Spittle

Discover the life and career of the talented comedian Alison Spittle.

Biography

Alison Spittle is an Irish comedian, writer, and actress, known for her unique sense of humor and engaging storytelling. She began her career in stand-up comedy and gained a significant following through her appearances on various platforms.

Career Highlights

  • Featured on RTE's Republic of Telly.
  • Host of the acclaimed podcast Spittle's Diary.
  • Appeared in the TV series Nowhere Fast.

Fun Fact

Spittle often incorporates personal experiences into her comedy, resonating well with her audience.
