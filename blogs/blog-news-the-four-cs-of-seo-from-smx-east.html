The Four Cs of SEO - SMX East Insights

Content

Quality content is the foundation of SEO. Create engaging and informative articles that resonate with your audience.

Context

Understanding the context of your audience's search intent is crucial. Tailor your content to meet their needs.

Connections

Build connections through backlinks and social media. Foster relationships with influencers in your niche.

Conversion

Optimize your website for conversions. Use clear calls-to-action and user-friendly design to enhance user experience.
