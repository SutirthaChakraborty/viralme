Blog Insights: Avoiding Common Animation Mistakes

When ordering your first video, it's crucial to avoid common animation pitfalls to ensure a successful outcome. Here are some key insights:

1. Overcomplicating Animations: Keep animations simple to maintain viewer attention.
2. Ignoring Brand Identity: Ensure animations align with your brand's image and message.
3. Neglecting Mobile Optimization: Test your animations on multiple devices to ensure compatibility.
