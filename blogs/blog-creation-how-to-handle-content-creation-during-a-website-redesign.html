Effective Content Creation During Website Redesign

Importance of Content Strategy

Developing a solid content strategy is crucial when redesigning your site. It ensures that your content remains relevant and engaging.

Keywords and SEO Optimization

Focus on using relevant keywords to improve your search engine ranking. Consider using tools like Google Keyword Planner to find the best terms.

Engaging Your Audience

Interactive content such as polls and quizzes can help keep your audience engaged during the redesign process.
