2016 Marketing Trends: In-Depth Content

Understanding Content Marketing

Content marketing in 2016 emphasized the importance of creating **high-quality, in-depth content** that resonates with the audience. The trend shifted from quantity to quality, focusing on delivering value through **long-form articles**, **animated videos**, and **interactive content**.

#12DaysOfContent

The #12DaysOfContent campaign highlighted the significance of diverse content formats. Marketers were encouraged to explore various types of content, such as **infographics**, **podcasts**, and **social media posts**, to engage their audience effectively.

Explore Content Types

In-depth content can include **eBooks**, **case studies**, and **webinars**. These formats help in nurturing leads and providing valuable insights to potential customers.
