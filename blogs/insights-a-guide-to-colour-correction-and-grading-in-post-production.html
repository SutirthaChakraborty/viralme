Insights: Color Correction & Grading Guide

Understanding Color Correction

Color correction is the process of adjusting the colors in your footage to achieve a consistent and pleasing look. Key terms include white balance, exposure, and contrast.

Color Grading Techniques

Color grading enhances the mood of your footage. Use tools like curves, luts, and color wheels to achieve your desired aesthetic.
