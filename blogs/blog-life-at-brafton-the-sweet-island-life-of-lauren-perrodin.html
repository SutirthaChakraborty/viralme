The Sweet Island Life of Lauren Perrodin

A Journey of a Brafton Copywriter in Hawaii

Lauren Perrodin, a talented copywriter at Brafton, has embraced the vibrant island life in Hawaii. After moving from Colorado, she pursued her dream of becoming a full-time writer while enjoying the sun and surf.

With her pet Chihuahua Italian Greyhound by her side, Lauren explores new hobbies and adventures, making the most of her time in paradise.

Lauren's journey is not just about work; it's about living life to the fullest. From hiking scenic trails to trying out local cuisine, she shares her experiences through engaging blog posts that inspire others to follow their dreams.
