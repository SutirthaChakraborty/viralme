What to Wear for a Portrait Headshot

**Choose Solid Colors**: Solid colors work best as they keep the focus on your face.
**Avoid Busy Patterns**: Patterns can be distracting and take attention away from your expression.
**Dress for Your Profession**: Wear something that reflects your professional image.
**Accessorize Wisely**: Simple accessories can enhance your look without overwhelming it.

**Consider Your Skin Tone**: Choose colors that complement your skin tone for a more flattering look.

**Fit Matters**: Ensure your clothes fit well; avoid overly tight or loose garments.

**Grooming**: Pay attention to grooming; neat hair and makeup can enhance your overall appearance.
