10 Most Creative Black Entrepreneurs in the World

1. Oprah Winfrey

Oprah Winfrey is an American media executive, talk show host, actress, producer, and philanthropist. She is best known for her talk show, The Oprah Winfrey Show, which aired from 1986 to 2011.

2. Jay Z

Jay Z (Shawn Corey Carter) is an American rapper, entrepreneur, and record producer. He is the co-founder of Roc a Fella Records and the CEO of Tidal Music & Video.
