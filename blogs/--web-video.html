What is Web Video?

A web video refers to digital videos that are available online, from streaming platforms to social media.

Types of Web Video

Includes vlogs, tutorials, webinars, live streams, and promotional videos.

Benefits of Video

Increases engagement, boosts SEO, conveys information effectively.

Video Marketing
