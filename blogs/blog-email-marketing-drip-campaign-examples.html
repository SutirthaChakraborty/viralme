Email Marketing

Email marketing is a powerful tool for businesses to communicate with their customers. It allows for personalized messages, segmented lists, and automated campaigns.

Drip Campaigns

Drip campaigns are a type of email marketing where messages are sent out over a period of time, often to nurture leads or remind customers of their purchases.
