A Day in the Life of a Brafton Designer

Morning Routine

9:00 AM - Arrival at the office, grab a cup of coffee and catch up on industry news.

9:30 AM - Team meeting to discuss ongoing projects and priorities.

Design Time

10:00 AM - Begin working on a new project, designing a series of social media posts.

12:00 PM - Take a lunch break, catch up on some reading and listen to a podcast.

Collaboration and Feedback

1:00 PM - Share designs with the team and receive feedback.

2:00 PM - Make revisions and finalize the designs.

Wrap-up

4:00 PM - Review the day's progress, plan for tomorrow and wrap up the day.
