Blog Legacy and Facebook Likes: An Analysis

Understanding Blog Legacy

The concept of blog legacy refers to the impact and credibility a blog earns over time. However, relying solely on legacy can lead to outdated practices that do not cater to current audience needs.

Are Facebook Likes Flawed?

Facebook likes, while a popular metric for engagement, don't accurately reflect user sentiment or content quality. They can be easily misinterpreted, leading to a skewed understanding of audience interaction.
