Discover the New Search Funnels Feature in Adwords

Adwords has recently introduced a new Search Funnels feature that provides valuable insights into search marketing. This updated functionality can greatly enhance your online marketing strategy by identifying customer behaviors during their search journey.

This feature allows you to track the entire customer journey from first search to conversion. By utilizing these insights, businesses can optimize their Google Ads campaigns for better performance and ROI.
