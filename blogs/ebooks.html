Welcome to the eBook World

What is an eBook?

An eBook (electronic book) is a digital version of a printed book that can be read on a computer or handheld device.

eBooks can be read on various platforms including eReaders, tablets, and smartphones, making reading convenient and accessible.

Benefits of eBooks

eBooks offer portability, space-saving advantages, and easy access to a vast library.

They can also include features like adjustable font sizes, built-in dictionaries, and the ability to highlight text.

Types of eBooks

eBooks come in various formats including PDF, EPUB, and MOBI.

Each format has its advantages, with some being better suited for specific devices or services.
