Terms

  • SXO: Small XOR Large (SXO) is a simple encryption technique that uses the exclusive OR (XOR) operator to encrypt and decrypt data. It is commonly used for small amounts of sensitive information, such as passwords or account numbers.
  • Encryption: The process of converting plaintext into ciphertext, which can only be decrypted using a secret key known only to the recipient.
  • Decryption: The process of converting ciphertext back into plaintext using the same secret key.
