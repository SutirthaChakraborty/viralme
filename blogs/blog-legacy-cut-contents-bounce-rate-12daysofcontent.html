Blog Legacy: Cut Contents & Bounce Rate

What is Blog Legacy?

Blog Legacy refers to the content that remains on a blog after the original topic or season has ended. This could include articles, videos, or other forms of content that are still relevant but no longer the focus of the blog's current activities.

Cut Contents

Cut Contents Explained

Cut contents refer to the process of trimming down or editing content to make it more concise and relevant. This is often done to improve readability, focus on key points, or to ensure that the content remains engaging and on-topic.

Bounce Rate

Understanding Bounce Rate

Bounce rate is a metric used to measure the percentage of visitors who leave a website after viewing just one page. A high bounce rate can indicate that visitors are not finding what they're looking for or that the content is not engaging enough.

12 Days of Content

The 12 Days of Content Strategy

The 12 Days of Content is a marketing strategy where content is released over a period of 12 days, often focusing on a specific theme or topic. This approach can help to increase engagement and create a sense of anticipation among readers.
