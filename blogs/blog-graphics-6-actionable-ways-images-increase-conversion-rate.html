6 Actionable Ways Images Increase Conversion Rate

  • 1. Capture Attention: Use bold and relevant images that stand out, grabbing your visitor's attention instantly.
  • 2. Communicate Value: Illustrate product benefits visually to enhance understanding and encourage purchases.
  • 3. Evoke Emotion: Use imagery that resonates with your audience’s emotions, building connections and trust.
  • 4. Improve Retention: Visuals increase information retention, making your message more memorable.
  • 5. Guide Action: Strategically place images to direct users towards calls-to-action, enhancing navigation.
  • 6. Optimize for SEO: Use descriptive alt texts for images to boost your site’s visibility in search engines.
