FAQs on Marketing Video Projects

What is the typical lead time for a marketing video project with Big 3 Media?
The typical lead time for initiating a marketing video project with Big 3 Media is generally 4 to 6 weeks. This allows for pre-production planning, script development, and scheduling.
Why is the lead time so important for video production?
Lead time is crucial in ensuring that every aspect of the project, from concept to final cut, is executed flawlessly and meets your business goals.
Can the lead time vary depending on the project scope?
Yes, projects with more complex requirements may necessitate additional lead time. It's best to consult with our team for a more accurate estimate based on your needs.
