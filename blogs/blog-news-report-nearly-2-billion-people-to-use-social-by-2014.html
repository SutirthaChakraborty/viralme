Social Media Surge

Nearly 2 Billion Users by 2014

The Growth of Social Media

By 2014, it was reported that nearly 2 billion people were using social media platforms worldwide. This rapid growth represented a significant shift in how individuals connected, communicated, and shared information.

Impact on Society

Social media has transformed relationships, marketing, and the way news is consumed, becoming an essential tool for businesses and individuals alike.
