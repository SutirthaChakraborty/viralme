Insights Media Agency vs. Marketing Agency: Key Differences

What are the Differences?

  • Media Focus: Insights agencies primarily manage communications across media channels, whereas marketing agencies cover a broader range of strategies, including advertising, branding, and digital.
  • Specializations: Media agencies excel in areas like traditional media planning, public relations, and influencer marketing, while marketing agencies have expertise in SEO, PPC, and data-driven strategies.
