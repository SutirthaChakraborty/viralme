What is Climate Change?

Climate change refers to the long-term warming of the planet, which is primarily caused by the increasing levels of greenhouse gases in the Earth's atmosphere.

+

Causes of Climate Change

  • Burning of fossil fuels (coal, oil, gas)
  • Deforestation
  • Agricultural activities
  • Industrial processes

+

Effects of Climate Change

  • Rising sea levels
  • More frequent and severe weather events
  • Changes in precipitation patterns
  • Loss of biodiversity

What Can We Do?

We can all play a role in reducing our carbon footprint and mitigating the effects of climate change. Here are some simple actions you can take:

+

Reduce Your Carbon Footprint

  • Use public transportation or walk/bike
  • Use energy-efficient appliances
  • Turn off lights and electronics when not in use
  • Use eco-friendly products

+

Get Involved

  • Support renewable energy projects
  • Participate in climate protests
  • Donate to organizations working on climate change
  • Spread awareness about climate change
