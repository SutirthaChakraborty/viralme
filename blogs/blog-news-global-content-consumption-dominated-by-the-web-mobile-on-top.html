Mobile Dominance in Content Consumption

In recent years, **mobile devices have become the primary platform for global content consumption**. With the rise of smartphones, users are increasingly accessing news, blogs, and multimedia content on-the-go.

According to recent studies, **over 60% of internet traffic now comes from mobile devices**, highlighting the need for responsive design and mobile-friendly content strategies.
