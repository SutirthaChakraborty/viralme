Pre Production

Pre-production is the phase where planning for a project takes place before the production process begins.

It involves tasks such as scriptwriting, storyboarding, casting, location scouting, and budgeting.

Proper pre-production sets the foundation for a successful project.

During pre-production, decisions are made regarding the creative and logistical aspects of the project.

It helps in identifying potential issues early on and ensures a smoother production process.
