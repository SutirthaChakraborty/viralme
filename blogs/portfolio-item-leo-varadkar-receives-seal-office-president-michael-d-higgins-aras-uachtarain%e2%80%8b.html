Leo Varadkar Receives Seal of Office

On a historic day, Leo Varadkar was formally presented with the Seal of Office by President Michael D. Higgins at Áras Uachtaráin. This significant event marks the transition of leadership in Ireland, symbolizing authority and responsibility.
