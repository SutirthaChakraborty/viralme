Understanding Contextual Advertising

Contextual advertising is a powerful tool that allows advertisers to display ads based on the content of the webpage. By using keyword targeting and topic targeting, ads can be shown to users who are already interested in related topics, increasing the likelihood of engagement.

Studies show that consumers are 69% more likely to engage with relevant ads that fit the website's content. This makes contextual targeting a cost-effective strategy for advertisers looking to maximize their reach and conversions.
