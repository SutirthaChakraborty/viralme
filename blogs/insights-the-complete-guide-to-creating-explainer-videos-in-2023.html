And we're not talking demographics here! We're talking about their likes, dislikes, personalities, and things they care most about. All the above boils down to strategy, which is at the heart of your video production. Without a clear strategy filled with the above insights, your video is going to be just a load of pretty imagery. Which is great and all, but it'll probably miss the mark when it comes to speaking clearly to your target audience.
