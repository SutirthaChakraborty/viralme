Understanding Teleprompters

A teleprompter is a device that displays text for a speaker to read while maintaining eye contact with the audience. It is widely used in television broadcasts, public speaking, and presentations.

Teleprompters help speakers deliver their lines smoothly without looking down at notes. This improves engagement and professionalism during presentations.

  • Enhanced delivery
  • Increased confidence
  • Improved audience engagement
  • Professional appearance
