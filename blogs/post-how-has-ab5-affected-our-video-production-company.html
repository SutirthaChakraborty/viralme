Impact of AB5 on Our Video Production Company

Understanding AB5

AB5 is a California law that redefines the criteria for classifying workers as independent contractors vs. employees.

Effects on Our Company

  • Increased Costs: Hiring freelancers has become more expensive due to benefits and taxes.
  • Project Delays: Adjusting to new regulations has slowed our production timelines.
  • Loss of Talent: Some freelance artists and writers have left the state, affecting our creative pool.
  • More Collaboration: We are now focusing on hiring in-house teams, promoting a stronger culture.
