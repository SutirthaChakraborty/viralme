Event Rider

An event rider is a professional horse rider who competes in the sport of eventing.

  • What is Eventing? Eventing is a triathlon for horses and riders, consisting of dressage, cross-country, and show jumping.
  • Types of Event Riders There are different types of event riders, including professional riders, amateur riders, and junior riders.
  • Skills Required Event riders need to have strong riding skills, horse knowledge, and the ability to work well under pressure.
  • Equipment Event riders use specialized equipment, including saddles, bridles, and riding boots.
