Blog News: 52% Say Page Speed is the Top UX Factor

Understanding Page Speed and User Experience

Recent studies show that **52% of users** consider **page speed** as the most critical factor in their **user experience (UX)**. A fast-loading website not only enhances user satisfaction but also improves search engine rankings.

Click here to learn more about optimizing page speed!

To optimize your page speed, consider compressing images, minimizing CSS and JavaScript, and leveraging browser caching. Tools like Google PageSpeed Insights can help analyze and improve your website's performance.
