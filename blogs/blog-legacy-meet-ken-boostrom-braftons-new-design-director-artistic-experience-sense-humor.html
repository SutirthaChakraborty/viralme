About Ken Boostrom

Ken Boostrom is a seasoned professional with a legacy in the digital realm. He brings a unique blend of artistic vision and a keen sense of humor to his projects. As the new Design Director at Brafton, his impact is already being felt.

Artistic Design Experience

Ken's design philosophy is a testament to his artistic experience. He believes in creating designs that not only look good but also tell a story. His designs are characterized by their simplicity and boldness, making them stand out in the crowded digital landscape.

A Sense of Humor in Design

Ken's approach to design is not just about aesthetics; it's also about injecting a sense of humor into every project. He believes that a touch of humor can make the mundane extraordinary, and his projects reflect this unique perspective.

Exploring the Interplay of Humor and Design

Ken's journey into the world of design has been a fascinating exploration of how humor can be integrated into design. He shares stories and insights into how he approaches projects, always with a sense of humor and a desire to entertain.
