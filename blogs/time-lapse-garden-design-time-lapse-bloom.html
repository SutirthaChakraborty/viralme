What is Time Lapse Gardening?

Time lapse gardening is a fascinating technique that captures the transformation of plants and gardens over time. It allows us to witness the growth, blooming, and overall beauty of a garden in a condensed format.

Understanding Blooming Phases

Different plants bloom at different times. Understanding these phases helps in planning a garden that blossoms throughout the seasons. Use this knowledge to select the right plants for your garden design.

Time lapse photography involves taking a sequence of images at set intervals. When played back at high speed, the result shows changes that take place slowly over time. This technique can highlight the beauty and intricacies of garden design and blooming processes.
