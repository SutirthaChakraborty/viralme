Thornwood Housing Initiative

About the Initiative

Simon Coveney, Minister for Housing, has officially launched a new social housing initiative in Thornwood, Dublin. This program aims to provide affordable housing solutions for families in need. With a focus on sustainability, community engagement, and modern amenities, Thornwood sets a new standard for social housing in Ireland.

Key Features

  • Affordable housing units for low-income families
  • Focus on eco-friendly construction
  • Community facilities and green spaces
  • Partnership with local organizations
