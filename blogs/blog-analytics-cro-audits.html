Blog Analytics & CRO Audits

What is Blog Analytics?

Blog analytics refers to the collection, analysis, and reporting of data related to the performance of a blog. This data can include metrics such as page views, unique visitors, bounce rate, time on site, and conversion rates. By analyzing these metrics, bloggers can understand their audience better and make informed decisions to improve the content and overall performance of their blog.

What is CRO Audits?

Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO) audits are a critical part of improving the performance of a website or blog. These audits involve analyzing the website or blog to identify areas that can be optimized to increase the conversion rate. Common areas of focus include improving the user experience, optimizing landing pages, and ensuring that calls to action are clear and compelling.
