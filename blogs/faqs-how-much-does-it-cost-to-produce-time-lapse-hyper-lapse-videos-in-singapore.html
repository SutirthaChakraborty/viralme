FAQs: Cost of Time-Lapse & Hyper-Lapse Video Production in Singapore

1. What is the average cost of time-lapse video production in Singapore?

The average cost ranges from SGD 1,000 to SGD 5,000 depending on the project's complexity and duration.

2. How much does hyper-lapse video production typically cost?

Hyper-lapse video production usually costs between SGD 1,500 and SGD 6,000, influenced by factors like duration and location.

3. What factors influence the pricing of these videos?

Pricing is influenced by project duration, location, required equipment, and post-production editing needs.

4. Are there additional costs involved?

Yes, additional costs may include permits, travel expenses, and specialized equipment rentals.
