FAQs: Motion Graphics Project Lead Time with Big 3 Media

What is the typical lead time for a motion graphics project?
The lead time for initiating a motion graphics project with Big 3 Media typically ranges from 4 to 6 weeks, depending on the project's complexity and requirements.
What factors influence the lead time?
Factors include project scope, client feedback cycles, and resource availability. Larger projects may require more time for revisions and approvals.
Can the lead time be expedited?
Yes, expedited services are available for urgent projects, but additional fees may apply. It's best to discuss your timeline with our team.
