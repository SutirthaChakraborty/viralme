Understanding Absenteeism

Absenteeism refers to the habitual pattern of absence from work or duty without a valid reason.

Factors contributing to absenteeism include job dissatisfaction, health issues, and personal problems.

Employers often use strategies like flexible work arrangements and wellness programs to address absenteeism.
