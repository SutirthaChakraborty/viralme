Blog News: Survey Says Email Marketing Still Key for Boosting Ecommerce

Why Email Marketing Matters

According to recent surveys, email marketing remains a crucial tool for ecommerce businesses. With high ROI rates, personalized campaigns, and direct communication, businesses can enhance customer engagement and drive sales. In fact, it has been shown that targeted emails can yield an open rate of over 20%.

Survey indicates that 78% of marketers believe email marketing is the most effective channel for customer retention and acquisition in ecommerce.
