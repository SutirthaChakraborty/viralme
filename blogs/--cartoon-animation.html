Learn About Cartoon Animation

What is Cartoon Animation?

Cartoon animation is a form of animation that uses illustrations to create a series of moving images.

History of Animation

Animation has evolved from simple cave drawings to complex software-generated imagery.

Techniques

Various techniques such as 2D, 3D, stop motion, and claymation are used to bring cartoons to life.

Popular Shows

Shows like "Cartoon Network", "Disney", and "Nickelodeon" have shaped the world of animation.
