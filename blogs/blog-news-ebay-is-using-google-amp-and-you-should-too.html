Introduction to Google AMP

Accelerated Mobile Pages (AMP) is an open-source HTML framework that helps create fast-loading mobile-optimized webpages. It restricts certain HTML elements to ensure quick page load times.

eBay's Adoption of Google AMP

eBay has incorporated AMP across its pages to provide a top-notch user experience and increase organic search traffic. This move aligns with the trend of major publishers and e-commerce websites leveraging AMP for improved performance.

Benefits of Using Google AMP

AMP pages load quickly, stand out in search results, and contribute to a better user experience. However, it's important to note the limitations related to dynamic content and interactive elements when implementing AMP.
