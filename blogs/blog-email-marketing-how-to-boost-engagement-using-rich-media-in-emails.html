How to Boost Engagement Using Rich Media

Why Use Rich Media in Emails?

Rich media items like videos, GIFs, and interactive elements can greatly enhance user engagement and increase click-through rates. They help in conveying messages effectively and make your emails more visually appealing.

Example Video

Tips to Implement Rich Media:

  • Keep it short and relevant to the email's purpose.
  • Optimize media for quick loading times.
  • Include calls-to-action next to media.

Enhancing Interactivity

Use polls, surveys, or dynamic content to make your emails more interactive. Interactive emails can drive user engagement and provide valuable insights into customer preferences.
