Understanding Credit Terms

Credit terms define the conditions under which credit is extended. This includes payment timelines, interest rates, and penalties.

Credit terms may include important details such as:

  • Payment due dates
  • Applicable interest rates
  • Fees for late payments

Conditions of credit outline the requirements that must be met to maintain credit privileges. These may include:

  • Maintaining a minimum balance
  • Timely repayments
  • Credit score considerations
