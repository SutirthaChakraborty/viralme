7 Killer Tips for Logo Design that Work

  • Tip #1: Keep it simple and clean. A cluttered logo can be overwhelming and difficult to remember.
  • Tip #2: Use a strong color palette. Choose colors that are easy to read and have a positive impact on your brand image.
  • Tip #3: Make sure your logo is scalable. It should look great at any size, from small business cards to large billboards.
  • Tip #4: Consider using negative space. A well-designed logo with plenty of white space can make it stand out and look more professional.
  • Tip #5: Avoid using too many fonts or symbols in your logo. Stick to one or two key elements that represent your brand identity.
  • Tip #6: Test your logo with different audiences and devices. Make sure it looks good on both desktop and mobile screens, and in different lighting conditions.
  • Tip #7: Don't be afraid to get creative! Experiment with different shapes, textures, and styles to find the perfect fit for your brand.
