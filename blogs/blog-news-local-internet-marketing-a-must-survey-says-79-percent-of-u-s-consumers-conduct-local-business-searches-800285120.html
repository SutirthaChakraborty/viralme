Local Internet Marketing: A Must

Did You Know?

According to recent surveys, **79% of U.S. consumers conduct local business searches**. This highlights the importance of local SEO and internet marketing strategies for businesses aiming to attract nearby customers.

Why Local Searches Matter

Local searches are crucial as they often lead to in-store visits. **50% of mobile users** who conduct local searches are likely to visit a store within a day. This makes optimizing your online presence essential for driving foot traffic.
