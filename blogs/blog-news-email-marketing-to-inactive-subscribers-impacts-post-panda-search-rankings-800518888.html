Impact of Email Marketing on Inactive Subscribers

Email marketing to inactive subscribers can significantly affect your post-Panda search rankings. Brands that neglect to monitor subscriber engagement may face penalties from search engines, impacting their visibility and reach.

Best Practices for Email Engagement

To maintain a healthy email list and improve search rankings, regularly clean your subscriber list by removing inactive users. Implementing a two-click subscription process and providing easy unsubscribe options can enhance user experience and engagement.
