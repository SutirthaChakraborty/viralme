Blog News Google Integrates Newsletter Signup into Paid Search Ads

\n

Google has recently integrated newsletter signup into its paid search ads. This means that when a user clicks on a paid search ad for a news website, they will be prompted to sign up for the website's newsletter before being taken to the article. This is a great way for websites to increase their email list and engage with their audience in a more personalized way.
