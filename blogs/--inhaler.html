About Inhalers

An inhaler is a medical device used for delivering medication into the body via the lungs. It is commonly used to treat respiratory conditions such as asthma and COPD.

Types of Inhalers

  • Metered-Dose Inhalers (MDIs)
  • Dry Powder Inhalers (DPIs)
  • Nebulizers
