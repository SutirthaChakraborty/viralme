Blog Creation: Content Pillars

Learn how to create a successful blog by defining your content pillars.

What are Content Pillars?

Content pillars are the core topics or themes that your blog will focus on. They are the foundation of your content strategy and help to guide your writing and publishing efforts.

Topic Pillars

Topic pillars are the main categories or subjects that your blog will cover. Examples might include technology, health, finance, or entertainment.

Sub-Pillars

Sub-pillars are the smaller, more specific topics that fall under each topic pillar. For example, if your topic pillar is technology, your sub-pillars might include artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, or data analysis.

Content Types

Content types refer to the different formats or styles of content that you will create. Examples might include blog posts, videos, podcasts, or infographics.
