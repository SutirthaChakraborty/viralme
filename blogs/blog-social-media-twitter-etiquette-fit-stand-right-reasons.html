Twitter Etiquette: 5 Key Tips

1. Be Responsive: Engage with your followers by replying to their tweets and comments.
2. Use Hashtags: Incorporate relevant hashtags to reach a larger audience without overloading your message.
3. Be Genuine: Share authentic content that reflects your true self and values.
4. Respect Opinions: Always engage respectfully, even when opinions differ.
5. Post Consistently: Maintain a regular posting schedule to keep followers engaged and informed.
