Why Buying Facebook Likes is Bad

Buying Facebook likes may seem like a quick way to boost your social media presence, but it can lead to several negative consequences:

  • Fake Engagement: Purchased likes often come from fake accounts, leading to low engagement rates.
  • Damage to Reputation: Users can easily spot fake likes, which can harm your brand's credibility.
  • Algorithm Penalties: Facebook's algorithm may penalize accounts with suspicious activity, reducing your organic reach.
