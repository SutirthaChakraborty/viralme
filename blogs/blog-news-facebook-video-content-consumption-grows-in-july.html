Facebook Video Content Consumption Grows in July

In July, video content consumption on Facebook surged dramatically, with users engaging more than ever before. As brands and creators adapt to these trends, understanding audience preferences becomes essential.

Factors contributing to this growth include:

  • Increased smartphone usage
  • Engaging live-streaming content
  • Short-form video popularity
  • Enhanced algorithm targeting
  • Creative storytelling techniques
