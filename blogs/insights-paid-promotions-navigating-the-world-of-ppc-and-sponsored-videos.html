Understanding PPC Basics

Search engine advertising, also known as paid search or search engine marketing, is one of the most popular forms of PPC. It allows advertisers to bid for ad placement in a search engine's sponsored links when someone performs a search related to their business offering.

Insights into Paid Promotions

Paid advertising is not set it and forget it. You need to manage and constantly monitor your ads to ensure that you're reaching optimal results. Management, analysis, and tracking are crucial to a PPC campaign because they provide you with valuable insights and help you create a more effective campaign.
