Glossary of External Linking

Understanding external linking is crucial for effective SEO, website navigation, and user experience. Below are key terms related to external linking:

1. External Link
An external link is a hyperlink that points to a domain other than the one hosting the webpage.
2. Backlink
A backlink is a link from another site to your site, often regarded as a vote of confidence that can improve your site's SEO.
3. Anchor Text
Anchor text is the clickable text in a hyperlink, which tells users and search engines what the link is about.
4. NoFollow Link
A nofollow link is an HTML attribute that indicates to search engines not to pass authority or ranking credit to the linked site.
5. Outbound Link
Outbound links direct users from your site to another site, often used to provide additional resources or references.
