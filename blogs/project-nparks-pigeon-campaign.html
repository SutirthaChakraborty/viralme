About

Project NParks Pigeon Campaign is an initiative to address the growing concerns over the conservation of pigeon species in urban environments. This campaign aims to educate the public, promote awareness, and encourage responsible actions towards the well-being of pigeons and their habitats.

Mission

The mission of the Project NParks Pigeon Campaign is to foster a greater understanding and appreciation for pigeon species, their ecological roles, and the importance of their habitats. By engaging with communities, we strive to implement sustainable practices that mitigate human-pigeon conflicts and enhance pigeon conservation efforts.

Vision

Our vision is to see a harmonious coexistence between humans and pigeon species in urban landscapes, where both can thrive in a balanced ecosystem. We envision a future where pigeons are recognized as integral members of urban biodiversity, protected, and celebrated for their contributions to the environment.

Achievements

Over the years, the Project NParks Pigeon Campaign has made significant strides in achieving its goals. This includes the successful implementation of pigeon-friendly urban planning practices, the establishment of pigeon rehabilitation centers, and the launch of educational programs aimed at fostering a positive relationship between humans and pigeons.
