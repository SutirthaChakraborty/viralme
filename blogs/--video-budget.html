Understanding Video Budget

What is a Video Budget?

A video budget is a financial plan that outlines the costs associated with producing a video project, including pre-production, production, and post-production expenses.

Key Components of a Video Budget

  • **Pre-Production**: Planning, scripting, and location scouting.
  • **Production**: Equipment rental, crew salaries, and set design.
  • **Post-Production**: Editing, sound design, and visual effects.
