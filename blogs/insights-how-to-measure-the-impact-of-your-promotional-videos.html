Insights: Measure the Impact of Your Promotional Videos

Key Metrics to Track

Views
Total number of views indicates initial reach.
Engagement Rate
Measures likes, shares, and comments showcasing audience interaction.
Conversion Rate
Percentage of viewers who take desired actions after watching the video.
Average Watch Time
Indicator of how long viewers engage before dropping off.
Return on Investment (ROI)
Assessment of revenue generated compared to the cost of video production.
