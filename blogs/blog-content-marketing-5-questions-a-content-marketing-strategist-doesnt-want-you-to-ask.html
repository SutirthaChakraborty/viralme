5 Questions a Content Marketing Strategist Doesn't Want You to Ask

1. What is your content strategy?
Understanding the content strategy is crucial for aligning goals and expectations.
2. How do you measure content success?
Metrics such as engagement, conversion rates, and SEO performance are key indicators.
3. What tools do you use for content creation?
Popular tools include Google Analytics, SEMrush, and various content management systems.
4. How do you handle negative feedback?
Addressing negative feedback promptly can enhance brand reputation and trust.
5. What are your thoughts on SEO in content marketing?
SEO is essential for visibility; integrating keywords naturally is crucial for success.
