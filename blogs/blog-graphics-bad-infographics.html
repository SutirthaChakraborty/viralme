Understanding Bad Infographics

Common Mistakes

  • Overloading with information
  • Poor color choices
  • Inconsistent fonts
  • Lack of clear hierarchy

Characteristics of Good Infographics

  • Clear and concise information
  • Effective use of visuals
  • Consistent design elements
  • Logical flow of information

Why Infographics Matter

Infographics enhance understanding by simplifying complex data into visual formats. They can engage readers and improve retention of information.
