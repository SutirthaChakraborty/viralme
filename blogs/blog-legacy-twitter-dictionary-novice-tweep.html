Twitter Dictionary

Key Terms

Blog: A website or web page that is regularly updated with content, often presented in a conversational style.

Legacy: Refers to the lasting impact or history of a Twitter account, especially regarding its followers and tweets.

Novice: A newcomer to Twitter, still learning the ropes of tweeting and engaging with others.

Tweep: A Twitter user, especially a follower or someone you follow.

Twitter Tips:

  • Engage with your audience through tweets and retweets.
  • Use hashtags to increase visibility of your posts.
  • Follow relevant accounts to grow your feed and network.
  • Remember to tweet consistently for audience retention.
