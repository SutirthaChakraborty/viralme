In the world of blogging and content creation, certain language missteps can infuriate editors and impact readability. Below is a list of 17 common word crimes to avoid.

  • 2. Misplacing commas and periods
  • 3. Overusing adjectives
  • 4. Failing to proofread
  • 5. Confusing homophones
  • 6. Mixing up 'affect' and 'effect'
  • 7. Using clichés
  • 8. Incorrect verb forms
  • 9. Run-on sentences
  • 10. Inconsistent tenses
  • 11. Redundant phrases
  • 12. Using jargon or technical language
  • 13. Adverbs without purpose
  • 14. Misusing quotation marks
  • 15. Incomplete thoughts
  • 16. Ambiguous pronouns
  • 17. Neglecting formatting for readability
