Brafton Case Study: Lightning Labels

Lightning Labels, a leading provider of custom labels and stickers, partnered with Brafton to drive traffic to their blog and increase conversions.

Key Statistics:

  • Year-over-Year Increase in Blog Sessions:

    25%

  • Year-over-Year Increase in Conversion Rate:

    15%

  • Average Time on Page:

    2 minutes 30 seconds

How We Achieved Consistent Year-over-Year Increases in Blog Sessions:

Brafton's team of experts developed a comprehensive content marketing strategy, including:

  • Keyword Research:

    We conducted thorough keyword research to identify relevant topics and phrases that would drive traffic to Lightning Labels' blog.

  • Content Creation:

    We created high-quality, engaging content that addressed the needs and interests of Lightning Labels' target audience.

  • Content Promotion:

    We promoted the content through social media, email marketing, and paid advertising to increase visibility and reach.

  • Analytics and Optimization:

    We tracked and analyzed the performance of the content, making data-driven decisions to optimize and improve the strategy over time.
