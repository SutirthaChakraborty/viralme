Experian, Hitwise, and Google are the leading search engines with a market share of 66 percent each. However, Bing falls behind with only 27 percent market share. This means that when people search for something online, they are more likely to find information from these three search engines than Bing.

Google has been around since 1998 and has grown into one of the most popular search engines in the world. It offers a wide range of services, including email, maps, and video streaming. In addition to its search engine, Google also owns YouTube, which is one of the most popular video sharing platforms on the internet.

Experian is a credit reporting agency that provides information about individuals' creditworthiness. They use data from various sources, including credit reports and public records, to generate their scores. Their scores are used by lenders to determine whether to approve loans or credit cards to individuals.

Hitwise is a digital marketing analytics platform that helps businesses understand how their customers interact with their websites and mobile apps. They provide insights into customer behavior, such as what pages they visit, how long they stay on those pages, and what actions they take on those pages. These insights can help businesses optimize their website and improve their customer experience.

Bing is another popular search engine owned by Microsoft. While it may not have the same market share as Google or Experian, it still offers a wide range of services, including web search, image search, and video search. Bing also offers tools for businesses to improve their online presence and reach more customers through advertising.
