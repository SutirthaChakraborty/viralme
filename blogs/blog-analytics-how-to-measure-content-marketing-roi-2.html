Blog Analytics: Measuring Content Marketing ROI

Understanding ROI in Content Marketing

To effectively measure **Content Marketing ROI**, you need to analyze your blog's performance through various metrics.

Key metrics include traffic sources, conversion rates, and engagement levels. Understanding these helps in determining the **value of your content**.

  • Traffic Sources: Organic, Referral, Social Media
  • Conversion Rate: Percentage of visitors completing desired actions
  • Engagement Levels: Average time spent, bounce rate
