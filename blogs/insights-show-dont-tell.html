Show, Don't Tell: A Writer's Insight

Understanding the Concept

The phrase "Show, Don't Tell" urges writers to illustrate their narratives through vivid descriptions and actions instead of merely stating facts. This technique engages readers more deeply, allowing them to experience the story.

Why It Matters
