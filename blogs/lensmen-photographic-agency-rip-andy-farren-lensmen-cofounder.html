About Lensmen

Lensmen is one of Ireland's leading and oldest photography and videography production agencies, covering 70 years of major political and social events. Founded in 1952 by Andrew Farren and Padraig MacBrian in Dublin, Lensmen has a rich history of capturing the essence of Irish life through stunning photography.

**Andy Farren**, one of the co-founders, took millions of stunning, one-of-a-kind photographs, which have great historical value. He photographed the changing times of Ireland, from Eamonn DeValera's cabinets to Mary Robinson's inauguration in 1990 [[1]].

Andy Farren and Padraig MacBrian have left a beautiful legacy of over 3 million negatives of their work, which includes famous faces and historical events [[1]].

Our Services

Lensmen offers a diverse range of services in photography and videography, including corporate public relations (PR) photography, press launches, editorial photography, events coverage, interior photography, and political functions [[7]].

We also specialize in creating 360 Virtual Tours and time-lapse videos, providing a close-up and personal look at various spaces and events [[2]], [[4]], [[7]].
