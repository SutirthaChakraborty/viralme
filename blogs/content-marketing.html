What is Content Marketing?

**Content marketing** is a strategic marketing approach focused on creating and distributing valuable, relevant, and consistent content to attract and retain a clearly defined audience.

Key Elements of Content Marketing

  • **Keywords**: Identifying and using relevant keywords to improve search engine visibility.
  • **Engaging Content**: Creating content that resonates with the target audience.
  • **SEO**: Optimizing content for search engines to increase organic traffic.
  • **Social Media**: Leveraging social platforms to distribute and promote content.
