FAQs: Travel Video Lead Time

What is the typical lead time for a travel video?
Typically, the lead time for a travel video ranges from 4 to 8 weeks, depending on the length and complexity of the project.
How long does production take?
Production usually takes 1 to 3 weeks, including shooting the footage and organizing the content.
What about editing time?
Editing can take anywhere from 2 to 4 weeks, depending on the amount of footage and desired effects.
When do I receive the final video?
After edits and revisions, the final video is delivered 1 week post-production approval.
