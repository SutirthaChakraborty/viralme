3D Animation Benefits:

  • Realistic visuals that enhance storytelling.
  • Great for product demonstrations and complex concepts.
  • Appeals to a tech-savvy audience.

2D Animation Benefits:

  • Cost-effective and quicker to produce.
  • Engaging for a broad audience with various styles.
  • Ideal for branding and simplistic storytelling.
