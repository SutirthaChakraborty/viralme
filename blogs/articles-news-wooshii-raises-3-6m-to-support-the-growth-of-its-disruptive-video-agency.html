Wooshii Raises $3.6M to Support the Growth of Its Disruptive Video Agency

Wooshii, the innovative video agency, has secured $3.6 million in funding to accelerate its growth and expand its disruptive services across various industries.

The investment will enable Wooshii to enhance its technology platform, attract top talent, and provide cutting-edge solutions to clients looking to revolutionize their video content strategies.

With this funding, Wooshii aims to strengthen its position as a leader in the video production space and continue to drive innovation in the industry.
