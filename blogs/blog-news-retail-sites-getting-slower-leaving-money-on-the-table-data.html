Retail Sites Slowing Down: Leaving Money on the Table

The Impact of Speed on Sales

Slow loading pages can severely impact customer experience, leading to high bounce rates and lost revenue opportunities. Studies show that a delay of just one second can reduce conversions by up to 7%!

Key Statistics

  • 47% of consumers expect a web page to load in 2 seconds or less.
  • 60% of users abandon a site that takes more than 3 seconds to load.
  • Faster websites lead to higher engagement and increased sales.

Speed Optimization Tips

  • Optimize images and media files.
  • Minimize HTTP requests.
  • Use content delivery networks (CDN).
  • Implement browser caching.
  • Reduce server response time.
