Celebrate Christmas!

Traditions

Christmas traditions include decorating the tree, exchanging gifts, and festive meals.

Many families also attend church services and sing carols.

Decorations

Common decorations are lights, ornaments, and wreaths.

People often use colors like red, green, and gold to enhance the festive spirit.

Celebrations

Christmas is celebrated on December 25th with family gatherings and festive activities.

Many enjoy special meals, holiday movies, and community events.
