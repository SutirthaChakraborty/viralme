Portfolio Item: Protest Closing Harold's Cross Greyhound Stadium

Overview

The Harold's Cross Greyhound Stadium, a historic venue in Dublin, faced closure due to various factors including economic pressures and changing entertainment preferences. This portfolio item explores the protests surrounding its closure, highlighting community engagement and cultural significance.

Key Issues

  • Impact on local community and jobs
  • Historical importance of greyhound racing in Ireland
  • Alternatives proposed by the community

Protest Highlights

Community members organized rallies, created petitions, and engaged with local media to voice their concerns. The protests aimed to preserve a cultural landmark and advocate for the rights of workers affected by the closure.
