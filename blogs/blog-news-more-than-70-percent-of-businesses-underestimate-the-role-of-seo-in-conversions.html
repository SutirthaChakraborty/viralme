Blog News: Over 70% of Businesses Underestimate SEO's Role in Conversions

What is SEO?

SEO stands for Search Engine Optimization, and it's the process of improving the quality and quantity of traffic to your website or blog from search engines like Google, Bing, and Yahoo.

Why is SEO Important for Conversions?

Conversions refer to the actions a visitor takes on your site, such as making a purchase, filling out a form, or subscribing to your newsletter. SEO plays a crucial role in driving targeted traffic to your site, increasing the chances of these conversions.

Key Takeaways

- SEO helps your website appear in search engine results, increasing visibility.

- Targeted traffic is more likely to convert because they've already shown interest in your content or products.

- Many businesses underestimate the power of SEO, missing out on potential customers.
