Blog News: Google Launches Search Plus Your World

\n

Google has recently launched a new feature called \"Search Plus Your World\" that aims to further personalize search results by showing more relevant information about businesses and local events. This feature uses Google+ data to provide users with more accurate and personalized search results.

\n

With Search Plus Your World, users can see photos, videos, and reviews of local businesses and events, as well as get recommendations for nearby restaurants and attractions. This feature is designed to make it easier for users to find what they’re looking for when they’re out and about in their local area.

\n

In addition to providing more personalized search results, Search Plus Your World also allows businesses to promote themselves on Google search results pages. This can help increase visibility and drive more traffic to their websites.

\n

Overall, the launch of Search Plus Your World is an exciting development for Google and its users. It provides a more personalized and engaging search experience, while also giving businesses the opportunity to promote themselves on one of the world’s most popular search engines.
