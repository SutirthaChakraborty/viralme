70% of Businesses Plan to Boost LinkedIn and Twitter Marketing

In a recent study, it was found that 70% of businesses are set to increase their marketing efforts on LinkedIn and Twitter. This trend reflects a growing understanding of the power of social media in connecting with customers and building brand awareness.

Why LinkedIn and Twitter?

LinkedIn, with its professional network, offers a unique opportunity for businesses to showcase their expertise and engage with potential clients. Twitter, on the other hand, provides a platform for real-time engagement, allowing businesses to respond to customer queries and share news quickly.
