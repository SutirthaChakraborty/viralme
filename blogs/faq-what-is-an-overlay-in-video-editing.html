FAQ: What is an Overlay in Video Editing?

Overlay Explanation

What is an overlay?

An overlay in video editing is a visual element that is placed over the main video content. It can be used for various purposes, such as adding text, graphics, or effects to enhance the viewer's experience.

Why use overlays?

Overlays improve readability, provide context, and can help to hide minor artifacts in the video. They are essential for creating engaging and informative video content.
