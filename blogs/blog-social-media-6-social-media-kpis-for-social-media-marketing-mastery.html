6 Essential Social Media KPIs for Marketing Mastery

Discover the key performance indicators (KPIs) that can transform your social media strategy.

  • 1. Engagement Rate
    Measures how actively your audience interacts with your content.
  • 2. Reach
    Indicates the number of unique users who see your content.
  • 3. Conversion Rate
    Measures the percentage of users who take a desired action.
  • 4. Impressions
    Total number of times your content is displayed, regardless of clicks.
  • 5. Follower Growth Rate
    Tracks the speed at which your social media following increases.
  • 6. Click-Through Rate (CTR)
    Measures the percentage of users who click on a link in your post.
