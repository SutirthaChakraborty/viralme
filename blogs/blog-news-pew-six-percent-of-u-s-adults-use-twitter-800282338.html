Blog News: Twitter Usage Among U.S. Adults

Only 6% of U.S. Adults Use Twitter

Recent studies indicate that only **6% of U.S. adults** actively use Twitter for news and social interaction. This statistic highlights the platform's niche role in the broader social media landscape, where **55% of adults** rely on various social media for news consumption.

Learn More About Twitter Usage

Twitter, while popular among certain demographics, has seen a decline in general usage compared to other platforms. Understanding these trends can help marketers and content creators tailor their strategies effectively.
