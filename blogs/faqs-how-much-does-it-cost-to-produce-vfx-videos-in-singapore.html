How much does it cost to produce a vfx video?
\n
The cost of producing a vfx video in Singapore can vary depending on several factors such as the length of the video, the complexity of the effects, and the number of people involved in the production. On average, a basic vfx video can cost around $5,000 to $10,000, while a more complex video with advanced effects can cost upwards of $20,000 or more. It's important to consult with a professional vfx producer to get an accurate estimate for your specific project.
