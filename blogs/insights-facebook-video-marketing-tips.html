Insights on Facebook Video Marketing

1. Optimize Video Length

The ideal video length for engagement is around 1:30 minutes. Keep your content concise and engaging!

2. Use Facebook Insights

Track metrics like engagement, view time, and audience demographics to refine your strategy.

3. Focus on Storytelling

Engage your audience by telling a compelling story rather than just promoting your product.

4. Promote Your Videos

Consider using paid promotions to increase reach and visibility of your videos.

5. Use Square Videos

Square videos tend to perform better on mobile devices, increasing engagement and views.

6. Tag Relevant Content

Tagging relevant pages and users can help expand your reach and engagement.
