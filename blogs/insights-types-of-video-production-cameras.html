Types of Video Production Cameras

DSLR Cameras

DSLR cameras are versatile and offer excellent image quality. They're great for both photography and video production.

Ideal for filmmakers looking for quality and performance. Popular models include Canon EOS 90D and Nikon D7500.

Mirrorless Cameras

Mirrorless cameras provide high-quality video in a compact body. They are lightweight and ideal for on-the-go shooting.

These cameras are known for their fast autofocus and high frame rates. Examples include Sony Alpha a7 III and Fujifilm X-T4.

Camcorders

Camcorders are specifically designed for video recording. They are user-friendly and usually have extended recording times.

Great for events and schooling, camcorders like Canon VIXIA HF G50 offer quick setup and easy handling.
