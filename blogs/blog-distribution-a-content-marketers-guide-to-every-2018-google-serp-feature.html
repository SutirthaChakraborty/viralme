Blog Distribution: A Content Marketer's Guide to Every 2018 Google SERP Feature

What is Blog Distribution?

Blog distribution is the process of publishing and promoting your blog content to a wide range of platforms, channels, and audiences to increase visibility, engagement, and conversions.

Feature 1: Featured Snippets

Featured snippets are the highlighted answers that appear at the top of Google search results, providing users with quick answers to their queries.

A content marketer can use tools like Ahrefs or SEMrush to identify and optimize their content for featured snippets.

Feature 2: Knowledge Graph

Knowledge graph is a visual representation of entities, concepts, and relationships that provides users with a deeper understanding of the topic.

A content marketer can use tools like Google Trends or Wikipedia to identify and optimize their content for knowledge graph.

Feature 3: Answer Boxes

Answer boxes are the boxes that appear at the top of Google search results, providing users with quick answers to their queries.

A content marketer can use tools like Ahrefs or SEMrush to identify and optimize their content for answer boxes.
