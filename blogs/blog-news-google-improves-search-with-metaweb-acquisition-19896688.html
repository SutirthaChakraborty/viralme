Google Improves Search with Metaweb Acquisition

The Impact of Metaweb on Google's Search Engine

In a strategic move to enhance its search capabilities, Google acquired Metaweb. This acquisition allows for better organization of data, which improves user search experiences.

Metaweb is known for its Freebase knowledge graph, a structured database of facts that the search engine can utilize to deliver more accurate results. This development allows for more relevant search results, enhanced user satisfaction, and a more intelligent search experience overall.
