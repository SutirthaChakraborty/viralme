How to Film Within Government Standards

Adhering to safe filming practices during COVID-19 is crucial. Here are some key points:

  • Maintain Social Distancing: Keep at least 6 feet apart.
  • Wear Masks: Ensure all crew members wear appropriate face coverings.
  • Sanitize Equipment: Regularly disinfect cameras and gear.
  • Limit Crew Size: Keep your team small to reduce exposure.
  • Follow Local Guidelines: Always check and adhere to local regulations.

For more resources, visit [CDC Guidelines](https://www.cdc.gov) and [WHO Resources](https://www.who.int).
