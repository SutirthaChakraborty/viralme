Glossary of Video Marketing Terms

Video SEO
Optimizing video content to improve visibility in search engines and drive organic traffic.
Call to Action (CTA)
A prompt that encourages viewers to take a specific action after watching a video.
Engagement Rate
A metric that measures the level of audience interaction with video content through likes, shares, comments, and watch time.
Video Marketing Funnel
A strategic framework that outlines the journey of a viewer from awareness to conversion through targeted video content.
Live Streaming
A method of broadcasting video content in real-time, allowing viewers to engage with the content as it happens.
