Google October Core Update: What You Need to Know

What is the October Core Update?

The October Core Update is a significant algorithm update by Google that aims to improve the search results for users. This update is designed to reduce the impact of spam and low-quality content, and to promote high-quality and relevant content.

How Does it Affect SEO?

The October Core Update has a significant impact on SEO. It changes the way Google ranks websites and pages in search results. The update focuses on promoting content that is high-quality, well-researched, and relevant to the user's search query.
