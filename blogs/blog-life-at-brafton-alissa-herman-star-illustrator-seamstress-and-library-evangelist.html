Life at Brafton with Alissa Herman

Meet Alissa Herman

Alissa Herman is not just a star illustrator but also a talented seamstress and passionate library evangelist at Brafton. Her creative flair and commitment to community engagement enhance the richness of the Brafton culture.

Through her illustrations, Alissa brings stories to life, inspiring both colleagues and clients. As a seamstress, she emphasizes craftsmanship and creativity in all her projects. Alissa also advocates for the importance of libraries, promoting literacy and access to knowledge for everyone.
