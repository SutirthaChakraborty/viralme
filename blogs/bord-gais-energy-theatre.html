Bord Gáis Energy Theatre

About the Theatre

The Bord Gáis Energy Theatre, located in Dublin, Ireland, is renowned for hosting world-class productions including musicals, operas, and theatrical performances. It enhances Dublin's cultural landscape and attracts both local and international audiences.

Key Features:

  • Seating capacity of over 2,000.
  • State-of-the-art acoustics and stage technology.
  • Architectural brilliance and great visibility from all seats.
  • Home to various arts events and festivals.
