What is a Lensman?

A lensman is a skilled videographer who captures stunning video content using various techniques and equipment. Whether for documentaries, films, or YouTube vlogs, the role requires creativity and technical expertise.

Top 5 Video Tips:

  • Use natural lighting whenever possible.
  • Stabilize your shots using a tripod.
  • Experiment with different angles and perspectives.
  • Pay attention to audio quality; invest in a good microphone.
  • Learn basic video editing techniques for enhancing your footage.
