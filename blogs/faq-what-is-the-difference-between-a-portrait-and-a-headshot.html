FAQ: What is the Difference Between a Portrait and a Headshot?

What is a Portrait?
A **portrait** is a representation of a person, typically showing their face and upper body, and can include artistic elements. It captures the personality and mood of the subject.
What is a Headshot?
A **headshot** is a specific type of portrait that focuses on the face, often used for professional purposes like acting or business profiles. It is typically more straightforward and less artistic than a portrait.
When to Use Each?
Use a **portrait** for artistic representation or personal branding, while a **headshot** is ideal for professional settings, such as resumes or social media profiles.
