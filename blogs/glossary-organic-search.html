Glossary of Organic Search Terms

SEO (Search Engine Optimization)
The process of improving the quality and quantity of website traffic from search engines.
Keyword Research
The practice of identifying and analyzing search terms that people enter into search engines.
Backlinks
Links from other websites that direct traffic to your site, crucial for building authority and ranking higher.
On-Page SEO
Optimization techniques applied directly on the webpage to improve its position in search rankings.
