What are Matterport Virtual Tours?

Matterport virtual tours provide an immersive 3D experience, allowing prospective students to explore college and university campuses from anywhere in the world. This innovative technology enhances the recruitment process by showcasing facilities and creating a sense of presence.

Benefits of Virtual Tours for Institutions

  • **Enhanced Engagement**: Interactive tours keep potential students engaged longer.
  • **Increased Reach**: Attract students globally by offering virtual access to your campus.
  • **Improved SEO**: Virtual tours can boost your website's visibility and ranking on search engines.
