Introduction to Racism at Work

In today's global workforce, understanding and addressing racism at work is crucial for creating a inclusive and equitable environment. Paren Kandola, a leading expert on diversity and inclusion, has dedicated her work to shedding light on the impact of racism in the workplace and offering solutions to combat it.
