Blog Strategy: Remarketing vs Retargeting

Discover the strategic differences between remarketing and retargeting for your blog.

What is Remarketing?

Remarkingeting involves displaying targeted ads to people who have already visited your website but did not make a purchase or take a desired action.

What is Retargeting?

Retargeting is similar to remarketing, but it can also be applied to people who are not current visitors of your website.

Difference

Key Differences:

  • Scope: Remarketing focuses on past visitors, while retargeting can include anyone who visited a competitor's site or browsed a related product.
  • Visitor Engagement: Remarketing requires a visitor interaction with your site, whereas retargeting can target non-visitors.
  • Budget: Retargeting may require a higher budget due to its broader audience.
