Blog News: Freebase Shutting and Knowledge Graph Impact on SEO

What is Freebase?

Freebase was a large collaborative knowledge base that Google used to enhance its Knowledge Graph, which helps improve search results by understanding the relationships between entities.

Impact of Freebase Shutting Down

The shutdown of Freebase means that Google has shifted to other sources for its Knowledge Graph, affecting how information is structured and retrieved in search results.

Understanding the Knowledge Graph

The Knowledge Graph allows Google to provide more relevant search results by understanding the context of queries, which is crucial for effective SEO strategies.
