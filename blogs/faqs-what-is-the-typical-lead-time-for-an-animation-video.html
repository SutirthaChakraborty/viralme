What is the typical lead time for an animation video?

On average, a simple animation video could take anywhere from a few days to a couple of weeks to complete. However, more complex animations may take months or even years to finish. It's best to discuss your specific needs with your chosen artist or team to get a more accurate estimate of the lead time for your project.
