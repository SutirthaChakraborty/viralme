Targeting Your Audience on Facebook Ads

Facebook Ads is most effective when you fine-tune your audience targeting. Use relevant keywords like "digital marketing," "audience segmentation," and "ad optimization" for better reach.

Tips for Audience Targeting:

  • Use Custom Audiences to retarget past website visitors.
  • Utilize Lookalike Audiences to reach new potential customers.
  • Segment your audience based on demographics, interests, and behaviors.
  • A/B test your ads to see which audience segments respond best.
