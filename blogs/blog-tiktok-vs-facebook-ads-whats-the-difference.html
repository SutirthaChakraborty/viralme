TikTok vs Facebook Ads

Understand the Key Differences for Effective Marketing

Key Differences

TikTok Ads

- Highly engaging short-form video content.

- Best for reaching Gen Z and Millennials.

- Utilizes creative challenges and trends.

Facebook Ads

- Extensive targeting options for diverse demographics.

- Supports various ad formats including images, videos, and stories.

- Suitable for B2B and B2C marketing.
