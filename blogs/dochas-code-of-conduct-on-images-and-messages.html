Dochas Code of Conduct: Guidelines for Images and Messages

Your Code of Conduct for Images and Messages at Dochas:

  1. Respect diversity and avoid offensive content
  2. Censorship-free: appropriate use of images, no hate speech
  3. Clear communication: messages should be professional and constructive
  4. No harassment or bullying, including through images
  5. Consent is key: always ensure images shared align with recipients' preferences
