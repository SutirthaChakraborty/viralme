Top Graphic Design Trends for 2016

Trends Overview

In 2016, marketing strategies are increasingly reliant on stunning visuals that enhance user engagement. Here are the top graphic design trends that emphasize usability on mobile and accessibility.

1. Minimalism

Clean layouts and simple typography dominate design this year.

2. Bold Colors

Bright, eye-catching colors capture attention and enhance branding.

3. Responsive Design

Designs adapt seamlessly across devices, ensuring better user experience.

4. Accessibility

Focusing on inclusive design that caters to all users.
