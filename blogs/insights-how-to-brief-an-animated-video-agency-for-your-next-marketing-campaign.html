Insights on Briefing an Animated Video Agency

Learn how to effectively brief an animated video agency to enhance your next marketing campaign. Clear communication and well-defined objectives are key to maximizing the impact of your animated video content.

Key Steps for a Successful Video Brief:

  • Define Your Goals: Identify the purpose of the video - brand awareness, engagement, or conversion.
  • Know Your Audience: Detail demographics and preferences of your target audience.
  • Specify the Message: Clearly outline the core message you want the video to convey.
  • Choose the Style: Discuss preferences such as 2D animation, 3D animation, or motion graphics.
  • Set a Budget: Be transparent about your budget to align expectations.
  • Establish a Timeline: Provide a realistic deadline for project completion.

Additional Tips:

  • Provide Examples: Share references of videos you admire to guide the agency's creative direction.
  • Encourage Collaboration: Be open to feedback and suggestions from the agency.
  • Measure Success: Determine how you will evaluate the video's effectiveness post-launch.
