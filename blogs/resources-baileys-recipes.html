Introduction

Baileys is a brand of Irish cream liqueur produced by Diageo. It is known for its rich, creamy taste and is a popular ingredient in various cocktails. Here, you'll find a collection of delicious Baileys recipes that you can make at home. Whether you're looking for a classic cocktail or a dessert recipe, you'll find something to satisfy your cravings.

Recipes

Classic Baileys Irish Cream Cocktail

A simple yet delicious recipe for a classic Baileys Irish Cream cocktail. Perfect for any occasion.

  • 1 part Baileys Irish Cream
  • 1 part Cream
  • Ice
  1. In a shaker, combine Baileys and cream.
  2. Add ice and shake well.
  3. Strain into a glass and serve.
