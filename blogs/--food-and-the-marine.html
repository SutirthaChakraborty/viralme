Marine Food and Its Importance

Seaweed

Seaweed is packed with vitamins and minerals, and plays a vital role in marine ecosystems.

Fish

Fish are primary protein sources for millions globally and are essential for marine biodiversity.

Shellfish

Shellfish like shrimp and oysters provide vital ecological functions and are delicious delicacies.

Plankton

Plankton form the base of the marine food web, supporting a multitude of marine life.
