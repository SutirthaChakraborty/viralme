Insurance for Film Production

Insurance for film production is essential to protect against unforeseen events that can disrupt or delay the production process. It covers risks such as equipment damage, liability claims, and production delays.

Types of insurance for film production include:

  • Equipment Insurance
  • General Liability Insurance
  • Errors and Omissions Insurance
  • Cast Insurance

Proper insurance coverage can provide peace of mind and financial protection for filmmakers and production companies.
