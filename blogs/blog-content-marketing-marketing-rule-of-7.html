Understanding the Rule of 7 in Content Marketing

What is the Rule of 7?

The Rule of 7 states that a potential customer needs to see or hear your marketing message at least seven times before they take action. This principle is crucial in content marketing as it emphasizes the importance of consistent messaging across various platforms.

Why is it Important?

In a crowded digital landscape, repetition helps build brand recognition and trust. By creating valuable content that resonates with your audience, you can effectively guide them through the buyer's journey.
