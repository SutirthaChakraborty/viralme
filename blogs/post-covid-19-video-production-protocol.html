Post-COVID-19 Video Production Protocol

The post-COVID-19 video production protocol aims to ensure the safety of all involved in video production, including cast and crew members. This protocol includes guidelines for social distancing, wearing masks, and other measures to prevent the spread of COVID-19.

Post-COVID-19 Video Production Protocol Guidelines

  • Social distancing should be maintained at all times.
  • All cast and crew members must wear masks at all times while on set.
  • Frequent handwashing and sanitizing should be encouraged.
  • Equipment should be disinfected regularly.
