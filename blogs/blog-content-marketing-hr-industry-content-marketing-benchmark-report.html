Content Marketing Benchmarks in the HR Industry

Understanding Content Marketing

Content marketing is essential for the HR industry to engage potential candidates and retain existing employees. It involves creating valuable content that attracts and retains a clearly defined audience.

Key Benchmarks

Recent reports indicate that companies focusing on content marketing see a 67% increase in leads compared to those that do not. The average blog post in the HR sector receives about 1,000 views, with a share rate of 5% on social media platforms.

Interactive Insights

Engaging content such as infographics and videos can increase user interaction by up to 80%. Regularly updating your content can also improve SEO rankings significantly.
