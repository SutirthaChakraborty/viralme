Insights: Freelance vs. Agency for Animated Video Production

Why Hire a Freelance Animator?

Freelancers often offer **cost-effective solutions** for your animated video needs. They provide **personalized service** and can be more flexible with timelines. Many freelancers specialize in specific styles, making them ideal for unique projects.

Look for keywords like motion design, 2D animation, and 3D animation when searching for the right freelancer.

Why Hire an Animation Agency?

Agencies typically have a **team of experts** that can handle various aspects of production, from scripting to final edits. They often have a **proven track record** and can manage larger projects more efficiently.

When considering an agency, look for terms like full-service production, brand storytelling, and high-quality animation.
