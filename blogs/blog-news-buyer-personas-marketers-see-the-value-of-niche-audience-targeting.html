Understanding Niche Audience Targeting

What are Buyer Personas?

A buyer persona is a semi-fictional representation of your ideal customer based on market research and real data about your existing customers. It helps marketers understand their audience better and tailor their strategies accordingly.

Value of Niche Targeting

Targeting a niche audience allows marketers to focus their efforts on a specific segment of the market, leading to higher engagement and conversion rates. This approach is particularly effective for businesses looking to differentiate themselves in a crowded marketplace.
