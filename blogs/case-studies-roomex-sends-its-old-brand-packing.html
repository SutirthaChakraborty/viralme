Roomex: Packing Away the Old Brand

Overview of Rebranding

Roomex has undertaken a significant transformation to its brand identity, shedding its old image and adopting a more modern, vibrant approach to connect with a broader audience.

Key Strategies

  • Market Research: Understanding customer perceptions and needs.
  • Visual Identity Overhaul: New logo and color scheme.
  • Enhanced User Experience: Revamping the website and services.

Results

These efforts have led to increased engagement, improved brand recognition, and growth in customer base.
