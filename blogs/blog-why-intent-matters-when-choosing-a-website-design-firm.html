Why Intent Matters When Choosing a Website Design Firm

Understanding the intent behind your project is crucial when selecting a website design firm. This is because the right firm can deliver results that align with your business goals and user needs. Let's explore why intent matters in website design.

Example: A B2B SaaS Company

For a B2B SaaS company, the intent might be to showcase the product's features and benefits, facilitate seamless sign-ups, and encourage user engagement. A firm that specializes in B2B SaaS solutions understands these needs and can create a design that effectively communicates the value proposition and guides users towards their goals.
