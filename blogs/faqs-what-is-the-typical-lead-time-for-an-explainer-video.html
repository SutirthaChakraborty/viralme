FAQs: What is the Typical Lead Time for an Explainer Video?

What is the average lead time for an explainer video?
Typically, the lead time for an explainer video ranges from **2 to 6 weeks**, depending on the complexity and length of the video.
What factors influence the lead time?
Factors include **script development**, **storyboarding**, **animation style**, and **client feedback** cycles.
Can the lead time be shortened?
Yes, using pre-existing templates and clear communication can help reduce the lead time significantly.
