About Daikin

Daikin is a global leader in air conditioning technology and HVAC solutions. Established in 1924, it specializes in creating energy-efficient heating, cooling, and refrigeration systems.

Our product range includes:

  • Air Conditioners
  • Heat Pumps
  • Air Purifiers
  • VRV Systems
  • Chillers

Daikin is committed to developing innovative solutions such as:

  • Inverter Technology
  • Smart Controls
  • Eco-Friendly Refrigerants
  • Energy Recovery Ventilation

Daikin leads with sustainability by:

  • Reducing Carbon Footprint
  • Promoting Renewable Energy
  • Manufacturing Eco-Friendly Products
