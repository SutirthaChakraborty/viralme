Celebrating Four Awards at the 2015 International Davey Awards

About the Awards

In 2015, Matcha Design Bags received four prestigious awards at the International Davey Awards, recognizing excellence in design and creativity.

Awards Won:

  • Gold Award for Best Product Design
  • Silver Award for Innovative Use of Materials
  • Bronze Award for Eco-Friendly Design
  • People's Choice Award
