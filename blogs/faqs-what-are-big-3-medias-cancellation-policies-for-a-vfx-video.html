FAQs: Cancellation Policies for VFX Videos

1. What is the cancellation policy for Company A?
Company A allows cancellations up to 48 hours before the scheduled video shoot for a full refund. Please refer to their terms for specific conditions.
2. How does Company B handle cancellations?
Company B requires a 50% deposit for all VFX projects. Cancellations made 72 hours in advance will receive a partial refund of the deposit.
3. Are there fees for cancelling with Company C?
Company C charges a cancellation fee of $100 if the project is cancelled less than 48 hours prior to the start date.
