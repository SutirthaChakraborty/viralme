Green Screen Photography: A Step-by-Step Guide

Here are some frequently asked questions (FAQ) to help you with green screen photography:

1. What is a green screen?
A green screen, also called a chroma key, is a solid green fabric or backdrop used to replace the background during post-production, making it easier to composite subjects onto different backgrounds or environments.
2. How to prepare the green screen?
Ensure the green screen is perfectly flat, clean, and free of shadows or textures. Use a consistent, evenly lit environment to avoid color inconsistencies.
