Effective Content Marketing: Google My Business Setup Guide

Why Google My Business?

Google My Business (GMB) is essential for local SEO and enhances your online visibility. It helps potential customers find your business easily.

Steps to Set Up Google My Business

  1. Go to the Google My Business website.
  2. Click on "Manage now".
  3. Enter your business name and address.
  4. Select your business category.
  5. Add your contact number and website URL.
  6. Verify your business through postcard, phone, or email.
  7. Optimize your profile with photos and business hours.

Additional Tips:

  • Keep your information up-to-date.
  • Encourage customers to leave reviews.
  • Utilize Google Posts to share updates.
