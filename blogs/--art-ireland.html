Art Ireland

Explore the vibrant art scene in Ireland, from traditional Irish folk art to modern contemporary pieces.

Irish Flag

The Irish flag is a symbol of Ireland's rich history and culture. Designed by Thomas Francis Meagher in 1848, the flag features three equal vertical bands of green, white, and orange.

James Howard Murray

James Howard Murray was a prominent Irish artist known for his impressionist landscapes. Born in 1865, Murray's work was heavily influenced by the Irish countryside.

Paul Ogden

Paul Ogden is a contemporary Irish artist known for his abstract paintings. Born in 1965, Ogden's work explores the relationship between color and texture.
