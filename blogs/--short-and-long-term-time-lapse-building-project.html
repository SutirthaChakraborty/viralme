Short Term Time Lapse

\n

A short term time lapse project involves capturing images or videos of a building project over a period of time. This allows viewers to see the progress of the project in real-time and can be used for marketing purposes.

\n \n
\n
\n

Long Term Time Lapse

\n

A long term time lapse project involves capturing images or videos of a building project over an extended period of time. This allows viewers to see the full completion of the project and can be used for promotional purposes.
