Retail Marketing Budgets Go Digital

Overview

The digital transformation of retail marketing budgets is crucial for businesses to thrive. As studies reveal, companies are increasingly allocating a significant portion of their budgets to digital strategies.

Key Findings

  • Over 60% of retail brands have shifted their focus to digital platforms.
  • Social media advertising accounts for nearly 30% of total marketing budgets.
  • Email marketing shows a high ROI of 42:1 for retailers going digital.
