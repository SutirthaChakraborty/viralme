Submit Spam Content Removal

If you have identified spam content that needs to be removed from search engine results pages (SERPs), you can submit a request for removal.

  1. Visit the search engine's website.
  2. Locate the spam content removal page or form.
  3. Provide the necessary information, including the URL(s) of the spam content.
  4. Submit the form or request.

Once your request is submitted, the search engine will review it and take appropriate action to remove the spam content from the SERPs.
