Copyright and YouTube

**Copyright** is a legal right that grants the creator of an original work the exclusive rights to use and distribute that work. It protects various forms of creative works such as music, art, literature, and videos.

YouTube is a popular platform for sharing and watching videos. However, it is important to be aware of copyright when using YouTube. **Content creators** should ensure that they have the necessary rights to use any copyrighted material in their videos.

YouTube has **policies** in place to enforce copyright. If a video infringes on someone else's copyright, the copyright owner can request to have the video taken down or monetized through YouTube's Content ID system.
