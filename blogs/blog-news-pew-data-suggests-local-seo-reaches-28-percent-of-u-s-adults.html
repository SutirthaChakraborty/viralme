Pew Data Insights

According to recent Pew Research, **28% of U.S. adults** regularly utilize location-based services on their mobile devices. This highlights the growing importance of **local SEO** in digital marketing strategies.

Younger adults, particularly those aged 18-49, are even more inclined to use these services, emphasizing the need for businesses to optimize their online presence through **Google Places** and **social media marketing**.
