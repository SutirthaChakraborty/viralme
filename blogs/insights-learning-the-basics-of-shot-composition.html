Insights on Shot Composition

Rule of Thirds

Divide your frame into a 3x3 grid. Place key elements along these lines or intersections for balanced shots.

Leading Lines

Use natural lines in your scene to lead the viewer's eye to the subject, enhancing visual interest.

Framing

Use elements in your environment to frame your subject, adding depth and focus to your shots.

Shot composition is essential for storytelling, focusing attention, and creating mood. Explore these principles to enhance your photography and videography skills!
