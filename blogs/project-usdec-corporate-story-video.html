About Project USDEC

Project USDEC (U.S. Dairy Export Council) is dedicated to enhancing the global competitiveness of the U.S. dairy industry. Our story focuses on innovation, sustainability, and community.

Join Us in Our Mission

Through strategic partnerships and market insights, we aim to expand opportunities for U.S. dairy exporters. Together, we are shaping a sustainable future.
