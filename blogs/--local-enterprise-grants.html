Local Enterprise Grants

Discover the benefits of local enterprise grants and how they can support your business growth.

What are Local Enterprise Grants?

Local enterprise grants are funding opportunities provided by local governments, foundations, and other organizations to support small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) in their local community.

Types of Local Enterprise Grants

There are various types of local enterprise grants available, including:

  • Startup grants for new businesses
  • Growth grants for expanding businesses
  • Innovation grants for R&D projects
  • Export grants for international trade

Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for local enterprise grants, businesses typically need to meet certain criteria, such as:

  • Being a SME with a certain number of employees
  • Operating in a specific industry or sector
  • Having a certain level of revenue or turnover
