Structuring Your Gold Medal Content Marketing Team

Key Roles in a Successful Content Marketing Team

Content Strategist
Responsible for creating and overseeing the content strategy and aligning it with business goals.
Content Creators
Writers, designers, and videographers creating high-quality content that engages your audience.
SEO Specialist
Optimizes content for search engines to improve visibility and organic traffic.
Social Media Manager
Distributes content across social platforms and interacts with the audience to build community.
Analytics Manager
Tracks and analyzes performance metrics to refine content strategy and measure success.
