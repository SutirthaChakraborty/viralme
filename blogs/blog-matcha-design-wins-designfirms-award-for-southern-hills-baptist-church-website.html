Matcha Design Wins DesignFirms Award!

Outstanding Achievement in Web Design

Matcha Design has been recognized for their exceptional work on the Southern Hills Baptist Church website, winning a prestigious DesignFirms Award. This award highlights their commitment to innovative web design and user-friendly interfaces.

This award-winning project showcases cutting-edge design principles, integrating beautiful aesthetics with functionality. The site is optimized for user experience, making it easy for visitors to access church services, events, and community resources.
