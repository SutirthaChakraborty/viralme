Aontas Community Education Network

Aontas is a community education network that provides resources, support, and networking opportunities for community education providers, policymakers, and other stakeholders.

Our mission is to promote community education and lifelong learning, and to support the development of community education providers and their services.

We believe that community education is a powerful tool for building stronger, more resilient communities, and that it has the potential to bring people together and promote social cohesion.

Our network is made up of community education providers, policymakers, and other stakeholders from across Ireland and beyond.

What We Do

  • Provide resources and support to community education providers
  • Facilitate networking opportunities for community education providers and policymakers
  • Promote community education and lifelong learning
  • Support the development of community education providers and their services

Why Join Us

Joining Aontas Community Education Network can help you:

  • Connect with other community education providers and policymakers
  • Access resources and support to help you in your work
  • Stay up-to-date with the latest developments in community education
  • Contribute to the development of community education and lifelong learning
