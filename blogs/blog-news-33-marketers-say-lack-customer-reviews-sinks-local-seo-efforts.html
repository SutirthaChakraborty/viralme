33 Marketers Say Lack of Customer Reviews Sinks Local SEO Efforts

Recent insights from 33 marketing professionals reveal a pressing issue: the lack of customer reviews can critically undermine local SEO strategies. Reviews are essential for building trust and improving search visibility.

SEO Tips to Boost Review Acquisition

  • Encourage satisfied customers to leave reviews.
  • Respond to all reviews, positive or negative.
  • Optimize your Google My Business profile.
  • Incorporate reviews into your website content.
  • Utilize social media to engage customers.
