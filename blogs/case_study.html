Case Study: Effective Marketing Strategy for E-commerce

Objective: To increase online sales by 20% within 6 months.

  • Conducted market research to identify target audience and their preferences.
  • Developed a comprehensive marketing strategy incorporating social media, email marketing, and search engine optimization (SEO).
  • Leveraged influencer marketing to reach a wider audience.
  • Created targeted ads on Facebook and Google Ads.
  • Optimized product pages for better user experience and conversion rates.
Keywords: Case Study, E-commerce, Marketing Strategy, Social Media, Email Marketing, SEO, Influencer Marketing, Facebook Ads, Google Ads, Product Page Optimization.
