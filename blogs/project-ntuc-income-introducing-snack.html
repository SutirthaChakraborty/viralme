About Snack

SNACK is an innovative initiative by NTUC Income that promotes healthy snacking options in the workplace. It aims to improve employee wellness and engagement through nutritious food choices.

Join us in revolutionizing how we think about snacks, focusing on health, productivity, and convenience.
