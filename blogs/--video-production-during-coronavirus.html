Video Production During Coronavirus

Overview

The COVID-19 pandemic forced many video production companies to adapt quickly to new constraints and challenges. This era reshaped how content was created, distributed, and consumed.

Adapting to New Norms

  • Remote Collaboration: Teams utilized virtual tools for meetings and production planning.
  • Health Protocols: Enhanced safety measures were implemented on set.
  • Home Studios: Filmmakers invested in home production setups.

Technologies in Video Production

  • Live Streaming: Increased use of live video for events and performances.
  • Virtual Reality: Engaging audiences in new, immersive ways.
  • Cloud Editing: Teams could collaborate regardless of location.

Changing Viewer Habits

  • Increased Viewership: More people turned to online content during lockdowns.
  • Demand for Diverse Content: A rise in interest for educational and comforting material.
  • Shift to Digital Platforms: Streaming services saw significant growth in subscriptions.
