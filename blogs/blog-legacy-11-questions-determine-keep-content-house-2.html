Blog Legacy: 11 Questions to Determine What Content to Keep

1. Is this content still relevant to my audience?

Relevance is key to engaging readers.

2. Does this content reflect our brand values?

Consistency with brand values builds trust.

3. Has this content been updated recently?

Outdated information can mislead readers.

4. Does this content attract traffic through SEO?

Use keywords effectively for better reach.

5. Are readers engaging with this content?

High engagement indicates value to your audience.

6. Does this content have potential for updates?

Evergreen content can grow with updates.

7. What do analytics say about this content?

Data-driven insights help decisions.

8. Is this content unique or repetitive?

Unique content enhances value and interest.

9. Are there better alternatives or updates available?

Always strive for the best quality.

10. Does this content align with future goals?

Alignment with goals ensures relevance.

11. What do our readers say about this content?

Reader feedback can provide valuable insights.
