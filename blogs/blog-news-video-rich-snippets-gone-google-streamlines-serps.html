Understanding Rich Snippets

Rich snippets are a form of content on search engine results pages (SERPs) that provide additional information beyond just a link and a short text. They can include details like star ratings, cooking time for recipes, and carousels of the latest news headings.

Example of a Rich Snippet

When you search for a recipe, you might see the cooking time and star ratings displayed alongside the search result.

Google Streamlining SERPs
