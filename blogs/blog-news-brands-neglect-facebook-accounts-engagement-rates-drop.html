Brands' Neglect on Facebook Accounts

The Dwindling Engagement Rates

Many brands are neglecting their Facebook accounts, resulting in a significant drop in engagement rates. In today's digital world, social media presence is crucial for brand visibility.

The average engagement rate for brands on Facebook has fallen below 1%. This decline poses a risk to brand loyalty and customer connection.

Latest Statistics:

  • 75% of brands report a decline in organic reach.
  • Only 20% of followers engage with posts.
  • Facebook's algorithm favors paid content over organic content.
