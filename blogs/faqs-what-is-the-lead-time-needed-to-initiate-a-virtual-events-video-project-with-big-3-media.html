FAQs: Lead Time for Virtual Events Video Projects

What is the typical lead time for initiating a virtual events video project?
The typical lead time for initiating a virtual events video project with Big 3 Media is usually around 4 to 6 weeks. This allows adequate time for planning, scripting, and production.
Why is lead time important for virtual events?
Lead time is crucial for ensuring that every aspect of the virtual event is meticulously planned and executed, from pre-production to live presentation, guaranteeing a professional output.
Can I expedite the lead time?
Yes, there are options available to expedite the lead time. However, this may compromise some aspects of the planning process. Discussing your needs with our team can provide tailored solutions.
