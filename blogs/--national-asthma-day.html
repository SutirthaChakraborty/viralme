What is Asthma?

Asthma is a chronic respiratory condition that inflames and narrows the airways, leading to difficulty in breathing. Symptoms include wheezing, shortness of breath, chest tightness, and coughing.

Common Triggers

Triggers for asthma attacks can include allergens (pollen, dust mites, mold), irritants (smoke, strong odors), strenuous exercise, and respiratory infections.

Managing Asthma

Effective management includes medication, avoiding triggers, monitoring symptoms, and having a written asthma action plan tailored to individual needs.
