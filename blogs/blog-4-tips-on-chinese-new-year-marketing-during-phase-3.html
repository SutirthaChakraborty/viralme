4 Tips for Chinese New Year Marketing in Phase 3

1. Embrace Cultural Relevance

Use elements of Chinese culture like red colors, lanterns, and zodiac symbols in your campaigns to resonate with your audience.

2. Leverage Social Media

Engage customers through platforms like WeChat and Weibo, utilizing hashtags related to Chinese New Year for a broader reach.

3. Offer Exclusive Promotions

Run limited-time offers and special discounts to draw attention during the festive season. Highlighting these on your website is crucial.

4. Create Engaging Content

Produce fun and interactive content, such as quizzes or countdowns to the New Year, that encourages sharing among your audience.
