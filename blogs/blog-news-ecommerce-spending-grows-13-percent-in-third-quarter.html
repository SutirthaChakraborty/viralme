Ecommerce Spending Grows 13% in Q3

The latest report reveals that consumer spending in ecommerce has grown by 13% in the third quarter, showcasing the resilience and ongoing transition to digital retail.

This growth indicates a shift in purchasing behavior as customers increasingly prefer online shopping. Key sectors contributing to this surge include electronics, fashion, and home goods. Marketers are encouraged to leverage these insights to optimize their campaigns and improve user experiences.
