What is the Legacy Content Questionnaire?

The Legacy Content Questionnaire is a tool designed to capture the experiences, skills, and values instilled through culinary education. It serves as a reflective exercise for students and professionals in the culinary arts.

Carly DeFilippo's Approach

Carly DeFilippo emphasizes the importance of storytelling and personal experiences in culinary education. Her insights motivate aspiring chefs to preserve their legacy through culinary innovation.

Why Culinary Legacy Matters

Culinary legacy shapes the future of the culinary industry. By understanding one's roots and influences, chefs can create meaningful dishes that celebrate tradition while embracing modern techniques.
