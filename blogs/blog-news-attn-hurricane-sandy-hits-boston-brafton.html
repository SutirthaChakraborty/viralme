Blog News: Hurricane Sandy Hits Boston

Overview of Hurricane Sandy

Hurricane Sandy, also known as Superstorm Sandy, struck Boston in late October 2012, causing significant damage and disruption. The storm brought heavy rain, strong winds, and flooding, impacting thousands of residents.

Impact on Boston

The storm resulted in widespread power outages, transportation disruptions, and property damage. Emergency services were mobilized to assist those affected, showcasing the resilience of the Boston community.
