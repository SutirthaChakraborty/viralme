Exo Building - Point Square, Dublin

The Exo Building, located at Point Square Dublin, is a landmark of architectural innovation, designed to foster creativity and collaboration among businesses. It stands as a beacon for modern design and sustainable practices at the heart of Dublin.

This building is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities and smart technologies, making it a perfect choice for tech companies and startups. Facilities include flexible workspaces, meeting rooms, and recreational areas that enhance work-life balance.
