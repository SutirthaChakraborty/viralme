2. Establish a Daily Routine

Following a consistent schedule can enhance productivity and maintain work-life balance.

Set specific work hours and stick to them, just as you would in an office environment.
3. Take Regular Breaks

Short breaks throughout the day can boost mental clarity and reduce fatigue.

Step away from your desk, stretch, or take a short walk to recharge.

Leveraging apps for task management can help keep you organized and on track.

Tools like Trello, Asana, or Todoist can help prioritize tasks effectively.

Regular communication fosters collaboration and a sense of team spirit.

Use video calls, chats, or virtual meetings to maintain relationships.
