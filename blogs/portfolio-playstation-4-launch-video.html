About PlayStation 4

The PlayStation 4 is a gaming console developed by Sony Interactive Entertainment. It was launched on November 15, 2013, and has since become one of the best-selling consoles of all time.

Its powerful hardware enables stunning graphics and immersive gameplay experiences, making it a favorite among gamers worldwide.
