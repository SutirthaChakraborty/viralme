FAQs: Lead Time for Interview Video Projects with Big 3 Media

Q: What is the typical lead time for starting an interview video project?
A: The typical lead time for initiating an interview video project with Big 3 Media is generally 4-6 weeks from the initial consultation to the first filming day.
Q: How can I speed up the project initiation process?
A: To expedite the initiation process, it helps to have your goals clearly defined, your questions ready for the interview, and your talent confirmed prior to our initial meeting.
Q: Is there flexibility in the timeline?
A: Yes, we strive to accommodate urgent requests. Please reach out to us directly to discuss your specific timeline and requirements.
