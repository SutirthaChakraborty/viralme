Blog Strategy: Brafton Content Marketing During COVID-19

What to Expect

During the COVID-19 pandemic, Brafton emphasizes the importance of adapting your **content marketing strategy** to meet changing consumer needs. Expect a focus on **SEO optimization**, **social media engagement**, and **email marketing** to maintain connection with your audience.
