How Do Chatbots Work? A Tactical Guide

Explore the AI technology behind chatbots

What is a Chatbot?

A chatbot is an AI application that simulates human conversation through voice commands or text chats, often used in customer service.

How Do Chatbots Work?

Chatbots utilize Natural Language Processing (NLP) to understand user requests, leveraging machine learning to improve responses over time.

Benefits of Using Chatbots

Chatbots offer 24/7 customer support, cost efficiency, and improve user engagement by providing instant responses to inquiries.
