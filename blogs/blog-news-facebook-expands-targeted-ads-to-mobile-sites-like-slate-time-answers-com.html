Blog News Facebook Expands Targeted Ads to Mobile Sites

Facebook has announced that it will expand its targeted advertising capabilities to mobile sites like Slate and Time Answers. This means that businesses can now reach their target audience on these websites more effectively.

To achieve this, Facebook will use data from the user's profile and device information to personalize the ads shown to them on mobile devices. This will help businesses to better engage with their target audience and increase the likelihood of converting leads into customers.
