Understanding Google Panda

Google Panda is a search filter introduced in 2011 to prevent sites with poor quality content from ranking high in search engine results. It aims to promote high-quality websites and penalize low-quality ones.

**Key Features of Google Panda:**

  • Content Quality
  • Thin Content
  • Duplicate Content
