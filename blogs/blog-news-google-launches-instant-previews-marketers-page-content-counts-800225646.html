Google Launches Instant Previews

Overview

Marketers can now enhance user engagement with Google's new Instant Previews feature. With over 800 million content counts, this innovative tool allows users to see a preview of a webpage before visiting it.

Instant Previews offer a significant advantage for content marketing, enabling marketers to showcase their best visuals and drive traffic more effectively.
