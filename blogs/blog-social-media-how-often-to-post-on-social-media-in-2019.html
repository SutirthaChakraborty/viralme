How Often to Post on Social Media in 2019

Maximize Engagement & Reach with Effective Posting Schedule

Optimal Posting Frequency

In 2019, studies suggested that brands should strategically post on social media to enhance their audience engagement:

  • Facebook: 1-2 times a day
  • Instagram: 1-3 times a day
  • Twitter: 3-5 times a day
  • LinkedIn: 1 post per day

Posting consistently maximizes visibility and engagement. Algorithms prioritize active accounts, so maintaining a regular schedule is essential for reaching your audience effectively.
