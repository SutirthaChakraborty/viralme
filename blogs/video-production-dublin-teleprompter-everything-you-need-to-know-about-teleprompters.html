Everything You Need to Know About Teleprompters

A teleprompter is a device used in video production to display scripts or text for presenters to read while looking directly into the camera.

Teleprompters help speakers deliver their lines smoothly and maintain eye contact with the audience.

There are different types of teleprompters, including hardware-based and software-based solutions.

Teleprompters are commonly used in news broadcasts, speeches, and video productions to enhance the delivery of content.
