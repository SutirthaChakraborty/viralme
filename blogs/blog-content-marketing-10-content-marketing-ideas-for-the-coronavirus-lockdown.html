10 Content Marketing Ideas for the Coronavirus Lockdown

Engage Your Audience

1. **Content Audit**: Review existing content to optimize for relevant keywords.
2. **Create Buyer Personas**: Develop detailed profiles of your target audience.
3. **Host Webinars**: Share valuable insights and engage with your audience live.
4. **Interactive Content**: Create quizzes or polls to increase engagement.
5. **Repurpose Content**: Turn blog posts into videos or infographics.
6. **Guest Blogging**: Write for other blogs to expand your reach.
7. **Social Media Challenges**: Encourage user-generated content through challenges.
8. **Email Newsletters**: Keep your audience informed with regular updates.
9. **Virtual Events**: Organize online meetups or conferences.
10. **Content Series**: Develop a series of related posts to keep readers coming back.
