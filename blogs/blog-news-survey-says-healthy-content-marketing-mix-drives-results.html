Healthy Content Marketing Mix

Why a Balanced Content Strategy Matters

A healthy content marketing mix includes blogs, videos, social media, and email newsletters. Survey results show that diverse content types drive better engagement and results, fostering brand loyalty and attracting new visitors.

Survey Says:

  • 40% prefer informative blog posts
  • 30% engage more with video content
  • 25% follow brands on social media for updates
  • 5% enjoy email newsletters the most
