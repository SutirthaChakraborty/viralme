Blog News: Digg and SEO Trends

Understanding Digg's Impact on Blogging

Digg, once a top social news platform, revolutionized how content is shared online. The introduction of Diggbar helped users easily promote articles while enhancing SEO potential.

Why the Diggbar is SEO Friendly

The Diggbar allows users to link back to original content, increasing backlink strength and traffic, essential for an SEO optimized blog.
