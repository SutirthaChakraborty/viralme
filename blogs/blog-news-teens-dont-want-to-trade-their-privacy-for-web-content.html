Teenagers and Online Privacy: The Trade-Off

As teenagers spend more time online, they are faced with a difficult decision: trade their privacy for access to web content.

According to a recent survey, 70% of teenagers believe that online privacy is important, but 60% are willing to trade their privacy for access to free content.

This raises questions about the impact of online privacy on teenagers' lives and the consequences of sacrificing their privacy for online benefits.
