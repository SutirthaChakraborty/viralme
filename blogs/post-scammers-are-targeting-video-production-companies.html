Protect Your Video Production Company from Scammers

Important Alert

Be aware of these common scams targeting video production companies.

Fake Job Offers

Scammers may send out fake job offers, asking for upfront fees or personal information. Always verify the company's legitimacy before sending any payment.

Ponzi Schemes

Be wary of investment opportunities that promise high returns with little effort. These schemes often turn out to be Ponzi schemes.

Counterfeit Products

Some scammers may offer to sell equipment or software at a fraction of the cost. These are often counterfeit and can lead to legal issues.
