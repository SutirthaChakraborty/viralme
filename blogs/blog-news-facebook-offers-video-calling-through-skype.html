Facebook has recently announced a new feature that allows users to make video calls to their friends who are not on Facebook. This innovative feature leverages the Skype platform, enabling seamless video calling between Facebook and Skype users.

This integration is part of Facebook's ongoing efforts to enhance its communication tools, making it easier for users to stay connected with friends and family. With this new feature, users can now enjoy high-quality video calls with anyone, regardless of whether they are on Facebook or Skype.

The announcement was met with excitement by many users, who see this as a significant step forward in the evolution of social media communication. This integration is expected to further strengthen Facebook's position in the competitive social media market.
