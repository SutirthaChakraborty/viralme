Understanding Agriculture

What is Agriculture?

Agriculture is the science and art of cultivating soil, growing crops, and raising animals for food, fiber, and other products.

Types of Agriculture

Agriculture can be classified into various types such as subsistence, commercial, organic, and sustainable agriculture.

Importance of Agriculture

Agriculture plays a vital role in the economy, provides employment, and ensures food security.

Sustainable Agriculture

Sustainable agriculture aims to meet society's food and textile needs without compromising future generations' ability to do the same.
