Overview

DNG 2 is a cutting-edge software for video production that offers unparalleled quality and flexibility. It supports the DNG (Digital Negative) format, which is widely regarded as the industry standard for high-quality digital photography, to enable unmatched color fidelity and dynamic range in video production.

Features

- Advanced color grading and correction

- High dynamic range support

- Support for HDR (High Dynamic Range) workflows

- Robust audio mixing and editing capabilities

How to Use

To get started with DNG 2, you'll need to import your DNG files into the software. Once imported, you can edit your video as you see fit, using the advanced features for color grading, audio mixing, and more.
