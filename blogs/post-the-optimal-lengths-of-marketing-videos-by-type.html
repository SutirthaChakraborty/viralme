Optimal Lengths of Marketing Videos

Social Media Videos

Optimal length: **15-30 seconds**. Short, engaging clips capture attention quickly.

Explainer Videos

Optimal length: **1-2 minutes**. This length allows for detailed explanations without losing viewer interest.

Webinars

Optimal length: **30-60 minutes**. Longer format allows for in-depth discussions and audience interaction.

Product Demos

Optimal length: **2-5 minutes**. Showcase features and benefits effectively to drive conversions.
