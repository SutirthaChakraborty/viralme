Blog SEO: What is User Intent?

Understanding User Intent

User intent refers to the purpose behind a user's search query. It is crucial for effective SEO and content strategy.

Types of User Intent

  • Informational: Seeking information or answers.
  • Navigational: Looking for a specific website or page.
  • Transactional: Ready to make a purchase or complete a transaction.
