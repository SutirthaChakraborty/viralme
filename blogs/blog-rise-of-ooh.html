The Rise of OOH Advertising

What is OOH Advertising?

Out-of-home (OOH) advertising refers to any advertising that reaches the consumer while they are outside their home. This includes billboards, transit advertising, and digital displays.

The global OOH advertising market is expected to reach $50 billion by 2025.

Trends in OOH Advertising

  • Increase in Digital Billboards
  • Greater Targeting Opportunities
  • Integration with Mobile Advertising
  • Sustainability Focus

Consumer Behavior

OOH advertising influences consumer behavior by enhancing brand visibility and engagement, particularly through eye-catching digital formats.
