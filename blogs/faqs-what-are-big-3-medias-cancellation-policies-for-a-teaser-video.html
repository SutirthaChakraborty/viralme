FAQs: Big 3 Media Cancellation Policies for Teaser Videos

What are the cancellation policies for teaser videos?
Big 3 Media typically allows cancellation within 24 hours of booking. After that, a fee may apply.
Is there a refund policy for teaser video cancellations?
Refunds are available for cancellations made within the 24-hour window. Other cancellations may not qualify for a refund.
Can I reschedule a teaser video shoot?
Yes, you can reschedule your teaser video shoot, but it must be done at least 48 hours in advance to avoid any fees.
