For Young and Aspiring Creators

Building Design Confidence

Design confidence is crucial for young creators to express their ideas effectively. Here are a few tips to nurture design confidence:

  • Practice regularly and experiment with different design techniques.
  • Seek feedback from mentors and peers to improve your skills.
  • Stay updated with the latest design trends and tools.

Exploring Pathways in Design

There are various pathways to pursue a career in design. Some popular options include:

  1. Graphic design
  2. User experience (UX) design
  3. Web design
  4. Product design

Research and explore these different pathways to find the one that suits your interests and goals.
