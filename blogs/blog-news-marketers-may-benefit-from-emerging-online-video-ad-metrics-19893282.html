Video Ad Metrics for Marketers

Unlocking the Power of Emerging Metrics

As video marketing continues to evolve, new online video ad metrics are emerging that can significantly benefit marketers. Understanding these metrics helps optimize ad campaigns, improve ROI, and enhance viewer engagement.

  • View-Through Rate (VTR)
  • Cost Per Completed View (CPCV)
  • Engagement Rate
  • Click-Through Rate (CTR)
  • Audience Retention
