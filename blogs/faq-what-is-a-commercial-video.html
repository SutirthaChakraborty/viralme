FAQ: What is a Commercial Video?

What is a commercial video?
A commercial video is a short film or promotional content created to advertise a product, service, or brand. It aims to engage viewers and convey a marketing message effectively.
Why are commercial videos important?
They enhance brand visibility, boost engagement on social media, and increase conversion rates by showcasing products in an appealing way.
How long should a commercial video be?
Typically, commercial videos range from 15 to 60 seconds, as shorter formats have been shown to capture attention better.
