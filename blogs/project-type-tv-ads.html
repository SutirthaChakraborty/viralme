What are TV Ads?

TV ads are short promotional videos that air on television to promote products, services, or brands. They are designed to capture the audience's attention and convey a message effectively.

Types of TV Ads

  • Commercials
  • Infomercials
  • Public Service Announcements (PSAs)
  • Sponsored Content
