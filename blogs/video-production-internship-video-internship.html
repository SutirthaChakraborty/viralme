Video Production Internship

Unlock your potential in the world of video production with our internship program. Gain hands-on experience in scriptwriting, filming, editing, and post-production processes.

As an intern, you will assist in storyboarding, camera operation, lighting setups, and editing software, while learning the essential skills needed for a successful career in video production.

Strong communication, creativity, video editing software (e.g., Adobe Premiere, Final Cut Pro), basic camera operation, and teamwork skills are essential for any aspiring video intern.

Interning in video production provides valuable insights, networking opportunities, and a chance to build a portfolio that showcases your work to potential employers.

Interested candidates can submit their resume and a cover letter detailing their passion for video production to our HR department.
