Welcome to the Social Media Category

**Social media** refers to websites and applications that enable users to create and share content or to participate in social networking. Some popular social media platforms include Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, LinkedIn, and TikTok.

**Key features** of social media platforms include user profiles, friend connections, messaging, photo and video sharing, and the ability to follow and interact with other users' content.

**Impact** of social media on society includes influencing public opinion, connecting people globally, facilitating business marketing, and raising awareness about social issues.
