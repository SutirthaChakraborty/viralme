Blog News: Google Places Listings Guidelines Clarified

Understanding Google Places Updates

Google has recently clarified its listings guidelines for local businesses. These updates aim to improve the accuracy and reliability of business listings on Google Places.

Key Changes to Note

  • Verification requirements for local listings.
  • Prohibition of inaccurate business information.
  • Encouragement of customer reviews to boost visibility.

For detailed guidelines, businesses should refer to the official Google Business Support.
