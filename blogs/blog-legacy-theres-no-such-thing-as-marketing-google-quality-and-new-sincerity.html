The concept of marketing legacy refers to the traditional methods of marketing that rely heavily on outdated models. In today's digital world, these methods must adapt to survive.

Legacy marketing often lacks the engagement that modern consumers crave. Understanding this shift is crucial for any brand aiming to thrive.
