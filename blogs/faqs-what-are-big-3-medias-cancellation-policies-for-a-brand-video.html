Big 3 Media Cancellation Policies FAQ

1. What is the cancellation policy for a brand video project?

Brands must notify Big 3 Media at least 14 days before the scheduled shoot date for a full refund. Cancellations made within 14 days may incur a fee.

2. Are there any fees for rescheduling a brand video?

Rescheduling a brand video project is allowed with a notification of at least 7 days prior to the project date. A rescheduling fee may apply.

3. Can payments be refunded after the project starts?

Once the production has begun, payments are non-refundable. We recommend reviewing the project details thoroughly before the start date.
