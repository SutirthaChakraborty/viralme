Tutorial Video Project FAQs

What is the lead time to initiate a tutorial video project with Big 3 Media?
The typical lead time to start your tutorial video project with Big 3 Media is approximately 4 to 6 weeks. This includes pre-production planning, script development, and scheduling filming dates.
Why is lead time important for our video project?
Adequate lead time is crucial to ensure quality production. It allows for thorough scriptwriting, effective resource allocation, and proper feedback cycles, enhancing the overall tutorial video quality.
Can we expedite the process?
We can work to accommodate urgent requests, but this may impact the quality of production. Please discuss your timeline with our team for best results.
