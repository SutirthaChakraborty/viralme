Top 3 Budgeting Mistakes in Web Video Productions

1. Underestimating Production Costs

Many creators overlook the full scope of production expenses, leading to financial shortfalls. Always itemize every detail from crew salaries to equipment rentals.

2. Ignoring Post-Production Expenses

Post-production is a vital phase that often gets neglected in budgeting. It's crucial to allocate sufficient funds for editing, color correction, sound design, and visual effects.
