Video Insights: What You Need to Know

Video insights are essential for any video content creator or marketer. They provide valuable information about how your videos are performing and what you can do to improve their effectiveness.

  • View Count: The number of times your video has been viewed.
  • Engagement: The number of likes, comments, and shares your video has received.
  • Completion Rate: The percentage of viewers who have watched your video to the end.
  • Drop-Off Points: The points in your video where viewers are most likely to stop watching.
