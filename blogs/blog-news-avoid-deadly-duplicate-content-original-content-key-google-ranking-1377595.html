Avoid Deadly Duplicate Content for Better Google Ranking

\t\t

Duplicate content on your website can have a negative impact on your search engine ranking. Google values original and high-quality content, so it's essential to avoid duplicate content on your site. This includes duplicate titles, meta descriptions, headings, and even text within paragraphs.

\t\t

To avoid this issue, create unique and engaging content for each post or page on your website. Use descriptive and keyword-rich titles that accurately reflect the content of your post. Additionally, make sure to use different headlines and subheadings throughout your content to keep readers engaged.
