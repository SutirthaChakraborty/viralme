FAQs on Big 3 Media Cancellation Policies

What is the cancellation policy for Big Media 1?
Big Media 1 allows cancellation within 30 days of service activation. Customers may be subject to a fee for early termination.
How can I cancel my subscription with Big Media 2?
To cancel, log into your account and follow the 'Account Settings' to find the cancellation option or contact customer support.
Are there any penalties for canceling Big Media 3 services?
Big Media 3 imposes a penalty fee if you cancel within the first 60 days of subscription.
