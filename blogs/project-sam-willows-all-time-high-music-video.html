Sam Willows - All Time High

About the Music Video

The "All Time High" music video by The Sam Willows showcases stunning visuals and a captivating storyline that resonates with fans around the world.

The video reflects themes of love, aspiration, and emotional highs, perfectly complemented by the group's harmonious vocals and contemporary pop sound. Released in 2020, it quickly gained popularity, highlighting the band's evolution and artistic growth.
