Shed Light on Dark Social Traffic

What is Dark Social Traffic?

Dark social traffic refers to social shares that are not attributed to a specific source. This often includes content shared via private messages, emails, and secure browsing sessions.

Why Monitor Dark Social?

Understanding dark social can provide deeper insights into user behavior, improve your marketing strategies, and enhance user engagement.

Tips for Tracking Dark Social in Google Analytics

  • Utilize UTM parameters for tracking shared links.
  • Analyze referral traffic and identify dark sources.
  • Use secondary dimensions in Google Analytics for better reporting.
Show Interactive Example

Imagine you share a blog post link via private message. When someone clicks your link, it appears as direct traffic in Google Analytics. The UTM parameters help you track this activity and categorize it correctly.
