Project NHB Sun Yat Sen

About the Project

Project NHB Sun Yat Sen aims to preserve the rich cultural heritage associated with Dr. Sun Yat-sen, a pivotal figure in Chinese history and the founding father of the Republic of China. This initiative focuses on revitalizing historic sites while promoting urban development.

Interactive Component

This project involves collaborative efforts from local authorities, historians, and urban planners to maintain Sun Yat-sen's legacy while integrating modern infrastructure. The preservation of historical artifacts and buildings is essential to educating future generations.
