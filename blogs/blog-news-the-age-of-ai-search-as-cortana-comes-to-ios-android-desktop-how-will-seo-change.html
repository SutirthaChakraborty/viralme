The Age of AI in Search and How It Will Affect SEO

Cortana for iOS

With the introduction of Cortana on iOS, users can now access voice search capabilities directly from their iPhone or iPad. This will change the way people interact with search engines and how they find information online.

Cortana for Android

Similar to its iOS counterpart, Cortana is now available for Android users. This means that users can use voice search on their smartphones without having to rely on a physical keyboard or touch screen.

Cortana on Desktop

Cortana is also expanding its reach by integrating with desktop search engines like Bing and Google. This means that users can now search using voice commands on their desktop computers as well.
