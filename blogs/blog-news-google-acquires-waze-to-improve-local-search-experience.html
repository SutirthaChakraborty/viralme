Google Acquires Waze to Improve Local Search Experience

Google has recently announced the acquisition of Waze, a popular map and navigation app, with the aim of enhancing its local search experience. This strategic move is expected to bring significant improvements to Google Maps, offering users more accurate and up-to-date information about local businesses, traffic conditions, and more.
