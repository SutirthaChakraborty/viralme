History of Oreo Cookies

Oreo cookies were first introduced in 1912 by the National Biscuit Company (now known as Nabisco). They were originally called "Oreo Biscuits" and quickly gained popularity due to their unique sandwich cookie design and rich chocolate flavor.

Innovation and Marketing

Oreo cookies have always been at the forefront of innovation and marketing. They were the first commercial cookie to use the "twist, lick, and dunk" advertising campaign, which encouraged consumers to twist open the cookie, lick the creamy filling, and dunk it in milk. This campaign became a huge success and is still associated with Oreo cookies today.

Impact on the Snack Industry

Oreo cookies have had a significant impact on the snack industry. They have inspired other brands to create their own sandwich cookies and have set the standard for taste and quality. Oreo cookies are now available in various flavors and variations, catering to different consumer preferences.
