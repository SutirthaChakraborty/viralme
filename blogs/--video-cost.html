Understanding Video Costs

The cost of producing videos can vary significantly based on several factors. Click on the topics below to learn more.

Pre-Production Costs

Pre-production includes scripting, planning, location scouting, and hiring talent. Depending on the complexity, costs can range from $1,000 to $10,000.

Production Costs

Production involves filming, equipment rental, and crew wages. A typical production can cost between $5,000 and $50,000.

Post-Production Costs

Post-production includes editing, sound design, and color correction. This stage can cost anywhere from $2,000 to $20,000.

Distribution Costs

Distribution involves marketing and advertising costs to get your video seen. This can range from $500 to $5,000 or more depending on the strategy.
