Video Production during COVID-19

Video production has faced significant challenges during the COVID-19 pandemic. However, with the right precautions and adaptations, it is still possible to create high-quality videos while ensuring the safety of the cast and crew.

Key Considerations for Video Production during COVID-19:

  • **Remote Collaboration:** Utilize video conferencing tools for remote meetings, script readings, and pre-production discussions.
  • **Location Scouting:** Choose outdoor or spacious indoor locations that allow for social distancing.
  • **Safety Protocols:** Implement strict safety protocols such as regular temperature checks, wearing masks, and sanitizing equipment.
  • **Minimal Crew:** Keep the crew size small to reduce the risk of exposure.
  • **Virtual Auditions:** Conduct auditions online through video submissions.
  • **Post-Production:** Utilize cloud-based editing tools to facilitate remote collaboration during post-production.
