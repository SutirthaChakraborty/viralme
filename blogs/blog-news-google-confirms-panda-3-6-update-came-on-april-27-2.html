Google Panda 3.6 Update

Confirmed on April 27, 2024

Overview of the Panda Update

The Google Panda 3.6 update was a significant change aimed at improving the quality of search results. It focused on reducing the rankings of low-quality content and promoting high-quality sites.

This update specifically targeted sites with duplicate content and those lacking depth in their articles. Publishers are encouraged to focus on delivering unique, valuable content to enhance user experience.
