Connected TV Ad Spend Predictions and Strategies

Predictions for 2024:

  • Boosted by 30% from 2023 to 2024, connected TV ad spend is expected to reach $15.6 billion.
  • Increased adoption of connected TV devices, particularly among younger audiences, will drive growth.
  • Improved targeting capabilities will allow advertisers to reach their desired audience more effectively.
  • Growing importance of streaming services will lead to increased ad spend on platforms like Netflix and Hulu.

Strategies for Advertisers:

  • Embrace programmatic buying to reach a wider audience and optimize ad spend.
  • Utilize data and analytics to better understand viewer behavior and target ads effectively.
  • Focus on storytelling to create engaging ad content that resonates with viewers.
  • Experiment with interactive formats like quizzes and games to increase engagement and brand interaction.
