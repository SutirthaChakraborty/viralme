About the Campaign

The Strike4Repeal and March4Repeal movements aim to advocate for the repeal of unjust laws affecting marginalized communities. These initiatives focus on raising awareness and mobilizing support through peaceful protests and community engagement.

Our Goals

Our primary goals include:

  • **Raising Awareness** about the issues at hand.
  • **Mobilizing Communities** to participate in peaceful demonstrations.
  • **Advocating for Change** through legislative action.
