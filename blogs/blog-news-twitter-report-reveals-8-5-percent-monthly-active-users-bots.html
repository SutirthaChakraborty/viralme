Twitter Bot Report

8.5% of Monthly Active Twitter Users are Bots

Recent reports reveal that a significant portion of Twitter's monthly active users are actually bots, accounting for an alarming 8.5%. This finding raises questions about user engagement and the authenticity of interactions on the platform.

Key Statistics:

  • Total monthly active users: Over 330 million
  • Estimated bots: Approximately 28 million
  • Impact on brand engagement: High

Bots can be used for various purposes including spam, spreading misinformation, and automating tweets. Understanding their influence is crucial for effective social media strategies.
