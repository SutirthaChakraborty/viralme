Insights into Advanced Camera Movements

Jib Shots

Jib shots elevate the camera on a movable arm to create dramatic high or low angles, giving a dynamic perspective to your scenes. Learn More Jib shots are often used in action sequences and large set pieces.

Dolly Zoom

The dolly zoom technique involves moving the camera closer or further away from the subject while simultaneously adjusting the zoom to keep it the same size, creating a surreal effect. Discover More Famous for its use in "Vertigo" by Alfred Hitchcock.
