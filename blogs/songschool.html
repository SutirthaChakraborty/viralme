Music Theory

Music theory is the study of the language and practices used in creating music. At SONGSchool, we'll cover the basics of music theory, including:

  • notes and rests
  • pitch and melody
  • harmony and chords
  • rhythm and meter

Vocal Techniques

Vocal techniques are the methods used to produce sound with the voice. At SONGSchool, we'll cover the basics of vocal techniques, including:

  • breathing and posture
  • placement and resonance
  • register and range
  • articulation and diction

Songwriting

Songwriting is the art of creating original music. At SONGSchool, we'll cover the basics of songwriting, including:

  • lyrics and melody
  • structure and form
  • harmony and chord progression
  • arrangement and production

Performing

Performing is the act of sharing your music with others. At SONGSchool, we'll cover the basics of performing, including:

  • stage presence and confidence
  • microphone technique
  • audience engagement
  • backing tracks and accompaniment
