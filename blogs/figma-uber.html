Figma & Uber: A Design Perspective

About Figma

Figma is a cloud-based design tool that brings designers together to create user interfaces and prototypes.

With real-time collaboration features, Figma enables teams to design with efficiency, enhancing workflow in product development.

About Uber

Uber is a ride-hailing service that has transformed urban transportation through its innovative app.

Uber's user-centric design focuses on seamless user experience, setting standards in mobility solutions worldwide.
