Why Marketers Must Stop Using the Term Edgerank

Understanding Edgerank

Edgerank was once the term used to describe Facebook's algorithm for determining content visibility.

Keywords: Facebook Algorithm, Social Media Strategy, Content Marketing, Engagement Rate

However, with the evolution of social media, the algorithm has changed significantly. Referring to it as Edgerank can mislead brands in their marketing strategies. Staying updated with current terms ensures better engagement and reach.
