How to Use Video in Customer Onboarding

Engage your customers with interactive video content!

Benefits of Using Video in Onboarding

  • Enhances Engagement
  • Improves Information Retention
  • Provides Visual Demonstrations

Types of Onboarding Videos

  1. Welcome Videos: Introduce the brand and what to expect.
  2. Tutorial Videos: Step-by-step guides on using the product or service.
  3. FAQ Videos: Address common questions and concerns.

Best Practices for Creating Onboarding Videos

  • Keep it Short: Aim for 1-3 minutes per video.
  • Use Clear Language: Avoid jargon and be straightforward.
  • Add Visuals & Graphics: Support your message with engaging visuals.
