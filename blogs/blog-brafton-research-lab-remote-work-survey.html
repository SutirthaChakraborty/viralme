Brafton Research Lab Remote Work Survey

Overview of the Survey

The Brafton Research Lab conducted a comprehensive survey on remote work, focusing on employee productivity, virtual collaboration, and the impact of flexible work hours.

68% of respondents reported increased productivity while working remotely, citing fewer distractions and better work-life balance.

75% of participants favored digital collaboration tools, with Zoom and Slack being the most popular choices.

80% expressed a desire for ongoing remote work options even after the pandemic, emphasizing the need for workplace flexibility.
