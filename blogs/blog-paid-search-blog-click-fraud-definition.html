Blog Paid Search and Click Fraud Definition

Understanding Paid Search

Paid search, also known as pay-per-click (PPC) advertising, is a marketing strategy where advertisers pay a fee each time their ad is clicked. It's a way of buying visits to your site, rather than attempting to "earn" those visits organically.

Defining Click Fraud

Click fraud is the practice of repeatedly clicking on pay-per-click ads to generate fraudulent charges for the advertiser. This can be done by individuals or automated scripts, and it aims to drain the advertiser's budget without delivering any real value.
