Bing Social Launches New Twitter People Recommendation Feature

Social Marketers Take Note

Bing Social has recently launched a new feature on its platform that allows users to see who their friends and connections are following on Twitter. This can be a valuable tool for social marketers as it can help them identify potential leads and target their advertising efforts more effectively. The feature is available to all Bing Social users and can be accessed from the \"People\" section of the platform.

Twitter Announces New Features for Developers

In addition to the new people recommendation feature, Twitter has also announced several new features for developers that will allow them to create more engaging and interactive experiences for their users. These features include improved support for video content, new ways to monetize tweets, and the ability to send push notifications to users. Twitter's developer community is expected to be very excited about these updates and they should be rolled out in the coming months.
