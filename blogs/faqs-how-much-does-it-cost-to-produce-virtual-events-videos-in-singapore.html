Virtual Event Video Production Costs in Singapore

  • What is the cost of producing a virtual event video in Singapore?

    The cost of producing a virtual event video in Singapore can vary widely depending on several factors such as the complexity of the event, the number of attendees, the quality of the production, and the specific requirements of the event. Generally, the cost can range from SGD 5,000 to SGD 20,000 or more for high-end productions. It's important to consult with a professional video production company in Singapore to get a more accurate estimate based on your specific needs.
