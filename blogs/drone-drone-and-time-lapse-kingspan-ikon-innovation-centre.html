Drone & Time Lapse at Kingspan Ikon Innovation Centre

Discover how drone technology revolutionizes building projects at the Kingspan Ikon Innovation Centre. Our innovative approach integrates time-lapse photography to document and analyze construction progress.

Using drones, we capture high-resolution aerial footage, providing real-time insights into sustainable architecture and design. This technology not only enhances project efficiency but also promotes transparency.

At the Kingspan Ikon Innovation Centre, we aim to lead the way in smart construction practices. Our detailed time-lapse recordings help stakeholders visualize timelines and understand potential challenges. By embracing cutting-edge drone innovation, we contribute to a future where sustainability meets advanced technology.
