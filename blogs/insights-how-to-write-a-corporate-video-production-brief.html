Writing a Corporate Video Production Brief

Key Elements

  • Objectives: Define the purpose of your video.
  • Target Audience: Specify who the video is for.
  • Key Messages: Outline important messages to convey.
  • Budget: Provide an estimated budget for production.
  • Timeline: Set a schedule for production and delivery.

Get Recommendations

1. Keep it concise and clear.

2. Use bullet points for easy reading.

3. Involve stakeholders from the start.

4. Review and revise for accuracy.

5. Include examples of what you like!
