What is the typical lead time for a VFX video?

\n

The typical lead time for a VFX video can vary depending on several factors such as the complexity of the project, the availability of resources, and the length of the project. However, in general, it can take anywhere from a few weeks to several months to complete a VFX video. It's important to communicate with your VFX provider and establish clear timelines and expectations to ensure that the project stays on track and meets your deadlines.
