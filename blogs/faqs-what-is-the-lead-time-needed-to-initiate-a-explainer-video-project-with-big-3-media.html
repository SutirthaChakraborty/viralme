FAQs: Lead Time for Your Explainer Video Project

What is the lead time needed to initiate an explainer video project?

At Big 3 Media, the lead time for starting an explainer video project typically ranges from 4 to 6 weeks. This timeframe allows us to thoroughly understand your needs, develop creative concepts, and align with your branding.

How can I expedite the process?

You can expedite the project by providing clear objectives, existing materials, and timely feedback. This ensures a smoother workflow and helps us deliver high-quality videos faster.

What factors influence the lead time?

The lead time can be influenced by the video's complexity, length, and the amount of client feedback required. Projects that require extensive research or unique animations may take longer.
