Blog News | Google Says Branding Does Not Change SEO

\n

In a recent update to its search quality report, Google stated that brand mentions and mentions of specific products or services do not affect a website's search engine ranking. This means that even if you have a strong brand name, it is not necessary to include it in your content to improve your search engine rankings.

\n

However, Google does emphasize the importance of having a consistent brand identity across all of your online properties. This includes your website, social media profiles, and any other digital channels you use to promote your business. A strong brand identity can help establish trust with your audience and make it easier for them to recognize and remember your business.
