Blog Analytics & ROI of Marketing Campaigns

Understanding ROI in Marketing

Return on Investment (ROI) is a critical metric for evaluating the effectiveness of marketing campaigns. Analyzing blog performance can provide insights into traffic, engagement, and conversion rates, ultimately impacting ROI.

Key Metrics to Analyze

  • Traffic Generation
  • User Engagement
  • Conversion Rates
  • Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)
  • Lifetime Value (LTV)
