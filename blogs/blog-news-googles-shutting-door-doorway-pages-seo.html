Understanding Google's Doorway Pages Policy

What are Doorway Pages?

Doorway pages are low-quality pages created to manipulate search engine rankings. Google aims to eliminate these pages to improve user experience and ensure high-quality content ranks better.

The Impact of Google's Update

This update significantly affects SEO strategies. Marketers must focus on creating unique, valuable content rather than trying to game the system.
