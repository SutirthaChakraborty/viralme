Michael Conlan

Michael Conlan is a Northern Irish professional boxer who competes in the featherweight division. He is a former amateur boxing world champion and a member of the Irish Olympic boxing team.

Conlan turned professional in 2017 and has since won several titles, including the WBA (Regular) and IBF featherweight titles. He is known for his aggressive fighting style and powerful punches.

Conlan has been praised for his sportsmanship and humility, and has been involved in several high-profile fights throughout his career. He is considered one of the top featherweight boxers in the world and is a fan favorite among boxing enthusiasts.
