Job Scams Awareness

What are Job Scams?

Job scams are fraudulent schemes that deceive job seekers into giving away personal information or money. They often promise high salaries for little work.

Tip: Always research the company before applying!

Common Signs of Job Scams

  • Offers that seem too good to be true.
  • Requests for payment to secure a job.
  • Lack of a formal interview process.

Additional Tips:

  • Verify the company's website and contact details.
  • Look for reviews from other job seekers.
  • Trust your instincts; if it feels wrong, it probably is.
