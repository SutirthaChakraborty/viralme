Why Not All Shopping Carts are SEO Friendly

When choosing a shopping cart for your e-commerce site, understanding its SEO compatibility is crucial. Many popular platforms may not optimize product pages, impacting your search visibility.

Factors such as URL structure, meta tags, and mobile responsiveness play a significant role in SEO. Before purchasing, analyze if the cart supports SEO best practices.
