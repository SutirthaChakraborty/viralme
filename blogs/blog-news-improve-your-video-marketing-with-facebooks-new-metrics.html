Boost Your Video Marketing with Facebook's New Metrics

Facebook has recently launched new metrics that can revolutionize your video marketing strategy. These insights provide deeper understanding of viewer engagement and content performance, helping you tailor your campaigns for better results.

View Duration
Measure how long viewers are engaging with your videos.
Engagement Rate
Analyze interaction levels through likes, shares, and comments.
Completion Rate
Track percentage of viewers who watch your videos to the end.
