Microsoft AdCenter Rolls Out New Features

\n

Microsoft AdCenter has recently announced the release of new features that include tablet traffic measurement. This is a significant development for businesses looking to optimize their advertising campaigns on mobile devices.

\n

Tablet Traffic Measurement

\n

With this new feature, businesses can now track and measure the performance of their ads on tablets. This includes metrics such as click-through rates, conversion rates, and spend. This data can help businesses make informed decisions about their advertising strategies and improve their ROI.
