Stephen Gray

Pioneer of Electricity

About Stephen Gray

Stephen Gray (1666-1736) was an English scientist and inventor recognized for his groundbreaking work in the study of electricity. He was one of the first to demonstrate the phenomenon of static electricity and conductivity.

Key Contributions

  • Developed experiments that proved that certain materials can conduct electricity.
  • First to identify the difference between conductors and insulators.
  • Made significant early advancements in the field of electrical science.
