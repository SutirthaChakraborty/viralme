Cookie Cutter vs Custom Website Design

Understanding Cookie Cutter Websites

Cookie cutter websites are built using pre-made templates, resulting in websites that lack originality and individuality. They often rely on plugins and may pose compatibility and security risks.

Advantages of Custom Website Design

Custom-coded websites minimize reliance on plugins, prioritize responsive design, and offer complete creative control, resulting in unique and tailored online experiences.
