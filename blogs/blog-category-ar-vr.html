Exploring AR & VR Technologies

What is AR & VR?

Augmented Reality (AR) enhances the real world by overlaying digital information, while Virtual Reality (VR) immerses users in a completely virtual environment. Both technologies are transforming industries such as gaming, education, and healthcare.

Applications of AR & VR

  • Gaming: Immersive experiences that engage players.
  • Education: Interactive learning environments.
  • Healthcare: Surgical simulations and patient care training.
