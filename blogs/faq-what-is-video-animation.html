FAQ: What is Video Animation?

What is video animation?
Video animation is the process of creating moving images using computer-generated graphics. It combines art and technology to produce engaging visual content.
Why is video animation important?
Video animation enhances storytelling, captures attention, and improves user engagement, making it a powerful tool for marketing and education.
What are the types of video animation?
Common types include 2D animation, 3D animation, stop-motion, and motion graphics, each serving different creative purposes.
