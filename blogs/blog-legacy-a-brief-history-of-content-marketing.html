The Evolution of Content Marketing

Content marketing has transformed dramatically over the years, reflecting changes in technology and consumer behavior. This brief history showcases the key milestones in the evolution of content marketing.

1895: The Birth of Content Marketing
1996: The First Blog Emerges
2000: Rise of SEO
2006: Content Becomes King
2010: Social Media Revolutionizes Sharing
2020: Video and Interactive Content Dominates
