Understanding the Production Process

1. Planning

The planning phase involves outlining the production goals, resource allocation, and timeline.

2. Sourcing

This step focuses on sourcing raw materials and components necessary for production.

3. Production

The actual manufacturing process, where raw materials are transformed into finished products.

4. Quality Control

Ensuring products meet industry standards and specifications before distribution.

5. Delivery

The final stage where products are shipped to customers or retailers.
