The Role of Minister for Agriculture

Responsibilities

The Minister for Agriculture plays a critical role in ensuring agricultural development, food security, and rural development. Responsibilities include:

  • Formulating agricultural policies
  • Implementing programs for sustainable farming
  • Promoting agricultural research and innovation
  • Supporting farmers through grants and resources

Importance

The agriculture sector is vital for the economy. The Minister ensures:

  • Food safety and quality
  • Promotion of exports
  • Environmental sustainability
  • Support for farmer cooperatives
