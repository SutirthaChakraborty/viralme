Blog Explainer Video Characters Overview

An explainer video is a powerful tool for businesses to communicate their message effectively. In these videos, animated characters play a crucial role in engaging the audience and conveying the story. Let's explore the different types of characters and their roles in explainer videos.

Types of Characters

There are various types of characters that can be used in explainer videos:

  • Protagonist: The main character who represents the target audience and goes through the story.
  • Antagonist: The character who creates conflict or obstacles for the protagonist.
  • Supporting Characters: These characters provide additional information or support to the protagonist.
  • Mascots: These are brand mascots or characters that represent the company or product.

Roles of Characters

The characters in explainer videos have specific roles:

  • Engage the audience and create an emotional connection.
  • Explain complex concepts or ideas in a simple and relatable way.
  • Guide the audience through the story and keep them interested.
  • Highlight the benefits and features of the product or service.

Best Practices

When using characters in explainer videos, it's important to follow these best practices:

  • Choose characters that resonate with your target audience.
  • Create characters with distinct personalities and traits.
  • Keep the animation and movements natural and smooth.
  • Use clear and concise dialogue to convey the message.
  • Ensure the characters align with your brand identity and values.

By following these best practices, you can create engaging and effective explainer videos that leave a lasting impression on your audience.
