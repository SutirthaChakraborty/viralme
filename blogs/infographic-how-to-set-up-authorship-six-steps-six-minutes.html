Set Up Authorship in 6 Steps

Step 1: Create a Google+ Profile

Ensure you have a complete Google+ profile with a profile picture and bio.

Step 2: Link Your Content

Add a link to your Google+ profile in the author section of your blog or website.

Step 3: Use Rel=Author Tag

Implement the rel=author tag in your HTML to connect your content to your profile.

Step 4: Verify Your Authorship

Check if your authorship is verified by searching for your content on Google.

Step 5: Monitor Your Results

Use Google Search Console to track how your authorship is performing.

Step 6: Keep Your Profile Updated

Regularly update your Google+ profile with new content and achievements.
