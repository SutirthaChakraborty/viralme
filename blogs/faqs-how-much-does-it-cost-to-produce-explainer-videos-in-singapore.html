FAQs: Cost of Producing Explainer Videos in Singapore

1. How much does it cost to produce an explainer video in Singapore?
The cost can range from SGD 1,500 to SGD 10,000 depending on length, quality, and complexity.
2. What factors affect the cost?
Factors include animation style, duration, script writing, and voiceover talent.
3. Is there a difference in cost between 2D and 3D animations?
Yes, 3D animations typically cost more due to the complexity of production.
4. Are there additional costs involved?
Yes, additional costs may include revisions, extra services such as subtitles, and music licensing.
