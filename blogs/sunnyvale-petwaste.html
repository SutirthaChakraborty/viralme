Sunnyvale Pet Waste Management

Why Pet Waste Management is Important

Proper pet waste disposal is crucial for maintaining a clean and healthy environment in Sunnyvale. It helps prevent pollution, protects local wildlife, and promotes community hygiene.

Best Practices for Pet Waste Disposal

  • Always carry waste bags when walking your dog.
  • Dispose of pet waste in designated bins.
  • Consider composting pet waste if possible.

Learn More About Pet Waste

Did you know? Pet waste can contain harmful bacteria and parasites that can contaminate water sources. Always clean up after your pet!
