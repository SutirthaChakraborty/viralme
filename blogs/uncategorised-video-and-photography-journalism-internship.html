Uncategorized Video and Photography Journalism Internship

This internship offers hands-on experience in multimedia storytelling, focusing on video and photography journalism. Enhance your skills in visual content creation, editing, and reporting.

Overview Requirements Application Process

Internship Overview

This internship is designed for aspiring journalists passionate about visual storytelling. Work alongside experienced professionals and gain knowledge in both video production and photography.

Requirements

  • Strong interest in journalism and media
  • Basic knowledge of photography and videography
  • Ability to work independently and in a team

Application Process

To apply, please submit your resume, a cover letter, and a portfolio showcasing your photography and video work.
