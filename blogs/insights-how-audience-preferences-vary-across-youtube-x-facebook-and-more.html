Insights into Audience Preferences: YouTube vs Facebook

YouTube Insights

YouTube is renowned for video content consumption, with viewers typically favoring longer content for deeper engagement. Key demographics include young adults aged 18-34.

YouTube users prefer entertainment, tutorials, and music videos, showing high engagement rates particularly during evenings and weekends.

Facebook Insights

Facebook serves a diverse audience, with a mix of video, images, and text-based content. Users often engage with shorter snippets, memes, and news updates.

Facebook attracts a wider age range, particularly users aged 25-54. Engagement peaks during lunch breaks and late evenings.
