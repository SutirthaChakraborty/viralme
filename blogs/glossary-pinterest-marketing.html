Pinterest Marketing Glossary

Pin: A saved image or video that is shared on Pinterest.
Board: A collection of Pins organized around a specific theme or topic.
Rich Pin: A type of Pin that includes extra information directly on the Pin itself.
Pinterest SEO: The process of optimizing Pins and Boards to improve visibility in search results.
Impressions: The number of times Pins are viewed in the Pinterest feed.
Engagement: The total actions taken on your Pin, including saves, clicks, and comments.
