New Relic Synthetics

\n

New Relic Synthetics is a feature that allows you to run your application tests in a controlled environment, without affecting your production application. This helps you identify and fix issues before they impact your users.

\n

With New Relic Synthetics, you can simulate user interactions, monitor performance, and test different scenarios. You can also set up alerts to notify you when specific conditions are met.

\n

To get started with New Relic Synthetics, simply sign up for a free trial or purchase a plan. Once you have access, you can create test scripts and run them on your own infrastructure.
