StepAside Educate Together National School (ETNS)

ETNS is a pioneering initiative that combines the best of StepAside and Educate Together philosophies, creating a unique educational environment where students learn to think critically, collaborate, and contribute to their community.

Location: Dublin, Ireland
Year Established: 2015
Curriculum: Innovative, Focused on Critical Thinking and Collaboration
