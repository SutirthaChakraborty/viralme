Video Production in Dublin, Ireland During COVID-19

During the COVID-19 pandemic, video production in Dublin adapted to new challenges, utilizing tools like EditReady and Dropbox to ensure seamless delivery of content.

Key Adaptations in Video Production

  • Remote Collaboration: Teams shifted to remote work, relying on cloud services like Dropbox for file sharing.
  • Efficient Editing: EditReady streamlined the editing process, allowing for quick turnaround times.
  • Health Protocols: Productions adhered to strict health guidelines to ensure safety on set.

Video production companies in Dublin implemented new protocols to maintain quality while ensuring safety. This included virtual meetings, online script reviews, and remote editing sessions.
