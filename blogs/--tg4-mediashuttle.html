Welcome to TG4 MediaShuttle

TG4 is the National Irish Language Public Service Broadcaster, providing a wide range of content including music, sports, documentaries, and children's programming. The TG4 Player serves as a global hub for Irish language and cultural content, accessible to audiences worldwide.

What is TG4 MediaShuttle?

TG4 MediaShuttle is a platform that allows users to manage and share media content efficiently. It supports various media formats and provides tools for seamless streaming and broadcasting.

With TG4 MediaShuttle, users can:

  • Upload and manage video content
  • Stream live events
  • Access a library of Irish cultural content
