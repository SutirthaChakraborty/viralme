Blog Analytics 2016: Content Trends

Competitive Analysis for Better Content

Leverage competitive analysis to create content that outperforms the rest. Analyze top-performing blogs, and identify keywords and strategies that convert.

Content Length & Engagement

Longer articles tend to rank higher and receive more shares. Aim for 1500+ words for in-depth insights!

Visual Content Dominance

Posts with images or videos receive 94% more views. Use engaging visuals to enhance your message.

Mobile Optimization

With the rise of mobile usage, ensure your content is mobile-friendly. Consider layout, speed, and usability.
