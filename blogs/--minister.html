The Role of a Minister in Government

What is a Minister?

A minister is a high-ranking official in the government, typically in charge of a specific department.

Ministers are responsible for implementing government policies, managing their departments, and facilitating communication between the government and the public.

Types of Ministers

There are various types of ministers such as cabinet ministers, junior ministers, and state ministers.

Each type has different responsibilities and levels of authority within the government structure.

Responsibilities of Ministers

Ministers manage government departments and implement policies.

Their responsibilities include budget management, public service delivery, and representing their department in the legislature.
