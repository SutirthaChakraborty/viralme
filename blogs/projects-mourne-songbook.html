Mourne Songbook Projects

Overview
The Mourne Songbook is a collection of songs that celebrate the beauty and history of the Mourne Mountains, located in County Down, Northern Ireland. This project aims to preserve and promote the rich musical heritage of the area, featuring traditional tunes, ballads, and hymns that have been sung for generations.
