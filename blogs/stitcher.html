Stitcher: Interactive Multimedia Solutions

Perspective: What is stitcher?

A stitcher is a software tool or technology that combines multiple video or audio streams into a single, seamless output, often used for live events, virtual tours, or creating immersive experiences like 360-degree content. It utilizes CSS3 and JavaScript to manage the alignment and blending of elements for a fluid user experience.
