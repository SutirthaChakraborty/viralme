Blog Content Marketing: Who’s Who in 2019

Content Strategists

Content strategists play a pivotal role in shaping effective blog content. They analyze audience behavior, set strategic goals, and guide the overall content direction.

SEO Specialists

SEO specialists optimize blog posts for search engines, ensuring that content reaches its target audience. Their skills in keyword research and on-page optimization are essential.

Social Media Managers

Social media managers amplify blog content through various platforms. They engage with the audience, create shareable posts, and drive traffic back to the blog.
