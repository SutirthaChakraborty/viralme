Corporate Blogging Glossary

  • SEO (Search Engine Optimization)
    The process of optimizing content for search engines to increase visibility and organic traffic.
  • CMS (Content Management System)
    Software used to create, manage, and modify digital content on a website.
  • CTA (Call to Action)
    An invitation for the reader to take a specific action, such as subscribing or downloading.
  • Audience Engagement
    The interaction between a brand and its audience through comments, shares, and likes.
  • Brand Voice
    The consistent expression of a brand's personality through language, tone, and style in all communications.
