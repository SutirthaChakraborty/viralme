Why Aren't QR Codes More Popular?

Despite their potential, **QR codes** have not achieved widespread adoption. Factors include:

  • **Lack of Awareness**: Many users are unaware of how to scan QR codes.
  • **User Experience**: Scanning requires a smartphone, which can be a barrier.
  • **Security Concerns**: Users worry about malicious links embedded in QR codes.
