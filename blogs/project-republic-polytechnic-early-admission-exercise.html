What is EAE?

The Early Admission Exercise (EAE) allows Secondary 4 students to secure a place in Republic Polytechnic before receiving their final exam results.

Students who meet specific criteria such as academic performance and strong interest in their chosen field are eligible to apply for EAE.

The application process typically includes submitting relevant documents, attending interviews, and showcasing your passion through a portfolio.

Gain an early assurance of a place in a desired course, allowing for focused preparation and reduced stress during your final exams.
