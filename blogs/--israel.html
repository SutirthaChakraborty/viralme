Israel Information

Israel is a country in the Middle East, located on the southeastern shore of the Mediterranean Sea and the northern shore of the Red Sea. It is known for its rich history, diverse culture, and religious significance.

Israel's capital city is Jerusalem, and its official languages are Hebrew and Arabic. The country has a population of approximately 9 million people.

Israel is a parliamentary democracy with a President as the head of state and a Prime Minister as the head of government. The country is known for its technological innovation, particularly in the fields of cybersecurity, agriculture, and medical research.
