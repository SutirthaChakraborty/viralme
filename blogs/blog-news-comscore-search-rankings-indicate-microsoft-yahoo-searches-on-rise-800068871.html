Comscore search rankings indicate that Microsoft and Yahoo searches are on the rise. According to data from comscore, both companies saw a significant increase in search activity over the past year. In particular, Microsoft saw a boost in search queries for its products and services, while Yahoo saw an increase in searches for news and entertainment content. This trend may indicate growing interest in these companies' offerings as well as changes in consumer behavior.
