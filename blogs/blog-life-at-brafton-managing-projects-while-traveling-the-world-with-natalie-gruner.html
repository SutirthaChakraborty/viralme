Managing Projects While Traveling the World

Introduction

Join Natalie Gruner as she juggles project management at Brafton while exploring new cultures around the globe. Learn how to balance work and travel effectively!

Tips for Remote Work

  • Utilize project management tools like Asana and Trello.
  • Establish a dedicated workspace away from home.
  • Set clear goals and deadlines.

Traveling Smart

Enhance your travel experience by:

  1. Researching internet availability at your destinations.
  2. Packing tech essentials: laptop, charger, and portable Wi-Fi.
  3. Adopting a flexible schedule to accommodate time zones.
