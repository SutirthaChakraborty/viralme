Blog News: SEO Success in Interaction Metrics

Experts from SES San Francisco emphasize the importance of interaction metrics in achieving SEO success. Engaging content that encourages user interaction is crucial for enhancing your blog's visibility.

Metrics such as click-through rates, bounce rates, and average session duration are key indicators of how users interact with your content.

Key Interaction Metrics

  • Click-Through Rate (CTR): Measures the percentage of clicks on your links.
  • Bounce Rate: Percentage of visitors who leave after viewing only one page.
  • Average Session Duration: Average time visitors spend on your site.
