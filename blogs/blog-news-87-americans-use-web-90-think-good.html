Blog News: Internet Usage in America

87% of Americans Use the Web

Recent studies highlight that 87% of Americans engage with the internet regularly, making it an integral part of daily life. From social media to online shopping, the web has transformed how we communicate and acquire information.

90% Believe the Internet is Beneficial

An overwhelming 90% of Americans think that the internet has had a positive impact on society. It's seen as a powerful tool for education, connectivity, and business.
