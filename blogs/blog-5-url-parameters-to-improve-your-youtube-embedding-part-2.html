5 URL Parameters to Improve Your YouTube Embedding

Enhance Your Video Embeds

1. Autoplay - Add `?autoplay=1` to start the video automatically.
This can increase viewer engagement but may annoy some users.
2. Controls - Use `?controls=0` to hide video controls.
This can create a cleaner look but limits user interaction.
3. Loop - Add `?loop=1` to replay the video automatically.
Great for promotional videos or music tracks.
4. Start Time - Use `?start=30` to begin the video at 30 seconds.
Useful for highlighting specific content.
5. Modest Branding - Add `?modestbranding=1` to reduce YouTube branding.
This can make your content feel more integrated.
