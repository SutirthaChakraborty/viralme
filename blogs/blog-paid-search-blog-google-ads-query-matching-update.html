What is Query Matching?

Query Matching in Google Ads determines how closely a user's search query matches your keywords.

Key Updates

  • Enhanced matching algorithms for better relevance
  • Improved support for long-tail keywords
  • Updates to phrase and exact match types

FAQs

How does this update affect my campaigns?

This update improves the relevance of your ads, which can lead to higher click-through rates (CTR) and conversions.

Should I change my keywords?

Consider reviewing your current keywords to leverage the new matching capabilities for better performance.

What type of keywords are most effective post-update?

Long-tail keywords tend to perform better as they capture more specific queries.
