Understanding Google's Penguin Update

What is the Penguin Algorithm?

The Penguin update is part of Google's core algorithms, focusing on penalizing sites that engage in manipulative SEO tactics. It's crucial for content creators to adapt their strategies.

Key Takeaways:

  • Focus on quality content over keyword stuffing.
  • Avoid deceptive link-building practices.
  • Regularly update your blog for fresh content.
