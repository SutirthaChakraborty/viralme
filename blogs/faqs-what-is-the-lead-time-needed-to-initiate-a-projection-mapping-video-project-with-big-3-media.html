FAQs About Projection Mapping with Big 3 Media

What is the typical lead time for a projection mapping video project?

The typical lead time for initiating a projection mapping video project with Big 3 Media is between 4 to 6 weeks. This duration allows for thorough planning, conceptualization, and execution.

Why is lead time important in projection mapping?

Lead time is crucial in projection mapping as it ensures high-quality content creation, including storyboarding, design, and technological setup. Rushed projects may compromise quality and effectiveness.

What factors can influence the lead time?

Factors include project complexity, venue requirements, team availability, and amendments requested by the client. Early communication can help streamline the process.

Can I expedite my project if needed?

Yes, expedited options are available for projects with tighter deadlines. However, this may require additional resources and may increase costs. Please consult with our team for available options.
