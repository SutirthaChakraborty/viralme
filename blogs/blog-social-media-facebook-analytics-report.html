Understanding Your Blog's Social Media Impact

What is Facebook Analytics?

Facebook Analytics allows bloggers to track engagement, understand audience behavior, and analyze social media interactions.

Key Metrics

  • Reach: Total number of unique users who saw your posts.
  • Engagement Rate: Likes, comments, shares, and overall interaction.
  • Audience Demographics: Age, gender, location of your followers.
  • Page Views: Total visits to your blog via Facebook.
