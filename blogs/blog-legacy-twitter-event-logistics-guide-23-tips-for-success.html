Legacy Twitter Event Logistics Guide

Introduction

Hosting a successful Twitter event requires careful planning and execution. In this guide, we will provide you with 23 essential tips to help you host your own branded Twitter event.

Choose a Topic

Pick a topic that is industry-based, but broad enough to go beyond your specific products or services. This will attract a wider audience and increase engagement.

Interact with Participants

Engage with participants on an individual level to increase the chances of them returning to future Twitter chats and sharing their experience.

Share Insights

Pick some of the best answers from the event and share them with your web audiences to provide value and extend the reach of the event.

Blog About the Event

Write a blog post explaining the event on your website and share it on your social sites to generate interest and attract participants.

Email Invitations

Share the event with your existing customers through email invitations to ensure they are aware of the event and have the opportunity to participate.

Direct Fan Engagement

Use direct fan engagement to encourage sign-ups and create a list of handles who want to get involved in the event.

Keep in Touch

Make sure to reach out to great contributors after the event to nurture the relationships you've built and maintain engagement.
