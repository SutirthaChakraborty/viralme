About Conor McGregor

Conor McGregor is a professional mixed martial artist and a prominent figure in UFC. Known for his knockout power and charismatic persona, he has become a global icon.

  • Two-division champion in UFC (Featherweight and Lightweight)
  • First fighter in UFC history to hold titles in two weight classes simultaneously
  • Famous for his fight against Floyd Mayweather Jr. in 2017
  • Entrepreneurial ventures including Proper No. Twelve Irish Whiskey
