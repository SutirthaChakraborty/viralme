FAQ: Typical Lead Time for Instructional Educational Videos

What is the average lead time for creating an instructional video?

Lead times for instructional educational videos can vary depending on the complexity, content production, and post-production requirements. On average, a simple video may take 2-4 weeks from concept to completion, while a more elaborate project with animations or extensive editing could take up to 8 weeks or more.
