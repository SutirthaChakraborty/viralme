Impact of Google's New Image Search Arrows

With Google's recent update introducing new image search arrows, many brands are concerned about losing clicks and visibility. These arrows allow users to navigate through images seamlessly, potentially diverting traffic away from traditional search results.

Related Keywords: Google image search, click-through rate, brand visibility, SEO impact, user experience, search engine optimization.

Brands may need to adapt their digital strategies to maintain engagement. Focusing on high-quality images and leveraging video content could mitigate click losses. SEO practices should also evolve to enhance visibility in light of these changes.

2. Create engaging multimedia content.
