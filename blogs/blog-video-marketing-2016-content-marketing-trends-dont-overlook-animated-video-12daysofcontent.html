2016 Content Marketing Trends

Don't Overlook Animated Video

In 2016, one of the key trends in content marketing was the rise of animated videos. These videos are effective in breaking down complex topics, making them accessible to a wider audience. Many users prefer watching a short, engaging animated video with sound over reading text.

Utilizing visual content with audio is perfect for how-tos, reviews, and advertising, enhancing user engagement significantly.
