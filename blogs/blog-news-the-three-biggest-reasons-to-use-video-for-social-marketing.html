3 Biggest Reasons to Use Video for Social Marketing

Why Video is Essential for Your Marketing Strategy

1. Increased Engagement

Videos capture attention more effectively than text or images, leading to higher engagement rates on social media platforms.

2. Better Retention of Information

Viewers retain 95% of a message when they watch it in a video compared to just 10% when reading it in text.

3. Enhanced SEO Benefits

Including video on your website can increase your chances of ranking on the first page of Google search results.
