Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What drone do you use for aerial photography?

We use the DJI Phantom 4 for aerial photography due to its excellent camera quality and stability. It's perfect for capturing high-resolution images and videos from the sky.

Which drone is best for surveying?

The senseFly eBee X is our go-to drone for surveying tasks. It offers precision and a variety of camera options tailored for geospatial mapping and analysis.

How do you choose the right drone for your project?

We consider factors like required camera quality, flight time, range, and specific project needs. Each drone serves unique purposes based on the client's objectives.
