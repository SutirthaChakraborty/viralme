Record 36 Hours: The Average American's Online Time in January

In January, the average American spent a shocking 36 hours online, a new record that highlights the growing digital engagement in our daily lives. With increased usage of social media, streaming services, and online shopping, this trend is set to continue.

This surge in online activity underscores the importance of understanding digital habits. As we navigate through the digital age, staying informed about our online use can be key to addressing issues like digital addiction and finding balance.
