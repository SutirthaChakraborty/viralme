Blog Strategy & Social Media Metrics

Blog Strategy

Blog strategy involves planning and executing a strategy to create, publish, and promote content on a blog. It's about understanding your audience, creating valuable content, and using various tactics to reach and engage your readers.

Key Metrics

- Unique Visitors

- Time on Site

- Bounce Rate

- Conversion Rate

Social Media Metrics

Social media metrics are key indicators that help measure the performance of social media strategies. They provide insights into how well your social media presence is engaging with your audience and driving desired actions.

Key Metrics

- Engagement Rate

- Reach

- Likes/Shares

- Comments
