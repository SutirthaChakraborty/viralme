Understanding News Junkies

21% of Online Consumers are News Junkies

According to ExactTarget, a significant **21% of online consumers** identify as news junkies, showcasing a growing trend in digital news consumption. This demographic is characterized by their constant engagement with news platforms, seeking the latest updates and insights.
