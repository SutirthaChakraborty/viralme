Why 2-Minute Videos Suck for Lead Generation

Short videos often fail to engage potential leads effectively. They lack depth and fail to convey complex ideas, which can lead to missed opportunities in capturing audience interest.

Moreover, viewers may not retain critical information from brief content, making it less likely for them to convert into leads.
