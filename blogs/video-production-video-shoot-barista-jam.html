Video Production: Barista Jam

Why Video Production?

Capturing the vibrant energy of a barista jam through video showcases the artistry of coffee-making. It enhances audience engagement and promotes coffee culture.

Key Steps for a Successful Video Shoot

  • Plan your shots – Consider angles and lighting.
  • Gather equipment – Cameras, microphones, and lighting gear.
  • Create a storyboard – Outline your video flow.
  • Focus on sound – Capture the hustle and bustle of the event.
