FAQs on Video Production Costs in Singapore

1. What is the average cost for video production in Singapore?

The average cost for video production in Singapore can range from SGD 1,500 to SGD 10,000, depending on the project's complexity and length.

2. What factors affect video production costs?

Factors include scriptwriting, pre-production planning, filming duration, equipment used, and post-production editing quality.

3. Is there a cost difference for corporate vs. commercial video production?

Yes, corporate videos tend to be less expensive as they focus on information delivery, while commercial videos often require more creativity and higher production value.
