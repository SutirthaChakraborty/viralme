Are Buzzwords Killing Your Personal Brand?

In today's digital landscape, particularly on LinkedIn, the overuse of buzzwords can severely impact your personal brand. While terms like "synergy," "innovation," and "thought leader" may seem appealing, they can dilute your message and make you seem less authentic.

Common Buzzwords to Avoid

  • Synergy
  • Innovative
  • Disruptor
  • Thought Leader
  • Passionate
