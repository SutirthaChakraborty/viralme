Email Marketing Costs Explained

Email marketing is a powerful strategy for businesses looking to engage with their audience. Understanding the costs involved is crucial for effective budgeting.

Factors Influencing Email Marketing Costs:

  • Size of your email list
  • Email service provider (ESP) pricing
  • Design and content creation

Email Marketing Cost Breakdown:

  • Basic plans: $10 - $50/month
  • Advanced plans: $50 - $200/month
  • Custom solutions: $200+/month
