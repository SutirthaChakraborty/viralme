About Dun Laoghaire-Rathdown County Council

Dun Laoghaire-Rathdown County Council is a local authority in Ireland that provides a range of services to the people of Dun Laoghaire-Rathdown, a county located in the province of Leinster.

The council is responsible for providing a range of services including waste management, housing, transportation, and community development.
