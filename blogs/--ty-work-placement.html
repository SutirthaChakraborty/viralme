Transition Year (TY) Work Placement

What is TY Work Placement?

Transition Year Work Placement is an integral part of the Transition Year program, allowing students to experience the workplace first-hand, develop skills, and gain a better understanding of career options.

  • Develops practical skills
  • Enhances employability
  • Boosts confidence
  • Encourages independence
  • Connects classroom learning to real-world experiences
  1. Research potential placements.
  2. Prepare a CV and cover letter.
  3. Practice interview skills.
  4. Set personal goals for the experience.

Be punctual, communicate effectively, show enthusiasm, and seek feedback throughout your experience!
