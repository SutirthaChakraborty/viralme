Understanding Google Local Pages Quality Guidelines Update

What You Need to Know

Google has updated its Local Pages Quality Guidelines to enhance user experience. It's crucial for business owners and marketers to stay informed about these changes.

Key topics include local search optimization, content relevance, and maintaining trustworthiness in your Google My Business profile.

FAQs

What are the main changes in the guidelines?
The main changes focus on optimizing local content relevance and ensuring businesses provide accurate information.
How can I improve my local page quality?
Ensure your information is complete, including address, phone number, and hours of operation. Regularly update content and engage with customer reviews.
