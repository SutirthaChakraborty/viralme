A Brief History of Netflix

Netflix started in 1997 as a DVD rental service, allowing users to rent movies online without late fees. This innovative model quickly gained popularity, leading to its transformation into a streaming service in 2007.

By 2013, Netflix began producing original content, such as House of Cards, which marked its entry into the competitive world of television production. This strategic move not only attracted new subscribers but also changed how audiences consumed media.

Today, Netflix is a global leader in streaming, boasting millions of subscribers worldwide and a vast library of films and series, including award-winning originals.
