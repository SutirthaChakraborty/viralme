Blog Strategy: 11 Types of Marketing You Should Know

1. Content Marketing

Creating valuable content to attract and engage your audience.

2. Social Media Marketing

Using social platforms to connect with your audience.

3. Email Marketing

Sending targeted emails to nurture leads and customers.

4. Influencer Marketing

Partnering with influencers to reach a wider audience.

5. SEO (Search Engine Optimization)

Optimizing your content to rank higher in search results.

6. PPC (Pay-Per-Click) Advertising

Paid advertising to drive traffic to your site.

7. Affiliate Marketing

Partnering with affiliates to promote your products/services.

8. Native Advertising

Embedding ads within content that blends with the platform.

9. Video Marketing

Using video content to engage and inform your audience.

10. Event Marketing

Promoting products/services through live or virtual events.

11. Mobile Marketing

Advertising through mobile devices for greater reach.
