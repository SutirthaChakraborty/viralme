Understanding Blog SEO & Types of Searches

Enhance your blog's visibility with effective SEO strategies. Explore various types of searches!

Types of Searches:

  • Informational Searches: Users seek information or answers.
  • Navigational Searches: Users look for a specific website or page.
  • Transactional Searches: Users intend to make a purchase.
  • Commercial Investigation: Users research products before buying.
