Days of the German Language

Tag des Deutsch

Celebrated on the 14th of September, it's a day to promote the German language.

Internationaler Tag der Deutschen Sprache

Held on the 18th of April, focusing on the importance of the German language globally.

Tag der Deutschen Einheit

Observed on the 3rd of October, celebrating the reunification of Germany.

Schiller Tag

Celebrated on the 10th of November in honor of the poet Friedrich Schiller.
