Listen Like You Used To on Spotify

Rediscover Your Favorite Tracks

With Spotify's Listen Like You Used To feature, dive back into nostalgic music experiences by accessing your favorite songs from the past.

More Information

This tool allows users to curate their playlists according to past listening habits, resembling the music they enjoyed during different periods of their lives.

Explore this feature for a personalized musical journey and to reconnect with tracks that defined your youth!
