Foursquare Raises $20 Million

Foursquare has successfully raised $20 million in funding led by Andreessen Horowitz. This significant investment highlights the strength of Foursquare's mobile marketing potential and their innovative approach to social services in the tech industry.

This funding round emphasizes the growing significance of location-based services and how social interactions through mobile marketing are shaping consumer behavior. With Foursquare's unique capabilities, they are poised to lead the market in harnessing data for enhancing user experiences.
