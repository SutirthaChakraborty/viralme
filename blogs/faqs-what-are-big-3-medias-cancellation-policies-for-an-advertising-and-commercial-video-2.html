FAQs: Big 3 Media Cancellation Policies for Advertising and Commercial Videos

1. What is the cancellation policy for Media Company A?

Media Company A allows cancellations up to 48 hours before the scheduled shoot without any penalties. Cancellations made within 48 hours may incur a 50% fee of the total project cost.

2. How does Media Company B handle cancellations?

Media Company B requires a 72 hours notice for cancellation. Late cancellations will be charged a 25% fee of the agreed contract.

3. What are Media Company C's terms for cancellations?

Media Company C has a strict 7-day cancellation policy. Any cancellations made after this period will result in a full charge of the project.
