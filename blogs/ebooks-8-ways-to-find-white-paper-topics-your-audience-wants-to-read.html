8 Ways to Find White Paper Topics Your Audience Wants

1. Conduct Audience Surveys
Use surveys to gather insights on what your audience is interested in. Analyze the data to generate topic ideas.
2. Analyze Industry Trends
Stay updated with the latest trends in your industry to identify relevant topics that capture interest.
3. Explore Social Media Conversations
Monitor discussions on social media platforms to find out what your audience is talking about.
4. Review Competitor Content
Analyze what topics your competitors are covering successfully to find potential gaps or opportunities.
5. Utilize SEO Tools
Employ SEO tools to determine what keywords and phrases are being searched for by your target audience.
6. Engage in Online Forums
Participate in industry-specific forums to gain insights into questions and topics that people are curious about.
7. Follow Influencers and Thought Leaders
Keep an eye on what experts in your field are discussing to inspire topic ideas that resonate with readers.
8. Feedback from Previous Publications
Assess feedback on your previous white papers to identify what your audience found valuable or wanted to learn more about.
