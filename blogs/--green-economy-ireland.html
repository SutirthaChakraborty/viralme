Green Economy Ireland

The Green Growth Strategy is the Northern Ireland Executive's multi-decade strategy, balancing climate, environment, and the economy in Northern Ireland. It focuses on ensuring that natural assets can deliver their full economic potential on a sustainable basis. Green Growth is about more than just climate targets. It means developing a low carbon, skilled workforce with more people working in jobs that contribute to climate action and a clean environment. Avail of grants for sustainable projects, with financial support for initiatives ranging from developing climate action plans to decarbonization projects and for developing next-generation green economy technologies. Reduced energy costs are a key principle for sustainability initiatives, aiming to use less and spend less.
