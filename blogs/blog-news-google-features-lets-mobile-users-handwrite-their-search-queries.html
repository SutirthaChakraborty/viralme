\n

\n In a recent update to its search engine, Google has introduced a new feature that allows mobile users to handwrite their search queries using a virtual keyboard. This feature is designed to make it easier for people who prefer typing on a physical keyboard or who have difficulty typing with their fingers to find what they are looking for.\n

\n

\n The new feature is available on both Android and iOS devices and can be accessed by opening the Google app on your device and selecting the search bar. Once you have selected the search bar, you will see a small keyboard icon appear at the bottom of the screen. Clicking on this icon will open a virtual keyboard where you can type in your search query using your finger or a physical keyboard.\n
