Welcome to Project Deepavali Game!

Join us in celebrating the spirit of Diwali through an interactive gaming experience! Project Deepavali game encourages creativity, cultural awareness, and family fun during the festival of lights.

What is Project Deepavali?

This game immerses players in the vibrant traditions of Diwali. Engage in festive activities, solve puzzles, and discover the significance of the festival.

Features:

  • Educational mini-games
  • Interactive storytelling
  • Fun puzzles and challenges
  • Colorful graphics and sound
