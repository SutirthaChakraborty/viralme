Brafton Research Lab: Google Analytics 4 Survey Recap

Understanding GA4 Transition

Recent surveys indicate that **43.6%** of marketers feel uneasy about transitioning to **Google Analytics 4 (GA4)**, with many seeking resources to better understand its functionalities.

Key Insights from the Survey

Marketers are exploring various resources, including the **Brafton Blog**, to enhance their knowledge about GA4. The survey revealed that many are still relying on traditional analytics tools while adapting to the new features of GA4.
