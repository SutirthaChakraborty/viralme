6 New Instagram Features to Help Businesses During COVID-19

1. Instagram Shopping

Showcase your products directly in your posts and stories. Customers can shop without leaving the app.

2. Live Shopping Events

Host live shopping events, allowing followers to interact and purchase products in real-time.

3. IGTV Monetization

Content creators can now earn money from IGTV ads, enhancing engagement and revenue opportunities.

4. New Content Scheduling Tools

Efficiently plan and schedule posts to maintain a consistent online presence even during lockdowns.

5. Enhanced Analytics

Gain insights into how your content resonates with your audience and refine your marketing strategies.

6. Instagram Guides

Create curated content collections to inspire and inform your audience, driving engagement and sales.
