McDonald's Foursquare Campaign: Lovin' It!

Overview

McDonald's has successfully leveraged Foursquare to enhance its marketing strategy, generating significant buzz among mobile consumers. The campaign, costing less than $1,000, resulted in a 33% increase in check-ins at various locations.

Tip for Mobile Marketers

Utilize Foursquare's Nearby Tips feature to help consumers discover local brands and increase engagement.

Foursquare's new features are designed to broaden its reach, making it an essential tool for marketers looking to connect with local audiences.
