Santa Claus: The Spirit of Christmas

Who is Santa?

Santa Claus, also known as Saint Nicholas or Kris Kringle, is a legendary figure who delivers gifts to well-behaved children on Christmas Eve.

Traditions

Traditions include hanging stockings, decorating Christmas trees, and leaving cookies and milk for Santa on Christmas Eve.
