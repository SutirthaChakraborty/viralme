What is Technology?

Technology refers to the application of scientific knowledge for practical purposes, especially in industry. It involves the development, manufacturing, and use of tools, machines, and systems to solve problems and improve life.

×

Technology Overview

Technology encompasses a wide range of topics, including hardware, software, algorithms, and systems. It is a dynamic field that encompasses the development of new technologies and the application of existing technologies in new ways.

The Impact of Technology

Technology has a profound impact on society, influencing everything from communication, to education, to healthcare. It has the potential to solve some of the world's most pressing problems, but also poses challenges in terms of privacy, security, and ethical considerations.
