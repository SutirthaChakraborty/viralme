Safety Statement and Risk Assessment

Safety Statement

A safety statement is a document that outlines an organization's commitment to providing a safe and healthy working environment for its employees and visitors. It includes information on potential hazards, risk assessment, and control measures to mitigate risks.

Risk Assessment

Risk assessment is the process of identifying, evaluating, and prioritizing potential risks and hazards that may cause harm or injury. It involves assessing the likelihood and severity of risks and implementing control measures to reduce or eliminate them.
