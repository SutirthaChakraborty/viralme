Ephemeral Video Content Insights

The rise of ephemeral video content has been a significant trend in social media, particularly with the rise and reign of stories. Platforms like Instagram and Snapchat have popularized the concept of sharing short, fleeting videos that disappear after a set period, often 24 hours. This shift towards ephemeral content has implications for user engagement, privacy concerns, and the dynamics of social media communication.
