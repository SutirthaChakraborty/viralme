Google Site Name Support for Blog SEO

What is Google Site Name Support?

Google Site Name Support is a feature that allows you to specify a specific name for your website in Google Search results.

  • Why is Google Site Name Support important for Blog SEO?

    Having a clear and concise site name in Google Search results can improve your website's visibility and credibility.

  • How to set up Google Site Name Support for your blog?

    To set up Google Site Name Support, you need to add a meta tag to your website's header. Here is the code:

    <meta name="site_name" content="Your Blog Name">

  • What are the benefits of using Google Site Name Support for your blog?

    Using Google Site Name Support can improve your website's search engine ranking, increase traffic, and enhance your website's credibility.

<html>
